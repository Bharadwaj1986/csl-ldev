<hom>1.</hom> <s>स्विद्</s> ¦ <lex>ind.</lex> (<ab>prob.</ab> <ab>fr.</ab> <hom>5.</hom> <s>सु + इद्</s>;
<ab>cf.</ab> <s>कुविद्</s>) a particle of interrogation or inquiry or doubt, often translatable by ‘do you think?’ ‘perhaps’, ‘pray’, ‘indeed’, ‘any’ (<ab>esp.</ab> used after the interrogative <s>क</s> and its derivatives <ab>e.g.</ab> <s>कः स्विद् एषाम् ब्राह्मणानाम् अनूचान-तमः</s>, ‘pray who [or ‘who do you think’] is the most learned of these <s1 slp1="brahman">Brahman</s1>s?’ <ls>ŚBr.</ls>;
but also without another interrogative <ab>e.g.</ab> <s>त्वं स्विन् नो याज्ञवल्क्य ब्रक्मिष्ठो ऽसि</s>, ‘do you think <s1 slp1="yAjYavalkya">Yājñavalkya</s1>, you are the greatest <ns>Brāhman</ns> among us?’ <ls>ib.</ls>;
also used after <s>उत</s>, <s>अपि</s>, <s>आहो</s>, and <s>उता<srs/>हो</s>, and disjunctively in the first or second or both parts of a double interrogation, thus: <s>किंनु-स्विद्</s>;
<s>किंस्विद्-स्विद्</s>;
<s>स्विद्-स्विद्</s>;
<s>स्विद्-उता<srs/>हो</s>;
<s>नु-स्विद्</s>;
<s>स्विद्-नु</s>;
<s>स्विद्-उत</s>;
<s>स्विद्-वा</s>;
<s>स्विद्-किमु</s>;
<s>स्विद्-किम्-नुकिम्</s>;
sometimes making a preceding interrogative indefinite <ab>e.g.</ab> <s>क्व/ स्विद्</s>, ‘anywhere’;
<s>क/ः स्विद्</s>, ‘whoever’, ‘any one’;
similarly with <s>यद्</s> <ab>e.g.</ab> <s>यद् स्विद् दीयते</s>, ‘whatever is given’ <ls>MaitrS.</ls>;
