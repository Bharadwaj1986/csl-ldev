{@Brahma-datta Caikitāneya@}¦ (‘descendant of Cekitāna’) is the
name of a teacher in the Bṛhadāraṇyaka Upaniṣad (i. 3, 26).
He is mentioned also in the Jaiminīya Upaniṣad (i. 38, 1;
59, 1) as patronized by {@Abhipratārin,@} the Kuru king.
