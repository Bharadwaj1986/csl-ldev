ŚRUTAŚRĪ¦. An asura. Garuḍa killed this Asura. Once
Garuḍa happened to go to Indraloka and he began to
brag about his own greatness. He claimed that he had
killed Śrutaśrī, Śrutasena, Vipasvān, Rocanāmukha,
Kālaka, Ahar, Prastuta and other Asuras. (M.B. Udyoga
Parva, Chapter 105, Verse 12).
