TĀLAJAṄGHA¦. Jayadhvaja, fifth son of Kārttavīrya,
got a son named Tālajaṅgha. The sons of this valiant
man are called Tālajaṅghas. There was once a great
fight between Vītihotra, the eldest of these sons, and
Paraśurāma. (Chapter 88, Brahmāṇḍa Purāṇa).
