PAÑCĀGNI¦. Rohiṇī, a daughter and Soma, a son, were
born to Niśā the third wife of Manu, an Agni. Besides
these they got five sons in the form of Agni (fire) and
these five are called Pañcāgnis. They are Vaiśvānara,
Viśvapati, Sannihita, Kapila and Agraṇī.
