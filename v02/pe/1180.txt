BHRĀSAKARṆA¦. A Rākṣasa. He was the son of
Ketumatī, by the Rākṣasa, Sumālī. They had ten sons—
Prahasta, Akaṃpana, Vikaṭa, Kālakāmukha, Dhūmrā-
kṣa, Daṇḍa, Supārśva, Saṃhrāda, Prākvāṭa, and
Bhrāsakarṇa and four daughters—Vekā, Puṣpotkaṭā,
Kaikasī and Kuṃbhīnasī. (Uttara Rāmāyaṇa).
