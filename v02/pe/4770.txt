NARIṢYANTA II¦. A King who was the son of Marutta.
Indrasenā was his wife; Dama was his son. While
Nariṣyanta was leading the life of a house-holder in the
forest, Vapuṣmān killed him. Indrasenā jumped into
the funeral pyre of her husband and died. (Mārkaṇḍeya
Purāṇa).
