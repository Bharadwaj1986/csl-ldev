SYANDIKĀ¦. A river in Kosala. This is very famous in
the Purāṇas. It is mentioned in Vālmīki Rāmāyaṇa,
Ayodhyā Kāṇḍa, Sarga 49, Verse 12, that Śrī Rāma,
who had started for forest-life, crossed the river “Syan-
dikā where peacocks and swans called.”
