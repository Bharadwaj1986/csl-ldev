RUKMAŚUKRA¦. A son of Priyavrata the brother of
Uttānapāda. Prajāpati Viśvakarmā gave in marriage
to Priyavrata, his two daughters Surūpā and Barhiṣ-
matī, who were exceedingly beautiful and good-natured.
By the first wife, ten sons were born to him. Devī
Bhāgavata, Skandha 8).
