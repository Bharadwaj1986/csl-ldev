VASIṢṬHĀŚRAMA¦. A holy place near Niścirā-
saṅgama. This holy bath is famous throughout the
three worlds. Those who bathe in this place will enjoy
the fruits of performing Vājapeya yajña. (Drinking
sacrifice). (M.B. Vana Parva, Chapter 87, Stanza 140).
