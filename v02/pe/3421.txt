KAUSALYĀ I¦. A queen of King Daśaratha and mother
of Śrī Rāma. Daśaratha had three wives Kausalyā,
Kaikeyī and Sumitrā. Kausalyā gave birth to Śrī Rāma,
Kaikeyī to Bharata and Sumitrā to Lakṣmaṇa and
Śatrughna. (Vālmīki Rāmāyaṇa, Bāla Kāṇḍa, Sarga
16).
