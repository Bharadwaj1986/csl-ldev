MAṆIMATĪPURĪ¦ (MAṆIMATPATTANAM). A meet-
ing place of the asuras. The notorious asura Ilvala
lived here for some time. (Śloka 4, Chapter 94, Vana
Parva). The demons Nivātakavacas hid here in this
city and Rāvaṇa challenged them to a fight after coming
to the gates of the city. (Uttara Rāmāyaṇa).
