SUMANDA¦. A brother of king Duṣyanta. Two sons,
Santurodha and Pratiratha were born to king Matināra
of Pūru dynasty and to Santurodha were born three
valiant sons called Duṣyanta, Pravīra and Sumanda.
To Duṣyanta was born of Śakuntalā, Bharata. (Agni
Purāṇa, Chapter 278.)
