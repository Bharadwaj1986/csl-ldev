KṢĪRASĀGARA¦. Sea of Milk. It is mentioned in
<div n="lb"/>Mahābhārata, Udyoga Parva, Chapter 102, that
<div n="lb"/>Brahmā drank too much of Amṛta and vomited. From
<div n="lb"/>the vomit the cow Surabhi came into being. The milk
<div n="lb"/>of Surabhi flowed and collected into a sea. This sea is
<div n="lb"/>called the milk-sea or Kṣīrasāgara.
