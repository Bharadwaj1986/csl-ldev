VIŚALYĀ I¦. A river famous in the Purāṇas. Mention is
made in Mahābhārata, Sabhā Parva, Chapter 9, Stanza
20, that this river stays in the Palace of Varuṇa glori-
fying him. This is a holy river. One could obtain the
fruits of Agniṣṭoma Yajña (a sacrifice) by taking
a bath in this river.
