AMITAUJĀ¦. A mighty Kṣatriya King of the Pāñcāla
kingdom. He was born from the element of a Rākṣasa
named Ketumān. Before the Pāṇḍavas went to war, they
had sent an invitation to him. He was one of the distin-
guished royal allies of the Pāṇḍavas. (M.B., Ādi Parva,
Chapter 67, Verse 12; Udyoga Parva, Chapter 4,
Verse 12; Udyoga Parva, Chapter 71, Verse 11).
