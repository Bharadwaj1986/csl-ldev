JAYANTĪ I¦. A holy place on the bank of the river
Sarasvatī. There is a tīrtha (bath) here known as
Somatīrtha. It is mentioned in the Mahābhārata,
Vana Parva, Chapter 83, Stanza 19 that those who
bathe in this tīrtha would obtain the fruits of the
sacrifice of Rājasūya. (Imperial consecration).
