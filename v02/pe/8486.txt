VIŚĀLAPURĪ¦. A holy place on the basin of the Ganges.
This place is in the forest Badarī. It is stated in Mahā-
bhārata, Vana Parva, Chapter 90, that the hermitage
of Naranārāyaṇas, stood in this place. This city was built
by the King Viśāla. (See under Viśāla).
