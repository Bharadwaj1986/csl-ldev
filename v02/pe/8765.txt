YAVĪNARA¦. A king of the Pūru dynasty. Bāhyāśva was
his father. Five sons were born to Bāhyāśva. They were
Mukula, Sṛñjaya, Bṛhadiṣṭha, Yavīnara and Kramila.
These five persons became famous in later ages under
the name of Pāñcālas. (Agni Purāṇa, Chapter 278).
