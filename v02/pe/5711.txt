<div n="NI"/>Once Raudrāśva fell in love with the celestial maid
Miśrakeśī. Mention is made in Mahābhārata, Ādi
Parva, Chapter 94, that as a result of the married life
of these two, ten archers beginning with Anvagbhānu,
were born.
