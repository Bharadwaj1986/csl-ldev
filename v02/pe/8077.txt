VAŚAVARTĪ¦. A group of devas (gods). It is said that
in the third Manvantara there were five groups of Devas,
each group containing twelve persons, known as the
Sudhāmās, the Satyas, the Japas, the Pratardanas and
the Vaśavartins. (For more details see under Manvan-
tara).
