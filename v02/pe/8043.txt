VARMĀ¦. In ancient days it was the custom to add the
word ‘Varmā’ to the names of Kṣatriyas. Brāhmaṇas
[Page832-a+ 65]
had to add the word Śarmā, Kṣatriyas the word Varmā
Vaiśyas the word Gupta and Śūdras the word Dāsa to
their names according to rules. (Agni Purāṇa, Chap-
ter 153).
