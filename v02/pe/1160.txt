<L>1160<pc>138-b<k1>भोः<k2>भोः
BHOḤ¦. A term used for greeting elders. When saluting
an elderly person, the term “Bhoḥ” is used as a suffix to
his name.
<div n="NI"/>For example:
<Poem>“Somaśarmā nāma aham asmi bhoḥ”
Bhoḥ śabdaṃ kīrtayedante
Svasya nāmnābhivādane /
Nāmnāṃ svarūpa bhāvo hi
bhobhāvo ṛṣibḥiḥ smṛtaḥ //</Poem>
(Manusmṛti, Chapter 2, verse 124)
