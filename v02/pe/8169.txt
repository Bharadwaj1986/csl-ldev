VEDAGARBHĀ¦. A name of Devī. When Devī killed
Śumbha and such other Asuras, Indra got pleased with
her and praised her as follows:
<Poem>“Āryā, Durgā, Vedagarbhā,
Ambikā, Bhadrakālī; Bhadrā, Kṣemyā
Kṣemakarī, Naikabāhū, I praise you.”
(Agni Purāṇa, Chapter 12).</Poem>
