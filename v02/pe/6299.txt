SARASVATĪSĀGARASAṄGAMA¦. The point where
Sarasvatī joins the ocean. It is considered to be a holy
place. Candra regained his brilliance once dimmed by
the curse of Dakṣa by bathing at this holy place. (For
details see under Candra IV, Para 6).
