ĀGNIVEŚYA¦ (AGNIVEŚA). An ancient sage who received
an armour and the sacred mantras associated with it
from Bṛhaspati. He was the Ācārya of Dhanurveda and
[Page087-a+ 65]
the revered preceptor of Droṇa. (Ślokas 67 and 68,
Chapter 94, Droṇa Parva, M.B.).
