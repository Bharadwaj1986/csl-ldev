TRINETRA¦. A minister of Mahiṣāsura. The cabinet of
Mahiṣāsura was extremely strong and brilliant. Cikṣura,
virile and an expert in military science, was the Defence
minister. The great economist, Tāmra, was the minister
for finance. Udarka was the Commander-in-Chief and
the three advisory members were Bāṣkala, Trinetra and
Kālabandhaka. Śukrācārya was the minister for edu-
cation. (5th Skandha, Devī Bhāgavata).
