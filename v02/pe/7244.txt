SURATHA¦ XII. A king who ruled over Kuṇḍalanaga-
rī. He captured Śrī Rāma's yājñic horse and also
took Hanūmān, Sugrīva etc. prisoners. At last Śrī
Rāma himself appeared on the scene, defeated Suratha
and released the captives: (Padma Purāṇa, Pātāla
Khaṇḍa, 49, 52).
