VIṢṆUJVARA¦. A great disease used by people to defeat
their enemies. The antidote for Viṣṇujvara is Śiva-
jvara. In ancient days, in the fight between Śrī Kṛṣṇa
and Bāṇa, Śivajvara was sent against Śrī Kṛṣṇa by
Bāṇa and to prevent it Śrī Kṛṣṇa sent Viṣṇujvara
against Bāṇa. (Bhāgavata, Skandha 10).
