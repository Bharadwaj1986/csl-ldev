DĀSA¦. A term used as a suffix to the name of a Śūdra. In
<div n="lb"/>ancient India the rule was that the proper suffix for a
<div n="lb"/>Brahmin's name should be ‘Śarmā’, for a Kṣatriya's name,
<div n="lb"/>‘Varmā’, for a Vaiśya's name, ‘Gupta’ and for a Śūdra's
<div n="lb"/>name, ‘Dāsa’. (See under Cāturvarṇya).
