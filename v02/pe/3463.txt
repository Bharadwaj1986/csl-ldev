KEKAYA¦. The Mahābhārata mentions another Kekaya,
a King of the solar dynasty. He wedded two Mālava
princesses from the elder of whom were born Kīcaka
and Upakīcaka, and from the younger was born
Sudeṣṇā, also called Kaikeyī. This Sudeṣṇā married
Mātsya, the Virāṭa King. (Virāṭa Parva, Southern
text, Chapter 16).
