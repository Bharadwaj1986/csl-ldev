BHĀSĪ¦. Daughter of Tāmrā, Dakṣa's daughter. Kaśyapa
<div n="lb"/>prajāpati married the following daughters of Dakṣa:
<div n="lb"/>Aditi, Diti, Danu, Kālikā, Tāmrā, Krodhavaśā, Manu
<div n="lb"/>and Analā, Tāmrā had five daughters, viz. Krauñcī,
<div n="lb"/>Bhāsī, Śyenī, Dhṛtarāṣṭrī and Śukī. And, the sons of
<div n="lb"/>Bhāsī are the Deva sect called the Bhāsas. (Vālmīki
<div n="lb"/>Rāmāyaṇa, Āraṇya Kāṇḍa, Canto 14).
