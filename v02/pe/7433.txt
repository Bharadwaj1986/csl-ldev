ŚVETAPARṆA¦. King of the city of Bhadrāvatī, which
was situated to the east of Hastināpura. In Jaimini
Aśvamedha Parva, Chapter 10, Verse 15, it is stated
that Bhīma after a fight captured and took with him
the beautiful horse of this King for the purpose of
Yudhiṣṭhira's Aśvamedha Yajña.
