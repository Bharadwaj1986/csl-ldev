SAÑJĪVANAMAṆI¦. The jewel in the head of serpents.
<div n="lb"/>There is a tradition among the poets that this jewel is
<div n="lb"/>the basis of the serpent's life. There is a story in Mahā
<div n="lb"/>bhārata, Aśvamedhika Parva, Chapter 30, Verse 42,
<div n="lb"/>stating how Babhruvāhana brought Arjuna to life by
<div n="lb"/>Sañjīvanamaṇi. (For details see under Babhruvāhana).
