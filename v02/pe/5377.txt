PRATIṢṬHĀNAPURA¦. A sacred place in Prayāga. The
place is at present called Jhūsi. Once this place was
ruled over by a king called Yayāti. The sage Gālava
accompanied by Garuḍa visited this king one day.
(Śloka 9, Chapter 114, Udyoga Parva).
