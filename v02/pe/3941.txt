KUVALAYĀPĪḌA¦. Name of the elephant posted at
the gates of Mathurāpurī to kill Śrī Kṛṣṇa and
Balabhadrarāma, who went there to witness the
dhanuryajña. But, they killed the elephant.
<Poem>Raṅgadvāraṃ samāsādya
Tasmin nāgamavasthitam /
Apaśyat Kuvalayāpīḍaṃ
Kṛṣṇo smbaṣṭhapracoditam //</Poem>
(Bhāgavata, Daśama Skandha, Chapter 48, Verse 2).
