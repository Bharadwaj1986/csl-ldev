PRĀCINVĀN¦. A king who was the son of Janamejaya
and grandson of Pūru. His mother's name was Anantā.
This king conquered in one day all the countries in the
east (prācī) and got his name Prācinvān. He got of his
wife Aśmakī a son named Saṃyāti. (Śloka 12, Chapter
95, Ādi Parva). Prācinvān got a son named Manasyu.
(Chapter 278, Agni Purāṇa).
