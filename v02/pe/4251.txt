MĀLINĪ I¦. A river which flowed by the side of the
āśrama of Kaṇva Muni. The river Cukka which flows
through the district of Saharanpur today was the Mālinī
of old. Some believe that Mālinī starts from the Himā-
layas. Śakuntalā was born on the shores of this river.
(Śloka 10, Chapter 72, Ādi Parva).
