KṚṢṆAVEṆĪ¦. A sacred river in South India. A bath
in the Devakuṇḍa in this river (Jātismarahrada) will
kindle in one remembrance about previous life. (Sabhā
Parva, Chapter 9, Verse 20). Agni is supposed to origi-
nate from this hrada. (Vana Parva, Chapter 222,
Verse 26).
