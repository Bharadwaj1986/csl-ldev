DHARMĀRAṆYA¦(M). A forest which is a holy place.
<div n="lb"/>It is mentioned in Mahābhārata, Vana Parva Chapter 82
<div n="lb"/>Stanza 46, that the moment one enters this forest one
<div n="lb"/>would become sinless. This place was once the capital
<div n="lb"/>of the King named Asūtarajasa. (See under Kuśa
<div n="lb"/>I).
