<div n="lb"/>Viśvāmitra stood on the bank of this river. The modern
<div n="lb"/>name of river Kauśikā is Kosī. The river Kosī flows
<div n="lb"/>through Bihar. Those who bathe in this river will

<div n="lb"/>Parva, Chapter 84).
