ŚATAMUKHA I¦. (ŚATAMUKHA RĀVAṆA). Father
of Indumukhī, the wife of Sahasramukha Rāvaṇa.
Śatamukha had defeated Pātāla Rāvaṇa in battle. He
gave the eternal weapon called Kaṭhorakuṭhāra as
dowry for his daughter's wedding. (See under
Sahasramukha Rāvaṇa).
