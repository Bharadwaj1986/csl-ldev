YUDHĀJIT I¦. A Kekaya King. This Yudhājit was the
brother of Kaikeyī, Daśaratha's wife, and the uncle of
Bharata. Daśaratha's death took place at the time when
Bharata was living in Kekaya country, at the invitation
of Yudhājit. (Vālmīki Rāmāyaṇa, Bāla Kāṇḍa, 73rd
Sarga).
