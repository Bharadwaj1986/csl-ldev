NIŚCIRĀ¦. A river glorified in the Purāṇas. It is well
known in all the three worlds. A bath in the river is
as good as performing an aśvamedha yajña, Those
who bathe in the confluence of Niścirā will attain
Indraloka. (Vana Parva, Chapter 84, Verse 138).
