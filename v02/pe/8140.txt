VĀTAVEGA I¦. (VĀYUVEGA). One of the hundred
sons of Dhṛtarāṣṭra. It is mentioned in Mahābhārata,
Ādi Parva, Chapter 195, that he had been present at the
Svayaṃvara (marriage) of Draupadī and in the Karṇa
Parva, Chapter 84, Stanza 2, that he was killed by Bhī-
masena in the Bhārata-battle.
