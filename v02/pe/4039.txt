MADAYANTĪ¦. Wife of King Kalmāṣapāda. He was
known as Mitrasaha and Saudāsa also. When Kalmā-
ṣapāda lay cursed Vasiṣṭha begot a child of Madayantī
named Aśmaka. Madayantī gave her ear-rings to the
sage Uttaṅka. (For details see under Uttaṅka and
Kalmāṣapāda).
