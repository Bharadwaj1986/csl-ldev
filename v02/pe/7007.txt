ŚUKA IV¦. A king of the Śaryāti dynasty. He was the
son of Pṛṣata. He had conquered all countries in the
world and after performing many yāgas, he renounced
his kingdom and attained Mokṣa by doing tapas on
Śataśṛṅga mountain. (Mahābhārata, Ādi Parva, Dākṣi-
ṇātya Pāṭha, Chapter 123).
