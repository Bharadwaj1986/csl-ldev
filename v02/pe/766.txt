ĀRUṢĪ¦. A daughter of Manu. Cyavana Maharṣi married
her. They got a son named Aurva. The child was
taken from the thigh of Āruṣī. Rcīka was the son of this
Aurva. Rcīka was the father of Jamadagni, father of
Paraśurāma. Even from childhood Aurva was a bold,
bright and good boy. (Ślokas 47 and 48, Chapter 66,
Ādi Parva, M.B.).
