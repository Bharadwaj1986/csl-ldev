ŚRUTADHVAJA¦. A brother of King Virāṭa. He was a
supporter of the Pāṇḍavas. Virāṭa's brothers were—
Gajānīka, Śrutānīka, Vīrabhadra, Sudarśana, Sruta-
dhvaja, Balānīka, Jayānīka, Jayapriya, Vijaya,
Labdhalakṣa, Jayāśva, Rathavāhana Candrodaya and
Kāmaratha. (M.B. Droṇa Parva, Chapter 158, Verse
41).
