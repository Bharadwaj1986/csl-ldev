VIVIDA¦. An asura who was the follower of Kaṃsa. It is
mentioned in Bhāgavata, Skandha 10, that the asuras
Pralambaka, Cāṇūra, Tṛṇāvarta, Muṣṭika, Ariṣṭaka,
Keśī, Dhenuka, Agha, Vivida and some others had
been the followers of Kaṃsa who caused havoc among
the people.
