GANDHARVATĪRTHA¦. An ancient place of pilgri-
mage on the banks of Sarasvati in North India. The
gandharva called Vibhāvasu usually dances at this
place with his companions. Balabhadra Rāma once
visited this holy place. (Śalya Parva, Chapter 37,
Verse 9).
