JĀHUṢA¦. A King of the period of Ṛgveda. It is stated in
Ṛgveda, Maṇḍala 1, Anuvāka 17, Sūkta 116, that
[Page337-b+ 61]
when this King was surrounded by enemies once, the
Aśvinīdevas got him into their chariot and through
easily passable ways took him to the top of a mountain.
