AYOMUKHĪ¦. A Rākṣasa woman. She met Śrī Rāma
and Lakṣmaṇa on their way to Mataṃgāśrama in
[Page085-b+ 65]
search of Sītā, and requested Lakṣmaṇa to marry her.
Lakṣmaṇa, as in the case of Śūrpaṇakhā cut off her
nose and breasts and drove her away. (Vālmīki Rāmā-
yaṇa, Araṇya Kāṇḍa, Chapter 69).
