MĀRTTIKĀVANA¦ (M). An ancient country in
Bhārata. During the time of the Pāṇḍavas this country
was ruled by King Śalya. (Śloka 14, Chapter 16, Vana
Parva). Paraśurāma killed all the Kṣatriyas of this
country. (Śloka 12, Chapter 70, Droṇa Parva).
Arjuna once made the son of Kṛtavarmā the King of
this country. (Śloka 69, Chapter 77, Mausala Parva).
