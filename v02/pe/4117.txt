MAHĀKAPĀLA II¦. A Rākṣasa who attacked Rāma
and Lakṣmaṇa at Pañcavaṭī. In Vālmīki Rāmāyaṇa,
Araṇya Kāṇḍa, 26th Sarga we read that when Rāma
and Lakṣmaṇa fought with Khara, Dūṣaṇa and Triśiras
[Page462-b+ 65]
there were three terrible Rākṣasas named Mahākapāla,
Sthūlākṣa and Pramāthin.
