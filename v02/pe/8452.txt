VĪRUDHĀ¦. One of the three daughters of Surasā, the
mother of the nāgas (serpents). The two other daughters
were Analā and Ruhā. It is mentioned in Mahābhārata,
Ādi Parva, Dākṣiṇātya Pāṭha, Chapter 66, that from
this daughter Vīrudhā, the Vīruts such as Latā, Gulma,
Vallī etc were born.
