DṚḌHĀŚVA¦. A famous king of the Ikṣvāku dynasty.
He was the son of Kuvalayāśva who had 21,000 sons.
All those sons but three were killed in the fight with an
asura called Dhundhu. The three sons who survived
the killing were Dṛḍhāśva, Kapilāśva and Candrāśva.
(Vana Parva, Chapter 204).
