KRŪRĀ¦ (KRODHĀ). A daughter of Prajāpati Dakṣa.
<div n="lb"/>She became the wife of Kaśyapa. A large number of
<div n="lb"/>Asuras were born to her. They all were very cruel and
<div n="lb"/>were called Krodhavaśas. (Mahābhārata, Ādi Parva,
<div n="lb"/>Chapter 65, Stanza 32).
