SUCCHĀYĀ¦. The wife of Śiṣṭi, the son of Dhruva. Two
sons named Śiṣṭi and Bhavya were born to Dhruva by
his wife Śambhū. Succhāyā, the wife of Śiṣṭi gave birth
to five sons who were sinless. They were Ripu, Ripuñ-
jaya, Vipra, Vṛkala and Vṛkatejas. (Viṣṇu Purāṇa,
Aṃśa 1, Chapter 13).
