VIRŪPĀKṢA III¦. One of the thirtythree notorious
Dānavas (demons) born to Prajāpati Kaśyapa by his
wife Danu. It is stated in Mahābhārata, Ādi Parva,
Chapter 67, Stanza 22, that it was this asura called
Virūpākṣa, who had, later, taken rebirth as King Citra-
varmā.
