PIṄGALĀ II¦. A prostitute of the country of Avantī.
Piṅgalā was born as the daughter of King Candrāṅgada
in her next birth because of the virtuous act she did
in doing service to the sage Ṛṣabha. The King named
the girl Kīrtimālinī and Bhadrāyu married her. (See
under Bhadrāyu).
