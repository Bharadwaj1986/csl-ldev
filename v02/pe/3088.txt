KĀLABANDHAKA¦. An adviser of Mahiṣāsura.
Cikṣura was his war-minister, Tāmra his finance
minister, Asiloman, Prime Minister, and Udarka, Chief
of the army. Kālabandhaka, Bāṣkala and Trinetra
were the advisers of Mahiṣāsura. (Devī Bhāgavata 5th
Skandha).
