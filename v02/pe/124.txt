AJAKĀŚVA¦. A king of the Pūru Vaṃśa. The mighty
<div n="lb"/>Jahnu was born to Ajamīḍha by his wife Keśinī. Two
<div n="lb"/>sons, Ajakāśva and Balakāśva were born to Jahnu.
<div n="lb"/>Kuśika is the son of Balakāśva. Kuśika is the grand-
<div n="lb"/>father of Viśvāmitra. For genealogy see Pūru Vaṃśa.
<div n="lb"/>(Agni Purāṇa, Chapter 278).
