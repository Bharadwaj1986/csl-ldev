DAKṢIṆAMALLA¦. A country in ancient India. This
country is called Mallarājya. Its capital was Kuśīnagara
or Kuśīnara. In Mahābhārata mention is made that
Bhīmasena, during his conquest brought this country
under control. (M.B. Sabhā Parva, Chapter 30).
