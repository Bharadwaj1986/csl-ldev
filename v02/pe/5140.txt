PAUṢYA II¦. Son of Pūṣā, king of Karavīra. Pauṣya had
three wives and yet was not fortunate enough to get a
son. At last by the grace of Śiva, he got a son named
Candraśekhara. The palace of Candraśekhara was in
Karavīranagarī situated near Brahmāvarta on the
banks of the river Dṛṣadvatī. (Kālikā Purāṇa, Chapter
49).
