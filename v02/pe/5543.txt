PUṢKARA II¦. The younger brother of Nala. It was
this Puṣkara who jointly with Kali defeated Nala in a
game of dice and drove him away from his country.
[Page622-a+ 65]
At last Puṣkara apologised to Nala and gave him back
his country. (See under Damayantī).
