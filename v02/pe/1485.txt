CĀRUDHI¦. A mountain near the Mahāmeru. There
are twenty mountains around the Meru, viz., Kāṅga,
Kuraga, Kuśumbha, Vikaṅkata, Trikūṭa, Śiśira, Pataṅ-
ka, Rucaka, Nīla, Niṣadha, Śitivāsa, Kapila, Śaṅkha,
Vaiḍūrya, Cārudhi, Haṃsa, Ṛṣabha, Nāga, Kālañjara
and Nārada. (Devībhāgavata, Aṣṭama Skandha).
