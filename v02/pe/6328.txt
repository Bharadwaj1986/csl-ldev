SARPĀSYA¦. A Rākṣasa. He was the commander-in-chief
<div n="lb"/>of the army of Khara and Dūṣaṇa, who had clashed
<div n="lb"/>with Rāma and Lakṣmaṇa at Pañcavaṭī. Khara and
<div n="lb"/>Dūṣaṇa had twelve reputed army-chiefs, including
<div n="lb"/>Sarpāsya. (Vālmīki Rāmāyaṇa, Canto 27).
