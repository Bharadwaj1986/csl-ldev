VAIDARBHĪ I¦. A wife of King Sagara. This king born
of the Solar dynasty, had two wives named Vaidarbhī
and Śaibyā. Vaidarbhī was also called Sumati and
Śaibyā had another name Keśinī. Of these two,
Vaidarbhī gave birth to sixtythousand sons and Śaibyā
to one son named Asamañjasa. (See under Sagara).
