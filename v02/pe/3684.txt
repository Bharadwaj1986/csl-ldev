KṚTĀGNI¦. A King of the Yadu dynasty. Descended
from Yadu thus: Sahasrajit—Śatajit—Hehaya—Dharma
-Kunti—Bhadrasena—Dhanaka—Kṛtāgni. Kṛtāgni
was the brother of Kṛtavīrya, who was the father of
Kārtavīryārjuna. Kṛtavīrya had three brothers called
Kṛtāgni, Kṛtavarman and Kṛtaujas. (Kṛtāyus).
