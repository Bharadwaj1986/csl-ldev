KĀNTIVRATA¦. A Vrata observed in the month of
<div n="lb"/>Kārttika. It consists in offering worship to Balarāma and
<div n="lb"/>Śrī Kṛṣṇa and taking food only at night. By observing
<div n="lb"/>this Vrata for one year, beauty, longevity and health
<div n="lb"/>can be obtained. (Agni Purāṇa, Chapter 177).
