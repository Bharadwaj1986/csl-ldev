BY¦, {%prep.%} (By the side of) expressed by {#उप#} prefixed; as,
‘he sits by me,’ {#माम् उपास्ते;#} ‘a tank by a well,’ {#उपकूपजलाशयः#}.
 — (Beside, denoting passage) expressed by {#अति#} prefixed;
as, ‘he passes by me,’ {#माम् अत्येति#}. — (Denoting the agent,
instrument, cause, means or manner) expressed by the
instr. case; as, ‘by him,’ ‘by that,’ ‘on that account,’
‘by that means,’ ‘in that manner,’ may all be expressed
by {#तेन#}. In modern Sanskrit the instrument and agent
are often expressed by {#द्वारा#} or {#द्वारेण#} and {#कर्त्तृक;#} as, ‘to
hit with a stick,’ {#काष्ठद्वारा#} or {#काष्ठद्वारेण तड्;#} ‘sacrifices by
sages,’ {#ऋषिकर्त्तृकयज्ञाः#}. ‘By,’ in some of these senses, may
often be expressed by the indec. part.; ‘a man may be
happy by doing penance,’ {#तपस् तप्त्वा सुखी भवेत्#}. — (According
to) {#अनुरूपं, अनुसारेण;#} ‘by rule,’ {#विधिवत्, विधितस्, यथाविधि#}.
 — (As soon as, not later than) {#मध्ये;#} as, ‘by next year,’
{#आगामिवत्सरमध्ये#}. — (The quantity at one time) expressed by
the affix {#शस्;#} as, ‘by thousands,’ {#सहस्रशस्;#} ‘by twos and
threes,’ {#द्विशस् त्रिशस्;#} ‘by companies,’ {#पंक्तिभिः#}. — (Successive
[Page072-b+ 50]
action) expressed by {#प्रति#} or {#अनु,#} or by repeating the
word; as, ‘man by man,’ {#प्रतिपुरुषं;#} ‘day by day,’ {#प्रतिदिवणं#}
or {#अनुदिवसं#} or {#दिवसे दिवसे#}. — (By himself, alone) {#एकान्ततस्,#}
{#निभृतं, रहसि, रहीभूतः -ता -तं#}. — (In swearing or adjuring)
expressed by the instr. c.; as, ‘swear not at all, neither
by heaven, nor by earth,’ {#कमपि शपथं मा ऽकार्ष्ट स्वर्गेण न पृथिव्या#}
{#न;#} ‘by name,’ {#नाम्ना, नामतस्, नामधेयतस्;#} ‘by force,’ {#बलात्,#}
{#बलात्कारेण, प्रसह्म;#} ‘by chance,’ {#दैवात्;#} ‘by day,’ {#दिवा#}.
