<div n="lb"/>NOT¦, {%adv.%} In absolute negation, denial, or refusal, ‘not’ is
<div n="lb"/>rendered by {#न, नो, ना, नहि#}. When prohibitive, by {#मा,#}
<div n="lb"/>{#मास्म,#} or sometimes by {#अलं#} or {#कृतं#} with instr. c. When
<div n="lb"/>privative, it is expressed by {#अ, अन्, निर्, वि#} prefixed;
<div n="lb"/>as, ‘there is not,’ {#नास्ति;#} ‘he will not go,’ {#न गमिष्यति;#}
<div n="lb"/>‘do not go,’ {#मा गच्छ;#} ‘do not grieve,’ {#अलं शोकेन;#} ‘not
<div n="lb"/>regardful,’ {#अनपेक्षः -क्षा -क्षं, निरपेक्षः#} &c.; ‘not at all,’ {#अलेशं,#}
<div n="lb"/>{#न किञ्चिदपि;#} ‘not so,’ {#मैवं#}. In prohibitive phrases {#मा#}
<div n="lb"/>[Page537-a+ 51]
<div n="lb"/>may be used with the 3d pret., the augment being cut
<div n="lb"/>off; as, ‘do not grieve’ {#माऽशोचीः;#} ‘do not fear,’ {#माऽभैषीः#}.
<div n="lb"/>The interrogative ‘is not?’ is rendered by {#ननु#}.
