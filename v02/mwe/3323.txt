{#तथापि#}. — (Only) {#केवलं, मात्रं#}. — (All but) {#अल्पोनः -ना -नं, ईषदूनः#}

‘But’ may often be omitted in Sanskrit, or expressed by
another form of sentence; as, ‘he will not gain his end
but by doing that,’ {#तद् अकृत्वा प्राप्तार्थो न भविष्यति;#} ‘I do not
doubt but I am to blame,’ {#सापराधोऽस्मि इति न संशयः;#} ‘but
a short time since,’ {#न चिरात्;#} ‘there is none but fears
him,’ {#सर्व्वे तस्माद् बिभ्यति;#} ‘she does nothing but grieve,’
{#सा निरन्तरं शोचति;#} “who but a child weeps,” {#को रोदित्य्#}
{#अबालिशः#}.
