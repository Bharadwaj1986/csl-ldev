MUST¦, {%v. n.%} (Be necessitated). Expressed by the fut. pass.
part.; as, ‘anger must be restrained,’ {#कोपो नियन्तव्यः;#}
‘I must go, or it is to be gone by me.’ {#मया गन्तव्यं;#} ‘I
must do that,’ {#तन् मया कर्त्तव्यं#} or {#करणीयं#} or {#कार्य्यं#}. The
adverb {#अवश्यं#} may be added; as, {#तन् मया अवश्यं कर्त्तव्यं#}.
Sometimes the verb {#अर्ह्#} (c. 1. {#अर्हति -र्हितुं#}) has the sense
of ‘must;’ as, ‘he must not receive that,’ {#न तत् प्राप्तुम् अर्हति#}.
