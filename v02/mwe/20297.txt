NEITHER¦, {%conj.%} expressed by the negative {#न#} or {#न च,#} or by the
prohibitive {#मा#}. Sometimes in conjunction with {#वा;#} as,
‘he took neither food nor clothes for his journey,’ {#यात्रार्थं#}
{#भक्ष्यं वस्त्रं वा न जग्राह;#} ‘take neither food nor clothes,’
{#भक्ष्यं वस्त्रं वा मा गृहाण;#} ‘do not eat the fruit neither touch
it,’ {#फलं मा खादत मा स्पृशत;#} ‘he did not touch, neither did
he smell,’ {#नास्पृशद् न चाजिघ्रत्#}.
