 — (Religious order) {#आश्रमः#}. There are four Āśramas,
corresponding to the four stages of the Brahman's life.
Every Brahman who has passed through these four
stages is supposed to have passed through the four
orders in succession. When, in the first, he is called

or that of the religious student, is also called {#लिङ्गं#}.
