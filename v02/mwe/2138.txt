{%To%} BELONG¦, {%v. n.%} (To be one's own property) {#आत्मीयः -या -यं#}
or {#स्वकीयः -या -यं#} or {#स्वाधीनः -ना -नं अस्#}. But the sense of
‘belonging’ is more usually expressed by the use of the
roots {#अस्#} or {#भू#} or {#विद्#} with the gen. c.; as, ‘money be-
longs to me,’ {#मम धनं विद्यते#} or {#अस्ति#}. — (To have relation
to) {#सम्बन्ध्#} in pass. ({#-बध्यते#}). — (To be fit, or becoming) {#युज्#} in
pass. ({#युज्यते#}); ‘this belongs to me,’ {%i. e.%} ‘this is my busi-
ness,’ {#मम अयं व्यापारः#}.
