BECAUSE OF¦, {%prep.%} (By reason of) {#हेतोः, हेतौ, कारणात्#}. — (For
the sake of) {#अर्थं, अर्थे, कृते, निमित्ते#} affixed to a crude noun;
‘because of that,’ {#अनेन हेतुना, इति हेतोः#}. ‘Because of’
is often expressed by the abl. c. of the noun; as, ‘be-
cause of their unbelief,’ {#तेषाम् अविश्वासात्#}.
