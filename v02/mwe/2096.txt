‘Being,’ is often expressed by the loc. c. of {#सत्,#} as, ‘the
man being gone,’ {#पुरुषे गते सति;#} ‘it being so,’ {#एवं सति,#}
{#तथात्वे, एवम्भूत्वा#}. ‘Being’ may also be expressed by the
abl. c. of the abstract noun in such phrases as ‘he being
a good man,’ {#सज्जनत्वात्#}.
