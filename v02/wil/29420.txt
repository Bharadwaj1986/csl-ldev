{#मध्यदेश#}¦ m. ({#-शः#}) The middle region; part of {%India,%} bounded by
{%Kurukṣetra%} on the north, {%Allahabad%} on the south, the {%Himālaya%}
mountains on the east, and the {%Vindhya%} mountains on the west; comprising
therefore the modern provinces of {%Allahabad, Agra, Delhi, Oude,%} &c.; the
northern limit is elsewhere defined to be the disappearance of the
{%Sarasvatī.%}
.E. {#भध्य#} middle, and {#देश#} country.
