{#दिक्पाल#}¦ m. ({#-लः#}) A regent of a quarter of the universe, as INDRA of
the east; AGNI of the south-east; YAMA of the south; NAIRṚTA of the south-west;
VARUṆA of the west; MARUT of the north-west; KUVERA of the north; ĪŚĀNA of the
south-east.
.E. {#दिश्#}, and {#पाल#} who protects: this word is applicable to the
{%Dikpatis,%} (see the last,) as that term is to these also.
