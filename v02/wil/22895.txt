<L>22895<pc>494<k1>पञ्चाग्नि<k2>पञ्चाग्नि
{#पञ्चाग्नि#}¦ n. ({#-ग्नि#})
.²1 A collection of five fires, amidst which a devotee performs penance during
the summer season; or four fires lighted severally to the north, south, east and
west, and the sun over head.
.²2 Five mystic fires, supposed to be present in the body. m. ({#-ग्निः#})
.²1 A householder who maintains five fires, or the domestic one, and one for
warming visitors in addition to the other three.
.²2 One who is acquainted with the doctrine of the five mystic fires.
.E. {#पञ्च#} five, and {#अग्नि#} fire.
