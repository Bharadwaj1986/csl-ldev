.²1 A name of VIṢṆU.
.²2 Fever, (personified.)
.E. {#त्रि#} three, and {#पाद#} foot or part; three-footed, alluding to
VIṢṆU'S three steps which paced the universe; and the demon of fever is
described in the {%Purāṇas,%} with three feet, and three heads, alluding
probably to its three stages of cold, hot, and sweating.
