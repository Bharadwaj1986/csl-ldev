{#नक्षत्र#}¦ n. ({#-त्रं#})
.²1 A star in general.
.²2 An asterism in the moon's path or lunar mansion, of which twenty-eight,
distinct in name, figure, and number of stars, are enumerated; the {%Paurānic%}
and popular enumeration of those constellations is twenty-seven; {%Abhijit,%}
the twenty-seventh, being considered as formed of portions of the two contiguous
asterisms, and not distinct from them.
.²2 A pearl.
.E. {#णक्ष#} to go, Uṇādi affix {#अत्रन्#} or {#क्षद#} {%Sautra%} root, to
injure, or {#क्षर#} to drop, or {#क्षी#} to waste or decay, with the negative
prefix, and {#ष्ट्रन्#} affix; deriv. irr.
