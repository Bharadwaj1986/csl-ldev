{#अन्नप्राशन#}¦ n. ({#-नं#}) A religious ceremony, in which after presenting
oblations to fire, a little rice is, for the first time, put into the child's
mouth; it should take place between the fifth and eighth month.
.E. {#अन्न#}, and {#प्राशन#} feeding.
