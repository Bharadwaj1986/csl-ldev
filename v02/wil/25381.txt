{#पूर्णपाच#}¦ n. ({#-चं#})
.²1 A full cup or vessel.
.²2 A vessel filled with clothes or ornaments, which are scrambled for by the
guests and relations at a festival.
.²3 A vessel full of rice, presented at a sacrifice to the superintending and
officiating priests; the {%Pūrṇa Pātra%} is properly a measure of 256
handfuls of rice; it may also be composed of as much as will satisfy one great
eater.
.E. {#पूर्ण#} full, and {#पाच#} a vessel.
