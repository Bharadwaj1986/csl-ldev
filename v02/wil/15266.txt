{#चिरम्#}¦ {%ind.%} A long time.
.E. {#चिर#} to injure, and {#क#} affix: this word, and others evidently
derived from it, are now considered as particles, and have accordingly
appropriate, though strained, etymologies; the derivatives however corresponding
with the inflections of the singular number of nouns masculine or neuter, it may
have been originally an imperfect noun of the 1st declension, thus; nom.
{#चिरः#} or {#चिरं#}, acc. {#चिरं#}, 1st abl. {#चिरेण#}, dat. {#चिराय#},
2nd abl. {#चिरात्#}, gen. {#चिरस्य#}, 3rd abl. {#चिरे#}; see these words
severally.
