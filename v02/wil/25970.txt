{#प्रति#}¦ {%ind.%} A particle and prefix, implying,
.²1 Substitution, (instead, in lieu;) as {#प्रद्युम्नःकेशवातप्रति#}.
.²2 Several order, (severally, each by each;) as {#वृक्षं प्रति षिञ्चति#}; 
{#प्रतिवेदं#} each {%Vedā;%} {#प्रतिदिनं#} every day; {#प्रतिछिद्रं#} each
opening; {#प्रतिगर्भं#} every birth; {#प्रत्येक#} one by one.
.²3 Direction, designation, (to, towards, upon;) as {#वृक्षं प्रति विद्योतते#} 
{#विद्युत्#}.
.²4 Belonging to, (as a part or portion.)
.²5 Exchange, return, (again, back again, {%re)%} {#तिलेभ्यः प्रति माषान्#} 
{#यच्छति#}.
.²6 A little, &c. as {#शाकप्रति, सूपप्रति#}.
.²7 Opposition in place, as {#प्रतिसूर्य्यं#} against the sun, {#प्रतिवात#}
against the wind.
.²8 With respect to, {#अनेकभार्य्यं, प्रत्याह#}.
.E. {#प्रथ#} to be famous, {#डति#} aff.; in the following compounds the final
{#इ#} of {#प्रति#} is optionally long, before derivatives formed with {#घञ्#},
as {#प्रतिहार#} or {#प्रतीहार#}, &c.
