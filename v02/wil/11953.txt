{#क्रम#}¦ r. 1st cl. ({#उ#}) {#क्रमु#} ({#क्रामति क्राम्यति#} or {#क्रम्यति#})
To go, to walk, to step: (in the following senses it is optionally deponent:)
({#क्रमते क्रम्यते#})
.²1 To proceed uninterruptedly.
.²2 To grow or increase.
.²3 To preserve. It is also active or deponent according to various prefixes and
in various senses, as,
<div n="p">with {#अति#} ({#अतिक्रामति#}) 
.²1 To go over or beyond, to leap over. 
.²2 To transgress, to pass beyond the bounds of virtue.
<div n="p">With {#अनु#} ({#अनुक्रमते#}) To proceed regularly or in order.
<div n="p">With {#अभि#} ({#अभिक्रामति#}) 
.²1 To overcome, to surpass. 
.²2 To assail.
<div n="p">With {#अप#} ({#अपक्रमते#}) To go away, to depart.
<div n="p">With {#आङ्#}, ({#आक्रमते#}) To rise as the sun, &c. or ({#आक्रामति#}) To rise, to mount, to overcome.
<div n="p">With {#उत्#}, ({#उत्क्रामति#}) To transgress.
<div n="p">With {#उप#}, ({#उपक्रामति#}) To depart. ({#उपक्रमते#}) To begin.
<div n="p">With {#निर्#}, ({#निष्क्रामति#}) To go forth.
<div n="p">With {#पर#} and {#आङ्#} ({#पराक्रमति#}) To display valour, to surpass or overcome.
<div n="p">With {#प्र#}, ({#प्रक्रामति#}) To depart, to go or approach, ({#प्रक्रमते#}) To begin.
<div n="p">With {#परि#}, ({#परिक्रामति#}) To wander about, to ramble.
<div n="p">With {#वि#}, ({#विक्रामति#}) 
.²1 To overcome. 
.²2 To mount or ascend. ({#विक्रमते#}) To pace, to proceed by steps, to move with the feet.
<div n="p">With {#वि#} and {#आङ्#} ({#व्याक्रमते#}) To transgress.
<div n="p">With {#सम्#}, ({#संक्रामति#}) to remove.
