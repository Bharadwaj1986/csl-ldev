{#पयोव्रत#}¦ n. ({#-तं#}) Living upon milk for a month, which with prayer
and residence in a cow house, is an expiation for receiving an unsuitable
present; offering of milk to VIṢṆU, and subsisting upon it for twelve days;
also for one or for three days as a religious act.
.E. {#पयस्#} milk, and {#व्रत#} religious observance.
