{#उज्जयनी#}¦ f. ({#-नी#}) {%Ujjayanī%} or {%Oujein,%} a city so called in
{%Mālava,%} formerly the capital of VIKRAMĀDITYA. It is one of the seven
sacred cities of the {%Hindus,%} and the first meridian of their geographers:

.E. {#उत्#} much, {#जयनी#} victorious or place of victory; from the military
character of its people; also {#अवन्ती#} and {#उज्जयिनी#}.
