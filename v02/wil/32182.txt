{#राजसूय#}¦ n. ({#-यं#})
.²1 A sacrifice performed only by a universal monarch, attended by his tributary
princes; as in the case of YUDHIṢṬHIRA, and others.
.²2 A lotus.
.²3 A mountain.
.²4 A sort of rice.
.E. {#राज#} a king, (by him,) and {#षू#} to be produced, to be effected, aff.
{#क्यप्#}; or {#राज#} the moon, for the moon plant, of which the acid juice is
drank at the ceremony, and {#सु#} to bring forth, the same aff., deriv. irr.
