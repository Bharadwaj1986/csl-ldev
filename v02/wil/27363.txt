{#प्लीहन्#}¦ m. ({#-हा#}) The spleen; the organ or the disease of it, as in
English; in the latter sense, however, it is equally applied to the enlargement
of the mesenteric glands, &c.
.E. {#प्लिह#} to increase, Uṇādi aff. {#कनिन्#}, and the vowel made long;
otherwise being {#प्लिहन्#}; and with {#क#} and {#टाप्#} affs., and the vowel
long or short {#प्लिहा#} or {#प्लीहा#}.
