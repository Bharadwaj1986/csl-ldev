{#अत्रि#}¦ m. ({#-त्रिः#}) The name of one of the seven {%Ṛṣis%} or saints,
born from the eye of BRAHMĀ, married to ANASŪYĀ, daughter of KARDAMA MUNI,
and the father of DATTA or DATTĀTREYA, DURVĀSAS and CANDRA.
.E. {#अद#} to eat and {#त्रिप्#} Uṇādi aff. The correct reading is {#अत्त्रि#},
but the word is always written with one {#त#}.
