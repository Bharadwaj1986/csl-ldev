<L>556<pc>013<k1>अजशृङ्गी<k2>अजशृङ्गी
{#अजशृङ्गी#}¦ f. ({#-ङ्गी#}) A plant; described as a milky and thorny plant,
with a fruit of a crooked figure, like a ram's horn, and used as a medicine for
the eyes; vulg, Mera Śṛṅgī, and Kaṅkara Śṛṅgī. See {#कर्क्कटशृङ्गी#}.
.E. {#अज#} a ram, and {#शृङ्गी#} a horn.
