{#वकव्रतिन्#}¦ m. ({#-ती#}) A false devotee, a religious hypocrite, or one who
assumes the appearance of devotion or meditation, for interested purposes.
.E. {#वक#} a crane, {#व्रत#} observance, and {#इनि#} aff.; compared to the
crane, who stands by a pool demure and sly, and apparently absorbed in
contemplation, till he sees a fish on which to dart; also with {#ठक्#}, aff.

[Page728]

{#वकव्रतिक#}, and derived from {#वक#}, and {#चर#} who goes,
{#वकचर#} or {#वकव्रतचर#} m. ({#-रः#}) &c.; see {#वैडालव्रतिक#}.
