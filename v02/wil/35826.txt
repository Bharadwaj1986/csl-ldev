.²1 Discrimination, judgment, the faculty of distinguishing things by their
properties, and classing them according to their real not apparent nature; in
the {%Vedānta%} system, it is applied to the power of separating {%Brahma%} or

.²3 A reservoir, a bath.
.E. {#वि#} severally, {#विच#} to judge, aff. {#घञ्#}.
