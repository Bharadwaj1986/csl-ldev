{#अघमर्षण#}¦ mfn. ({#-णः-णा-णं#}) An expiatory prayer: reciting mentally
a particular passage from the Vedas, while a little water, in the palm of the
right hand, is held to the nose; this forms a part of the daily ceremonies of
the Brahmans. m. ({#-णः#}) The author of the passage recited upon the above
occasion.
.E. {#अघ#} sin, and {#मृष#} to bear with, or to sprinkle, {#युच#} aff.
