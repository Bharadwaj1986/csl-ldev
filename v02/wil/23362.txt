{#परशुराम#}¦ m. ({#-मः#}) A hero and demigod; the first of the three
RĀMAS, and the sixth {%Avatāra%} or descent of the deity VIṢṆU; who appeared
in the world as the son of the saint JAMADAGNI, for the purpose of repressing
the tyranny and punishing the violence of the {%Kṣatriya,%} or military tribe;
PARAŚURĀMA appears to typify the tribe of {%Brahmans%} and their contests with
the {%Kṣetriyas.%}
.E. {#परशु#} an axe, and {#राम#} who delights in; also with {#कन्#} added,
{#परशुरामक#}; and with the first member, {#पर्शु, पर्शुराम#}, q. v.
