{#स्वस्तिवाचन#}¦ n. ({#-नं#}) A religious rite, preparatory to any important
observance, in which the {%Brahmans%} strew boiled rice on the ground, and
invoke the blessings of the gods on the ceremony about to commence.
.E. {#स्वस्ति#} inceptive and benedictory particle, {#वाचन#} uttering.
