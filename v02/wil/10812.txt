{#कुमुदबान्धव#}¦ m. ({#-वः#}) The moon.
.E. {#कुमुद#} a lotus, and {#बान्धव#} the friend; the white esculent lotus
expanding its petals during the night, and closing them in the day time; hence
other similar appellations of the moon, as {#कुमुदवन्धु, कुमुदप्रिय#}, &c.
