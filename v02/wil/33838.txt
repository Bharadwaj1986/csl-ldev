{#वर्षपर्व्वत#}¦ m. ({#-तः#}) A mountainous range, supposed to separate the
various {%Varṣas%} or divisions of the globe from each other: six ranges are
enumerated from south to north; viz. {%Himavān, Hemakūṭa, Niṣadha, Nīla,%} 
{%Śveta,%} and {%Śṛṅgī%} or {%Śṛṅgavān; Meru%} constitutes the
seventh; other names occur.
.E. {#वर्ष#} as above, and {#पर्व्वत#} a mountain.
