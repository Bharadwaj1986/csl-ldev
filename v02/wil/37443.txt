{#शकुन्तला#}¦ f. ({#-ला#}) The daughter of MENAKĀ, the courtezan of
{%Svarga,%} by the sage VIŚVĀMITRA, brought up by KAṆVA the hermit, and
afterwards married to DUṢYANTA, by whom she became the mother of BHARATA, the
sovereign of all {%India.%}
.E. {#शकुन्त#} a bird, {#ला#} to get, aff. {#क#}, and {#टाप्#}, being born in a
solitary forest, and protected by the birds till found by KAṆVA.
