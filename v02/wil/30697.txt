<L>30697<pc>668<k1>मूर्द्धाभिषिक्त<k2>मूर्द्धाभिषिक्त
{#मूर्द्धाभिषिक्त#}¦ m. ({#-क्तः#})
.²1 A man of the second or military class, the {%Kṣatriya%} or soldier.
.²2 A king, a prince.
.²3 A chief minister, a counsellor of state.
.²4 The name of a mixed class, sprung from a {%Brahman,%} and female of the
second or {%Kṣatriya%} tribe; the principal of the mixed races, and soldier by
profession.
.E. {#मूर्द्ध#} the head, and {#अभिषिक्त#} sprinkled, anointed; kings being
consecrated by having poured on their heads, while seated on a throne prepared
for the purpose, water from some sacred stream, mixed with honey, clarified
butter, and spirituous liquor, as well as two sorts of grass and the sprouts of
corn: the term applies to the {%Kṣatriya%} as identified with the king, the
duties of royalty, belonging especially, to the military caste.
