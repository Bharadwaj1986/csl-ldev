{#त्रसरेणु#}¦ mf. ({#-णुः#}) An atom or mote visible in a sunbeam, especially
considered as an ideal weight, either of the lowest denomination, or equal to
three, or according to some, thirty invisible atoms. f. ({#-णुः#}) A wife of the
sun.
.E. {#त्रस#} moveable, and {#रेणु#} dust; also read {#त्र्यमरेणु#}.
