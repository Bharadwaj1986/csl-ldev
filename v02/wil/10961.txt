mountains, which are supposed to exist in each {%Varṣa%} or division of a

{%Sahya, Śuktimān, Ṛkṣa, Vindhya,%} and {%Pāripātra.%}
.E. {#कुल#} a race, and {#अचल#} a mountain; also {#कुलाद्रि#}, &c.
