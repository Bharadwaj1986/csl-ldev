{#सर्व्ववेदस्#}¦ m. ({#-दाः#}) A man who gives away all his property to the
priests who have been employed by him at particular sacrifices, of which such a
destination of the principal's whole wealth is an essential part.
.E. {#सर्व्व#} all, {#विद#} to gain, causal form, aff. {#असुन्#}.
