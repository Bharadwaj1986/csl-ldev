{#शोभाञ्जन#}¦ m. ({#-नः#}) A tree, commonly {%Sajinā%} or {%Sahajnā,%}
(<bot>Hyperanthera morunga</bot>;) the legumes, blossoms, and leaves are esculent, and the
root of the young tree is used as a substitute for horse-radish: in medicine the
root is used as a rubefacient externally, as a stimulant internally, and the
expressed oil of the seeds is employed to relieve arthritic pains, &c.
.E. {#शोभा#} beauty, {#अञ्ज#} to pervade or anoint, and {#ल्युट्#} or {#युच्#}
aff; also {#शौभाञ्जन#} and {#सोभाञ्जन#}, &c.
