{#मेदस्#}¦ n. ({#-दः#})
.²1 Marrow; applicable not merely to the marrow of the bones, but more properly,
to the serous or adipose secretion that spreads amongst the muscular fibres, and
which is considered as performing the same functions to the flesh that the
marrow of the bones performs to them: in {%Hindu%} physiology its proper seat is
said to be the abdomen.
.²2 Morbid or unnatural corpulency.
.E. {#मिद#} to be unctuous, {#असुन्#} aff.
