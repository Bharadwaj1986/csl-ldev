{#विद्या#}¦ f. ({#-द्या#}) Knowledge, learning, science, whether sacred or
profane, though more especially the former: it is sometimes classed into
fourteen divisions; or the four {%Vedas;%} the six {%Aṅgas,%} or grammar,
astronomy, &c.; the {%Purāṇas%} as the eleventh class; and the {%Mīmāṃsā%} or
theology, {%Nyāya%} or logic, and {%Dharma%} or law, as the remaining three.
.²2 The goddess DURGĀ.
.²3 A tree, (<bot>Premna spinosa</bot>.)
.²4 A magical pill or bolus, by putting which into the mouth a person has the
power of ascending to heaven.
.E. {#विद#} to know, and aff. {#क्यप्#}.
