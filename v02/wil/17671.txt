{#तुरङ्गवदन#}¦ m. ({#-नः#}) A celestial quirister.
.E. {#तुरङ्ग#} a horse, and {#वदन#} a face; these beings are described as
having the bodies of men, and the heads of horses; also similar compounds, as
{#तुरङ्गानन, तुरङ्गवक्त्र, तुगङ्गास्य, अश्ववक्त्र#}, &c.
