{#अनुमरण#}¦ n. ({#-णं#}) Dying with or following in death; the term usually
denotes the cremation of a Hindu widow, whose husband's corpse is not on the
spot, and with part of whose dress she, therefore, ascends the pile: this is
prohibited to {%Brahman%} women. It is also synonymous with {#सहमरण#}
burning with the body.
.E. {#अनु#} with or after, and {#मरण#} dying: dying with her husband.
