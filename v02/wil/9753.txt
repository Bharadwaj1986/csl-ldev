{#काकपक्ष#}¦ m. ({#-क्षः#}) The side locks of the head of hair; three or five
on each side left when the head is first shaved, and which may be allowed to
remain, especially in persons of the military cast.
.E. {#काक#} and {#पक्ष#} a feather, a wing, a crow's wing; the word is also
used as an epithet of a warrior, a name of RĀMA, &c.
