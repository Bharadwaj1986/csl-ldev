{#विश्वामिच#}¦ m. ({#-चः#}) A {%Muni,%} the son of GĀDHI, originally of the
military order, but who became by long and painful austerities a {%Brahmarṣi,%}
in which character he appears in the {%Rāmāyaṇa,%} as the early preceptor and
counsellor of RĀMA.
.E. {#विश्व#} all, {#मिच#} the friend, and the vowel of the prefix made long in
this sense; if attributively used, the compound is {#विश्वमिच#}.
