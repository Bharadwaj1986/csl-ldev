{#उपनिधि#}¦ m. ({#-धिः#}) A deposit, a pledge, property put into the care of
a creditor, friend, &c.
.E. {#उप#} and {#नि#} before {#धा#} to have, {#कि#} affix; in law this word
ordinarily implies more especially a sealed or enclosed deposit, but according
to some, it is any article entrusted to a friend which he may use whilst in his
keeping.
