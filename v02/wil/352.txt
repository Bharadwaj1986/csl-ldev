{#अग्रहायण#}¦ m. ({#-णः#}) The name of a month; the eighth of the lunar year
of the Hindus, when the moon is full near the head of Orion, or about
November-December.
.E. {#अग्र#} and {#हायन#}, year; according to the ancient system, the first
month of the Hindu year: also {#आग्रहायण#}.
