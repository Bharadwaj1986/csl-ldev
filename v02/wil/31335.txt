{#यवमध्य#}¦ n. ({#-ध्यं#}) A form of penance, diminishing the food daily
during the dark fortnight, fasting on the new moon, and gradually augmenting the
food till the full moon. f. ({#-ध्या#}) A form of metre, a triplet, the
divisions of which are of unequal length, the middle line having one syllable
more than the first or last.
.E. {#यव#} barley, {#मध्य#} centrical.
