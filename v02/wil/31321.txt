{#यवक्षार#}¦ m. ({#-रः#}) Saltpetre, nitre, nitrate of potash.
.E. {#यव#} barley, and {#क्षार#} ashes or salt; being prepared, according to
some of the commentators, accurately enough, from burnt barley, the potash being
thus procured; according to others however it is so termed, from the crystals
being supposed to resemble the spike of the plant.
