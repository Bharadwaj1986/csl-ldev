{#द्विमातृज#}¦ mfn. ({#-जः-जा-जं#}) Having two mothers, born in two ways; it
may be said of some of the deities, thus GAṆEŚA was the son of PĀRVATĪ, but
not born in the usual way, being made of the scurf of her body: see
{#द्वैमातुर#}.
.E. {#द्वि#} two, {#मातृ#} a mother, and {#ज#} born.
