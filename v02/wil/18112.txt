{#त्रिपुण्ड्र#}¦ n. ({#-ण्ड्रं#}) Three curved horizontal marks, made across the
forehead with cowdung, ashes, &c.; they are worn especially by the followers of
ŚIVA, or ŚAKTI, and are indispensable in proceeding to worship the former.
.E. {#त्रि#} three, triple, and {#पुण्ड्र#} a line; also with {#उन्#} added
{#त्रिपुण्ड्रक#}.
