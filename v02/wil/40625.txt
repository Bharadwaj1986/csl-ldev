<L>40625<pc>897<k1>समानोदक<k2>समानोदक
{#समानोदक#}¦ m. ({#-कः#}) A kinsman, one who when distinct from the
{%Sapiṇḍa%} is next in order and succession, and is connected by oblations of
water only, to the manes of common ancestors; this relationship extends to the
fourteenth in descent, i. e. the seven first or {%Sapin'da's%} presenting water
as well as the cakes, are also {%Samānodakas,%} whilst the seven next
presenting water alone are {%Samānodakas%} only.
.E. {#समान#} common, {#उदक#} water.
