{#जह्नुतनया#}¦ f. ({#-या#}) The Ganges.
.E. {#जह्नु#}, and {#तनया#} the daughter; the river in its course disturbed
the devotions of the saint JAHNU, upon which he drank up its waters; having
released them at the intercession of BHAGĪRATHA, he is thus considered as the
parent of the stream: see {#जाह्नवी#}; also {#जह्नुसुता#}, &c.
