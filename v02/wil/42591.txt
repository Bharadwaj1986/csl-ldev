{#सूत्र#}¦ m. ({#-त्रं#})
.²1 A thread in general.
.²2 A rule, a precept, in morals or science; a short, obscure, and technical
sentence, enjoining some observance in law or religion, or intimating some rule
in grammar, logic, &c.; in each case it is the fundamental and primitive part of
{%Hindu%} learning, and is the form in which the works of the early and supposed
inspired writers appear; the ingenuity and labour of subsequent authors having
expanded and explained the original {%Sūtras%} in various commentaries and
glosses.
.²3 An opinion or decree in law.
.²4 A string, a collection of threads, as that worn by the three first classes,
&c.
.E. {#षिव#} to sew, {#ष्ट्रन्#} Uṇādi aff., and {#इव#} changed to {#ऊ#}, or
