.²1 The {%Vīṇā,%} or {%Indian%} lute, a fretted instrument of the guitar kind,
usually having seven wires or strings, and a large gourd at each end of the
finger board; the extent of the instrument is two octaves: it is supposed to be
the invention of NĀRADA, the son of BRAHMĀ, and has many varieties,
enumerated according to the number of strings, &c.
