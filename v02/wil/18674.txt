{#दशकामजव्यसन#}¦ n. ({#-नं#}) The aggregate of ten vices, proceeding
from the quality of desire, viz. hunting, gambling, sleeping during the day,
abusiveness, lechery, drunkenness, dancing, singing, playing, and hypocrisy.
.E. {#दश#} ten, {#काम#} desire, {#ज#} born, {#व्यसन#} calamity.
