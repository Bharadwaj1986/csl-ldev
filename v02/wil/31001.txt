{#मेषाक्षिकुसुम#}¦ m. ({#-मः#}) A sort of Cassia, (C. tora.)
.E. {#मेष#} a ram, {#अक्षि#} the eye, and {#कुसुम#} a flower; which is compared
to a sheep's eye; also analogous terms, as {#मेषलोचन#}, &c.; it is also read
{#मेषाख्य#} and {#मेषाह्व#}, &c. as being named from the {%Meṣa%} or ram.
