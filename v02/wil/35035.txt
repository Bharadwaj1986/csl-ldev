{#वितर्द्धि#}¦ f. ({#-र्द्धिः-र्द्धी#})
.²1 A quadrangular building, or sort of covered terrace of wood in the centre of
the court-yard of a temple or palace.
.²2 A quadrangular resting place made of wood, a seat, a bench.
.²3 A place in a court-yard for sitting in or standing under.
.²4 A floor on four posts in the midst of a quadrangular house.
.²5 A temporary stage or shed in the middle of a court-yard, for performing
sacrifices, representing plays, &c.
.²6 A terrace furnished with pillars, a verandah, a balcony.
.²7 A covered temporary terrace on which idols are sometimes placed and
worshipped.
.E. {#वि#} privative, {#तर्द#} to injure, aff. {#इन्#}; also {#ङीष्#} optionally
added, and with {#कन्#} added in the fem. form {#वितर्द्धिका#}.
