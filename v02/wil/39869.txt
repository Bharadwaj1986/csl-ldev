{#सकुल्य#}¦ m. ({#-ल्यः#})
.²1 A kinsman, one of the same family name and common origin: see {#सगोत्र#}.
.²2 A distant kinsman or relation; one who shares a divided oblation, as the
grandson's grandson or other descendant as far as three degrees from him, or the
offspring of the grandfather's grandfather or other remoter ancestor.
.E. {#स#} same, {#कुल्य#} of family: some authorities extend the name of
{%Sakulya%} to the tenth descendant.
