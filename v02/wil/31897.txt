<L>31897<pc>695<k1>रथयात्रा<k2>रथयात्रा
{#रथयात्रा#}¦ f. ({#-त्रा#}) A festival at which the chariots of the gods go
in procession: in Bengal and Orissa it is applied to the procession of the car
of KṚṢṆA, as JAGANNĀTHA, on the second of the light fortnight of
{%Āṣārha.%}
.E. {#रथ#}, and {#यात्रा#} going.
