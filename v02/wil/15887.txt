.²1 {%Jambu Dvīpa,%} said to be so named from the {%Jambu tree%} abounding in
it, and implying, according to the {%Purāṇas,%} the central division of the
world, or the known world: according to the {%Bauddhas,%} it is confined to
{%India.%}
.²2 A fabulous river, said to flow from the mountain {%Meru.%} f. ({#-म्बुः#}) A
fruit tree, the rose apple, (<bot>Eugenia</bot> jambolana;) also applied to every species
of <bot>Eugenia</bot>. fn. ({#-म्बुः-म्बु#}) The fruit.
.E. {#जम#} to eat, {#बुक्#} augment, and {#कु#} or {#कू#} Uṇādi affix; hence
