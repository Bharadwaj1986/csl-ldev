{#कुत्स#}¦ {%kutsa, as,%} m., N. of a Ṛṣi with the
epithet Ārjuneya, author of several hymns of the
Ṛg-veda (when attacked by the demon Śuṣṇa,
Indra defended him and killed the demon; but in
other hymns of the Ṛg-veda, Kutsa is represented as
persecuted by Indra); N. of a descendant of Aṅgiras,
author of several other hymns of the Ṛg-veda;
(pl.) the descendants or the family of Kutsa; light-
ning, thunderbolt; [cf. {%kautsa, puru-kutsa,%} &c.]
{%—Kutsa-kuśikikā,%} f. the intermarriage of the
Kutsa and Kuśika families. {%—Kutsa-putra%} or {%kutsa-%}
{%vatsa, as,%} m. a son of Kutsa.
