{#व्यूह्#}¦ {%vy-ūh%} ({%vi-ūh,%} see rt. 1. {%ūh%}), cl. 1. P.
{%-ūhati, -ūhitum,%} to push or move apart, place
asunder, separate, divide, distribute; to separate,
resolve (vowels, syllables, &c. in Vedic grammar);
to dispose, arrange, place in order; to array, to place
in battle-array, arrange in order of battle; to move
from the proper place, change the position, trans-
pose, alter, disarrange.
