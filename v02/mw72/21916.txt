{%Damūnas, ās, ās, as,%}¦ Ved. belonging to the
house or family, devoted to the house or family;
a friend of the house; epithet of Agni, Savitṛ, Indra,
&c.; (Sāy.) of subdued mind, docile. In Ṛg-veda
V. 42, 12, the pl. is regarded by Sāy. as an epithet
of the Ṛbhus.
