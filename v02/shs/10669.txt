{#कुडव#}¦ m. ({#-वः#}) A measure of grain, wood, iron, &c. the fourth part of a
Prastha, described by some as a vessel four fingers wide, and
as many deep, and containing twelve Prakritis or handfuls: it is
also said to contain thirteen and a half cubic Angulas; or to be a
finger and half deep, and three fingers each length and breadth.
In medicine it is equal to two Prakritis or thirty-two Tolakas. E.
{#कुड्#} to heap, {#कवन्#} affix; also {#कुडप#} and {#कुटप#}.
