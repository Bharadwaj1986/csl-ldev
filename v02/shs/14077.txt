{#गोविन्द#}¦ m. ({#-न्दः#}) 1. One of the most usual appellations of KRISHNA or
VISHNU in that form. 2. A name of VRIHASPATI, regent of
JUPITER. 3. A cow-keeper, one who protects, cherishes or owns
cattle. E. {#गो#} language, here the language of the Vedas especially,
[Page248-b+ 60]
and {#विन्द#} who knows, from {#विद्#} to know, with {#श#} affix; also {#गो#}
heaven, a cow, {#विद्#} to obtain, by whom heaven is obtained, or who
obtains felicity by protecting kine. {#गां वेदमयीं वाणीं, भुवं धेनुं स्वर्गं वा#}
