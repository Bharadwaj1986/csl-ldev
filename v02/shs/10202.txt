{#कालञ्जर#}¦ m. ({#-रः#}) 1. A name of SIVA. 2. The name of a rock in Bun-
delkhand, the modern Kallinjer. 3. The adjacent country. 4. An
assembly or collection of religious mendicants; Kallinjer is one
of the places at which such assemblies meet, being enumerated in
the Vedas amongst the Tapasyasthanas, or spots adapted to pra-
ctices of austere devotion. f. ({#-रा#} or {#-री#}) An appellation of DURGA,
E. {#काल#} in the second case, time or death, and {#जर#} who makes old,
or causes to decay; irregularly deduced from the causal of {#जॄ#} to
become old.
