{#वेदाङ्ग#}¦ m. ({#-ङ्गः#}) A sacred science, considered as subordinate to, and in
some sense, a part of the Vedas: six sciences come under this
denomination; ŚIKSHĀ, or the science of pronunciation and articu-
lation; KALPA, the detail of religious ceremonies; VYĀKARAṆA, or
grammar; CHHANDAS, prosody; JYOTISH, or astronomy; and NI-
RUKTĪ, or the explanation of the difficult or obscure words and
phrases that occur in the Vedas. E. {#वेद#} the Vedas, {#अङ्ग#} a member.
