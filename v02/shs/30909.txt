{#महादेव#}¦ m. ({#-वः#}) ŚIVA. f. ({#-वी#}) DURGĀ the wife of ŚIVA. E. {#महा#} great,
and {#देव#} god; the epithet Mahā compounded with a substantive
forms many appellatives of these deities especially; thus ŚIVA
is Mahākāla, Mahārūdra, Maheśwara, &c. and DURGĀ is named
Mahāvidya, Mahāsmriti, Mahāmedha, Mahāmoha, &c. &c.
