{#प्रस्तावना#}¦ f. ({#-ना#}) 1. Commencement, introduction. 2. A dramatic prelude,
an introductory dialogue, usually spoken by the manager and one
of the actors. 3. Praising, praise. E. {#प्र#} before, {#स्तु#} to praise, aff.
{#ल्युट्;#} the commencement of books, &c. being usually the praise
of gods or princes. It is thus defined by Bharata:--{#“नटी विदूषको#}
{#वापि पारिपार्श्विक एव वा । सूत्रधारेण सहिताः संलापं यत्र कुर्वते । आमुखं नाम#}
{#तज्ज्ञेयं सैव प्रस्तावना मता ।”#}
