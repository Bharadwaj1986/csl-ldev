{#पद्मिन्#}¦ m. ({#द्मी-#}) An elephant. f. ({#-नी#}) 1. A multitude of lotuses, or
place abounding in them. 2. A woman of one of the four classes
into which the sex is distinguished; the first and the most
excellent. She is thus described in Vāchaspatya:--
<Poem>{#भवति कमलनेत्रा नासिकाक्षुद्ररन्ध्रा ।#}
{#अविरलकुचयुग्मा दीर्घकेशी कृशाङ्गी ॥#}
{#मृदुचरणसुशीला गीतनृत्यानुरक्ता ।#}
{#सकलतनुसुवेशा पद्मिनी पद्मगन्धा ॥#}</Poem>
3. A lotus, (Nelumbium speciosum.) 4. A pool or pond, espe-
cially one deep enough for the growth of the lotus. 5. The
fibrous stalk of a lotus. 6. A female elephant. E. {#पद्म#} coloured
marks. &c. or a lotus, {#इनि#} aff.
