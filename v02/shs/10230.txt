{#कालरात्रि#}¦ m. ({#-त्रिः#}) 1. A particular night, one which occurs on the 7th
day of the 7th month of every 77th years; after such a period of
life, a man is exempt from attention to the usual ordinances. 2.
The last night of a Kalpa 3. A dark night. 4. A form of Sakti
or DURGA. E. {#काल#} black, time, and {#रात्रि#} a night: see {#भीमरथी#}.
