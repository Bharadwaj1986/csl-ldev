{#व्यवहारविषय#}¦ mn. ({#-यः-यं#}) A title of jurisprudence, an act which may
become the object of dispute amongst men, and should therefore be
regulated by law; according to MANU, there are eighteen heads,
but these are the principal only, and many things not comprised
under them may still be actionable; the eighteen are:--1. {#ऋणादानं#}
debt; 2. {#निक्षेपः#} deposit; 3. {#अस्वामिविक्रयः#} sale without ownership;
4. {#सम्भूयसमुत्थानं#} concerns amongst partners; 5. {#दत्तस्यानपकर्म्म#} subtrac-
tion of what has been given; 6. {#वेतनादानं#} non-payment of wages;
7. {#सम्बिद्व्यतिक्रमः#} non-performance of agreement; 8. {#क्रयविक्रयानुशयः#}
[Page697-a+ 60]
rescission of sale and purchase; 9. {#स्वामिपालयोर्विवादः#} disputes
between master and servant; 10. {#सीमाविवादः#} disputes about bound-
aries; 11. {#वाक्पारुष्यं#} defamation, abuse; 12. {#दण्डपारुष्यं#} assault; 13.
{#स्तेयं#} theft, larceny; 14. {#साहसं#} robbery, and other violence; 15.
{#स्त्रीसंग्रहणं#} adultery; 16. {#स्त्रीपुंधर्म्मः#} duties of man and wife; 17. {#विभागः#}
portioning of property, inheritance; 18. {#द्यूतं#} gambling of any kind
as fighting animals, laying wagers, &c. E. {#व्यवहार#} legal process,
and {#विषय#} object.
