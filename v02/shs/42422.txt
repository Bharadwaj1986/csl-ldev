{#सपिण्डीकरण#}¦ n. ({#-णं#}) 1. Offering food to the deceased relatives called
Sapiṇḍas: to be performed at the end of a full year after the
death of a relative; but can be performed on the twelfth day
after death by Brāhmins only, on some special occasions. 2.
Investing a person with the rights of relationship, indicated by
offering the funeral cake. E. {#सपिण्ड, करण#} making, {#च्वि#} aff.
