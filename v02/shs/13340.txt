{#गर्भागार#}¦ n. ({#-रं#}) 1. An inner and private room, the female apartments, the
lying-in chamber. 2. The sanctuary of a temple. The chamber where
the object of worship is placed. E. {#गर्भ#} the fœtus, and {#आगार#} an
abode, hence it is applied to the womb: see {#गर्भाशय#}.
