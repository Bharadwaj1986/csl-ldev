{#प्राकृतमित्र#}¦ m. ({#-त्रः#}) A natural friend or ally; as applied to a sovereign,
it means one whose states are removed by an intervening kingdom
from those to which it should be friendly, the {#प्राकृतारि#} or natural
enemy is the chief of the intervening state, and the {#प्राकृतोदासीनः#}
or natural neutral is the outer one of the four. E. {#प्राकृत#} natural,
and {#मित्र#} a friend.
