({#-र्त्त#}) 1. An act of pious liberality, as digging a well, planting a
grove, building a temple, &c. 2. Nourishing, cherishing. It is
thus defined in Vāchaspatya:--{#“वापीकूपतडागादि देवता यतनानि च । अन्न#}
{#प्रदानमारामः पूर्तमित्यभिघीयते” ॥#} E. {#पृ#} to cherish or fill, aff. {#क्त,#} and
the vowel changed.
