{#पुत्त्रिकापुत्त्र#}¦ m. ({#-त्त्रः#}) 1. The son of a daughter, appointed to raise issue
for her father; one of the twelve heirs acknowledged by the old
Hindu law. 2. A daughter's son, who by agreement becomes
the son of her father, (according to some interpretation.) 3. A
daughter who returns to her father's house being considered as
his son, (according to other interpretation.) 4. A grandson in
general. E. {#पुत्त्रिका#} a daughter, and {#पुत्त्र#} a son.
