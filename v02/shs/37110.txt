{#विभागभाक्#}¦ m. ({#-भाक्#}) One who shares in a portion of property already
distributed, applied especially to a son by a father and mother of
the same tribe, born subsequent to a distribution of property
amongst his parents and brethren, in which case he inherits the
portion allotted or reserved to the parents. E. {#विभाग#} a portion, and
{#भाक्#} who shares.
