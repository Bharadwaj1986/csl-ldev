{#सैरिन्ध्र#}¦ m. ({#-न्धः#}) A menial servant, one employed to dress and decorate
the person and perform all servile offices; also who may be
employed in the chase, snaring animals, &c. the offspring of a
Dasyu by an Ayogava female. f. ({#-न्ध्री#}) 1. An independent female
artizan working in another person's house. 2. A female attendant
in the women's apartments. 3. A woman of one of the mixed
and degraded classes. 4. DRAUPADĪ, the wife of the PĀṆḌU
princes, and who upon the temporary servitude of her husbands,
became workwoman in the house of the Rāja of Virāta. E. {#सीर#}
a plough, {#धृ#} to hold or take, aff. {#क,#} and {#मुक्#} augment, {#अण्#} added
and the penultimate vowel changed optionally to {#इ;#} whence also
{#सैरन्ध्री,#} or {#स्वैर#} independent, {#धृ#} to hold or be, aff. {#कन्,#} fem. aff. {#ङीष्,#}
and form irr.
