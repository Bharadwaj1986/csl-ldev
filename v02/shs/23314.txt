{#परमाणु#}¦ m. ({#-णुः#}) 1. An atom, the invisible base of all aggregate bodies;
thirty of them are supposed to form a mote in a sunbeam;
the lowest measure of weight; in Logic, six of them form the
first perceptible object. 2. A measure of time, the sun's passage
past an atom of matter. E. {#परम#} first, and {#अणु#} an atom.
