{#यवमध्य#}¦ n. ({#-ध्यं#}) A form of penance, diminishing the food daily during

menting the food till the full-moon. f. ({#-ध्या#}) A form of metre, a

line having one syllable more than the first or last. E. {#यव#} barley,
{#मध्य#} centrical.
