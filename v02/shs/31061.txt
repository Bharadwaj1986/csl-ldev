<L>31061<pc>560-a<k1>महासान्तपन<k2>महासान्तपन
{#महासान्तपन#}¦ n. ({#-नं#}) A sort of penance; subsisting for six successive
days, respectively, on cow's urine, cowdung, milk, curds, Ghee, and
water in which Kūsa grass has been boiled, and fasting on the
seventh day: instead of one day some authorities assign a period
of three days to each, considering the first as the common Sānta-
pana penance; others omit the two last periods, making it last
fifteen days. E. {#महा#} great, {#सान्तपन#} a penance.
