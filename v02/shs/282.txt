{#अग्निमारुति#}¦ m. ({#-तिः#}) A name of the saint, AGASTYA. E. {#अग्नि#} fire, i. e. the
fire of the stomach, the digestive faculty, and {#मारुति#} a name of
HANUMAN; because the saint could digest as well as the monkey;
the epithet alludes to a story of his devouring and digesting
VĀTĀPI, a demon, who had assumed the form of a sheep.
