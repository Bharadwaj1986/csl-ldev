{#परि(र)णाम#}¦ m. ({#-मः#}) 1. Change of form or state. 2. Maturity, fulness,
ripeness. 3. End, last stage or state. 4. Digestion. 5. Result.
6. A figure of speech closely resembling Rupaka and minutely
distinguished by it; it consists in transferring the properties
of any object to that with which it is compared. E. {#परि#} before,
{#नम्#} to bend, aff. {#घञ्#}.
