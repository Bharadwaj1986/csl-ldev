{#पितृगण#}¦ m. ({#-णः#}) A class of progenitors, the sons of the Rishis or
Prajāpatis: the principal classes are the Somasads descended
from VIRĀT, the Agnishwattās from MARĪCHI, Bārhishads from
ATRI, Somapās from BHRIGU, Havishmats from ANGIRAS,
Ājyapas from PULASTYA, Sukālins from VASHISTHA; there are
also the Agnidaghas and Anagnidagdhas, Kavyas and Saumyas.
E. {#पितृ#} father, (of mankind,) {#गण#} a troop.
