{#राज्याङ्ग#}¦ n. ({#-ङ्गं#}) A requisite of regal administration; variously enu-
merated, as seven, eight, or nine, viz.:--first, the monarch;
second, the prime minister; third, a friend or ally; fourth, trea-
sure; fifth territory; sixth a strong-hold; and seventh, an army;
the eighth is the population or companies of citizens, and the
ninth the Purohita or spiritual adviser. E. {#राज्य#} royalty, and {#अङ्ग#}
limb or part.
