<L>46479<pc>826-a<k1>हंहो<k2>हंहो
{#हंहो#}¦ Ind. 1. An interjection of calling, (ho, hollo!) 2. A particle of
haughtiness or arrogance. 3. An arrogantly interrogative particle.
In drama it is found used as a form of address in speeches
assigned to characters of the middling class. E. {#हं#} and {#हो#} both
vocative particles.
