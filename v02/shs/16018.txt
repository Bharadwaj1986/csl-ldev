{#जलयन्त्रगृह#}¦ n. ({#-हं#}) A house or fabric erected in the midst of water, or
subterraneous apartments constructed in the bank of a river, for
retiring to in the hot season; a summer house, &c. E. {#जलयन्त्र#} a
water-work, and {#गृह#} a house; also similar compounds, as {#जलयन्त्र-#}
{#निकेतन, जलयन्त्रमन्दिर,#} &c.
