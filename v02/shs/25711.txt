{#पूर्व्वफल्गुनी#}¦ f. ({#-नी#}) The eleventh lunar asterism; the first Phalguni,
the twelfth Nakshatra, being termed Uttara or subsequent; this
asterism figured by a couch contains two stars, one of which is
the Leonis. E. {#पूर्व्व#} first, and {#फल्गुनी#} the name of the two asterisms.
