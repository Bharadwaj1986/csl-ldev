{#सत्प्रतिपक्ष#}¦ m. ({#-क्षः#}) Contrariety of doctrine or argument, knowledge of
the opposite arguments proving the existence or non-existence of
a thing; viz:--sound is eternal because it is audible; on the

logic.) E. {#सत्#} being, {#प्रतिपक्ष#} opposition.
