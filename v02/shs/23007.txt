{#पतञ्ज(ञ्च)लि#}¦ m. ({#-लिः#}) 1. The name of a saint or Muni and teacher of the
Yoga philosophy, thence called after him PĀTANJALA; also the
author of Mahābhāshya or commentary on PĀNINI. 2. A name of
the poet VARARUCHI. E. {#पत्#} falling, {#अञ्जलि#} the hands joined as a
mark of respect; having fallen from heaven, it is said, in the
shape of small snake, into the hands of the saint PĀNINI as he
was performing this act of reverence.
