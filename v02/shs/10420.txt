{#किम्पुरुव#}¦ m. ({#-षः#}) 1. A heavenly musician: see {#किन्नर#}. 2. A division of
the universe, one of the nine Khandas or portions into which the
world is divided, and described as the country between the
Himachala and Hemakuta mountains. 3. A despicable or low man.
E. {#किम्#} what, contemptuously, and {#पुरुष#} a man; also with the first
vowel of that word lengthened, {#किम्पूरुष#}.
