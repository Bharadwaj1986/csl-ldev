{#त्रिवेणी#}¦ f. ({#-णी#}) A name of the Ganges. E. {#त्रि#} three, {#वेणी#} a braid of hair:
the name is especially applied to the river at Allahabad, where it
[Page326-a+ 60]
receives the Jumuna, and is supposed to receive under ground the
Saraswati; it is also that of a village in Bengal, above Hugli.
