{#शतभिषा#}¦ f. ({#-षा#}) The twenty-fifth of the Nakshatras or lunar mansions,
containing 100 stars, one of which is Aquarii. E. {#शत#} a hundred,
{#भिषज्#} to cure, aff. {#ड;#} requiring many physicians; as the Hindus
say DHANWANTARI himself can not cure any person affected with
disease, whilst the moon is in this asterism.
