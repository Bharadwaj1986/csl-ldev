<L>9420<pc>163-a<k1>कर्म्मविपाक<k2>कर्म्मविपाक
{#कर्म्मविपाक#}¦ m. ({#-कः#}) 1. Future reward or punishment, the consequence
of human acts in a subsequent birth. 2. A work on this subject,
explaining expiatory rites to be performed in cases of disease, &c.
supposed to be the punishment of offences in a previous existence.
E. {#कर्म्म,#} and {#विपाक#} maturity.
