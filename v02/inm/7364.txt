{@Mṛgasvapnodbhavaparvan@}¦ (“the dream of the
deer,” the 44th of the minor parvans of Mhbhr.). (Cf.
Mṛgasvapnanidarśana, Mṛgasvapnodbhava.) § 518: In
consequence of a dream of {%Yudhiṣṭhira%}, in which the still
remnant deer of {%Dvaitavana%} entreated him to spare them by
changing his residence, the {%Pāṇḍavas%} with {%Indrasena%}, etc.,
and the {%brahmans%} removed to the {%Kāmyaka%} forest, situated
at the head of the desert, near lake {%Tṛṇabindu%}. They had
then dwelt in {%Dvaitavana%} during a year and eight months
(III, {@258@}).
