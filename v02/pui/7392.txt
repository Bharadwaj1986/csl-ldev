<L>7392<pc>2-269<k1>न्यास<k2>न्यास
{%Nyāsa(mantra)%}¦ — in connection with the installation of
an image; eg. {%Ratnanyāsa%} for the eight directions: diamond,
pearl, vaiḍūrya, conch, spaṭika, puṣparāga, indranīla and
nīla; pastes of eight kinds: tālaka, śilāvajra, collyrium,
śyāma, kañji, kāśi, mākṣikam, and gairikam; grains of eight
kinds: wheat, barley, seasamum, mudga, nīvāra, śyāmaka,
sarṣapa, and vrīhirice, scents like sandal, red sandal, agaru,
añjanam, uśīram, vaiṣṇavī sahadevī, and Lakṣmaṇa; metals
of eight kinds: gold, vidruma, copper, bronze, ārakūṭaka,
silver, puṣpam and iron. Then the worship of the Lokhapālas
with {%Nyāsamantras.%}
