{%Kāśyapas (Kāśapas)%}¦ — the lines of kings commencing
with Sannuhotra Kāśa, ruled at Kāśī. Six of them were
{%Brahmavādins.%} Among them were Brāhmaṇas and Kṣa-
triyas whose sons were righteous, powerful and strong.
