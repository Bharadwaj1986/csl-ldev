<L>14515<pc>3-432<k1>शिशुनाग<k2>शिशुनाग
{%Śiśunāgas%}¦ — ten in number commencing with Śiśunāga,
and ending with Mahānandi; these ruled for 360 years.<sup>1</sup>
These are Kṣatrabandhus and contemporaries of Ikṣvākus,
Pāñcalas, Kālakas, Haihayas, Ekalingas, Śakas, Kuravas,
Maithilas, Sūrasenas, Vītihotras.^2 (See also Śiśunāka).
<F>1) Bhā. XII. 1. 7; Br. III. 74. 127-35.</F>
