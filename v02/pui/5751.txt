{%Dakṣasāvarṇi%}¦ — the ninth Manu born of Varuṇa.<sup>1</sup>
Three groups of twelve gods in each of Pāra, Mārīcigarbha
and Sudharmāna. Their Indra was Adbhuta. Savana, Dyu-
timan and others were seven sages. Father of Dhṛtiketu
and other sons.<sup>2</sup>
<F>1) Bhā. VIII. 13. 18.</F>
<F>2) Vi. III. 2. 20-24.</F>
