<L>6626<pc>303-a<k1>अभूमि<k2>अभूमि
{#अभूमि#}¦ Tatpur. f. ({#-मिः#}) <sup>1</sup> Anything but earth, anything but
a solid ground; e. g. {%Kātyāy. Śrautas.:%} {#शूलं चाभूमौ#} ({%Yā-%}
{%jnikad.:%} {#अध्वर्युस्तद्धृदयशूलं न पृथिव्यां नाप्सु निक्षिपेत्,#} he is
not to put it down anywhere, i. e. he is to hold it up).
<sup>2</sup> No object for; e. g. {%Śākunt.:%} {#स खलु मनोरथानामभूमिर्वि-#}
{#सर्जनावसरसत्कारः#} (comm. {#अभूमिः = अविषयः#}) ‘the honour
(Indra conferred on me) at the occasion of dismissing me,
was indeed no object for (i. e. beyond) my wishes’; or
{%Śiśupālabadha:%} {#अभूदभूमिः प्रतिपक्षजन्मनां भियाम्#} (comm.
{#अभूमिरविषयः#}) ‘he was no object for fear from enemies’,
i. e. he was fearless. [<sup>3</sup> A bad or unsuitable ground.] E.
{#अ#} neg. or deter. and {#भूमि#}.
