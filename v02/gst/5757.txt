{#अब्ज#}¦ Tatpur. 1. m. f. n. ({#-ब्जः-ब्जा-ब्जम्#}) Born or produced
in, or by, water.
<div n="P">2. m. ({#-ब्जः#}) <sup>1</sup> The moon. <sup>2</sup> Dhanwantari, the physi-
cian of the gods (he having arisen from the ocean when it
was churned for the amṛta). <sup>3</sup> A fish. <sup>4</sup> The ratan (Calamus
rotang). <sup>5</sup> The name of two other aquatic plants: <sup>a.</sup> Valisneria
octandra; <sup>b.</sup> Barringtonia acutangula. <sup>6</sup> The proper name of
the father of Viśala; see also {#अब्जककुभ#}. <sup>7</sup> (In Arithmetic
sometimes used to denote) the numeral {%1.%} Comp. {#चन्द्र#}.
<div n="P">3. m. n. ({#-ब्जः-ब्जम्#}) The conch.
<div n="P">4. n. ({#-ब्जम्#}) <sup>1</sup> A lotus; (according to the {%Sārasvatā-%}
{%bhidhāna%} the white esculent variety; {#‘पङ्कजकैरवे’#}). <sup>2</sup> A
large number, a million of millions; {%Līlavatī:%} {#एकदशशत-#}
{#सहस्रायुतलक्षप्रयुतकोटयः क्रमशः । अर्बुदमब्जं खर्वनिखर्वम-#}
{#हापद्मशङ्कवस्तस्मात् । जलधिश्चान्त्यं मध्यं परार्धमिति दशगु-#}
{#णोत्तराः संज्ञाः । संख्यायाः स्थानानां व्यवहारार्थं कृताः पूर्वैः#}.
<sup>3</sup> A sort of collyrium; see {#रसाञ्जन#}.

4. 3. and 5. are given in {%Sureśvara'%}s {%Śabdapratīpa,%} where
the word is thus defined: {#अब्जं रसाञ्जने पद्मे स्त्रियां शुक्तौ#}
