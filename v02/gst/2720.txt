<L>2720<pc>081-a<k1>अनियुक्त<k2>अनियुक्त
{#अनियुक्त#}¦ Tatpur. 1. m. f. n. ({#-क्तः-क्ता-क्तम्#}) Not authorised,
not called, not appointed &c. See {#नियुक्त#}.
<div n="P">2. m. ({#-क्तः#}) (In Law.) A Brāhmaṇa who attends a court
without having been appointed a member of it, whose vote
is therefore not binding, and who is at liberty to attend
the court meetings and to debate in them, or not. (See,
on the contrary, {#सभासद्#}.)
