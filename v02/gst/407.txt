{#अग्निभू#}¦ Tatpur. I. m. ({#-भूः#}) <sup>1</sup> A name of Skanda, the Hindu
deity of war. See {#कार्त्तिकेय, स्कन्द, अग्निजन्मन्#}. (Śiva hav-
ing cast his seed into flame, for the purpose of generating
a warrior, able to subdue Tāraka, a demon, Skanda was
produced.) <sup>2</sup> (In arithmetic sometimes used to denote) the
numeral six (Skanda having six faces).
<div n="P">II. n. ({#-भु#}) Water (rain being generated, according to
the Hindu belief from the clouds which are produced by
the smoke of fire). E. {#अग्नि#} and {#भू#} (generating from).
