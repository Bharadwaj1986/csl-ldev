{#अनङ्गक्रीडा#}¦ Tatpur. f. ({#-डा#}) A metre regulated by quantity;
it is a species of the {#द्विच्छन्दिक#} (q. v.) and consists of a
couplet with sixteen long syllables in the first and thirty-
[Page066-a+ 56]
two short syllables in the second verse. Also called {#सौ-#}
{#म्या#}. E. {#अनङ्ग#} and {#क्रीडा#}.
