{#अनपजय्य#}¦ Bahuvr. m. f. n. ({#-य्यः-य्या-य्यम्#}) (ved.) What has
no possibility of being made un-conquered or un-victorious
[Page070-a+ 56]
(as a victory). {#अनपजय्यम्#} used also adverbially: so as
not to become un-victorious (e. g. to conquer so as not to
have a barren victory, as not to have the ennemy rising
again). E. {#अ#} priv. and {#अपजय्य#}.
