{#अभिपुष्प#}¦ Tatpur. n. ({#-ष्पम्#}) An excellent flower or an excess
of flowers. (As a Bahuvr. m. f. n. the word may mean:
covered with flowers--comp. {#अभिपद्म#}--, but in the verse
of the {%Rāmāy.:%} {#धृतिप्रबालः प्रसवाभिपुष्पस्तपःफलः शौर्यनि-#}

it is a Tatpur.) E. {#अभि#} and {#पुष्प#}.
