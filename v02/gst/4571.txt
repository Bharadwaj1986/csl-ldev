{#अपतानक#}¦ Tatpur. m. ({#-कः#}) (In Medicine.) One of the diseases
of the vital air (see {#वातव्याधि#}), viz. a spasmodic contraction
of the body; described thus: ‘if the vital air, much af-
fected with phlegm, stops in the tubular vessels, it causes
the patient to fall down at intervals’. E. {#तन्,#} in the caus.,
with {#अप,#} kṛt aff. {#ण्वुल्#}.
