{#अपञ्चीकृत#}¦ Tatpur. n. pl. ({#-तानि#}) (In the Vedānta philosophy.)
The five subtle elements ({#सूक्ष्मभूत#} or {#सूक्ष्माङ्ग#}), æther, air,
fire, water and earth (in which originate but) which are
‘not yet become the five’ gross elements or {#स्थूलभूत#} q. v.
Also called {#तन्मात्र#} q. v. E. {#अ#} neg. and {#पञ्चीकृत#}.
