{#पाश्चाभीलापलपश्च ये । शरीरं सर्वे प्राविशन्नायुजः प्रयुजो#}
{#युजः;#} where {#अभीलापलपः#} is either a Dwandwa: discoursing
and speech in general; or perhaps an irregular intensitive
formation with the reduplication of the whole radical in-
stead of its first syllable; the masc. {#ये#} being accounted for
by the preceding masculines. Comp. {#अभीमोद#}. E. {#लप्#}
with {#अभि,#} kṛt aff. {#घञ्,#} and the second syllable protracted.
