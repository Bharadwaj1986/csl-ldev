{#अप्रकरण#}¦ Tatpur. n. ({#-णम्#}) The not being the principal or
<div n="lb">relevant matter, incidental or irrelevant matter (of a book,
<div n="lb">a discussion &c.); e. g. {%Śaṅkara%} on a passage of the {%Bṛhadār.:%}
<div n="lb">{#ज्ञानकर्मणी एव हि मेधातपःशब्दवाच्ये । तयोः प्रकृतत्वान्नेतरे#}
<div n="lb">{#मेधातपसी । अप्रकरणात्;#} applied also to those passages in
<div n="lb">the Brāhmaṇas which are considered as not connected with,
<div n="lb">or irrelevant to, the principal topics of an injunction with
<div n="lb">which they may happen to be found together in the same
<div n="lb">chapter or book, and which therefore do not share in the
<div n="lb">rules that apply to the latter, but become the subject of
<div n="lb">special injunctions; comp. {#अनारभ्याधीत#} and the following;
<div n="lb">e. g. in the {%Jaimini Sūtra:%} {#अप्रकरणे तु तद्धर्मतो विशेषात्#}.
<div n="lb">E. {#अ#} neg. and {#प्रकरण#}.
