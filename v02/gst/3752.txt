<L>3752<pc>118-a<k1>अन्तर्<k2>अन्तर्
{#अन्तर्#}¦ ind. (see {#निपात, गति, उपसर्ग#}). A particle implying
<sup>1</sup> within, in the middle, <sup>2</sup> between, <sup>3</sup> under, in its literal and
metaphorical acceptations. It is used either adverbially or as
a separable preposition or as a prefix to verbs and occurs
also, but seldom, in the sense of a noun. With nouns, if
preceding them it may form Tatpur., Bahuvr. and Avyayībh.,
if following them indecl. Tatpur. compounds. It means:
<div n="P">I. (if used adverbially) <sup>1</sup> In the middle, within; mostly
followed or preceded by a noun in the locat.; e. g. {#अप्स्वन्त-#}
{#रमृतम्#}. <sup>2</sup> In the interior (said of a kingdom &c.); e. g.
{#लघुवृत्तितया भिदां गतं वहिरन्तश्च#}--comm. {#वहिर्मित्रादि-#}
{#जनपदेषु । अन्तरमात्यादिषु--नृपस्य मण्डलम् । अभिभूय#}
{#हरत्यनन्तरः#} &c.). <sup>3</sup> In the mind, in the heart, e. g. {#अभ्यु-#}
{#दयानन्तरसावधित मुदा नैषधप्रियानन्तरसा#}. <sup>4</sup> By way of
[Page118-b+ 56]
enclosing or seizing, e. g. {#अन्तर्हत्वा मूषिकां श्येनो गतः#}
(comm. = {#परिगृह्य#}); comp. III. 1. [This meaning is meant
by the native Koshas when they render {#अन्तर्#} by {#स्वी-#}
{#कारे,#} for the Kaśikā interprets Pāṇini I. 4. 65. {#‘परिग्रहः#}
{#स्वीकरणम्’;#} Mathureśa has probably misunderstood the
bearing of {#स्वीकरण#} when substituting for the latter word
the interpretation {#अङ्गीकृतौ#}.]
<div n="P">II. (as a separable preposition) <sup>1</sup> Within, <sup>2</sup> Between;
followed or preceded <sup>a.</sup> by the genitive, e. g. {#तस्मान्नाक्ष्या-#}
{#दित्ययोरन्तः परमेश्वरः;#} or {#हिरण्मय्योः कुश्योरन्तरवहित आस;#}
<sup>b.</sup> (ved.) by the accus., e. g. {#रोदसी अन्तरुर्वी#}.
<div n="P">III. (as a prefix to verbs) <sup>1</sup> In the middle, between, e. g.
{#अन्तर्हत्य#} ‘having struck in the middle’; compare on the
contrary {#अन्तर्हत्वा#} 1. 3. <sup>2</sup> Under (implying disappearance);
e. g. {#धा#} with {#अन्तर्#} to hide, to make invisible, comp. also
{#अन्तर्धि, अन्तर्धा#} &c. [The intimate relation between {#अन्तर्#}
and the radical or verb in the latter cases is indicated not
only by the influence this particle exercises on the meaning
of the radical and its derivatives, but also by the effect it
has on the formation of the gerund (comp. {#अन्तर्हत्वा#} I. 4.
and {#अन्तर्हत्य#} III. 1.) and by the change it may produce in
the dental {#न्#} of the radical and its derivation affixes, comp.
e. g. {#अन्तरयण, अन्तर्हणन#} &c.]
<div n="P">IV. (as an indeclinable noun) <sup>1</sup> The interior, any thing
not belonging to the exterior world, e. g. {#अन्तर्वहिरिति#}
{#कार्यद्रव्यस्य कारणान्तरवचनादकार्ये तदभावः;#} or {#भुवोऽन्त-#}
{#रूर्ध्वमधश्च#}. <sup>2</sup> The heart, e. g. {#राक्षम भक्ष मां…अन्तरदाम्#}
{#(= हृदयं दत्तवती)#}. Compare also {#अन्तर्वत्#} and {#अन्तरा#}.
<div n="P">V. (in composition with nouns) In the middle of, in the in-
terior of, in the heart of; <sup>1</sup> before the noun: <sup>a.</sup> in Tatpur., e.
g. {#अन्तर्ज, अन्तर्दुष्ट;#} in several comp. of this class, it may
seem as if the latter part depended on {#अन्तर्#} in the genitive,
e. g. {#अन्तःशरीर#} the interior of the body, {#अन्तर्हृदय#} the
interior of the heart, but it is more correct to consider
these interpretations as arising from such as ‘the body
in its interior &c.’; comp. several comp. beginning with
{#अग्र,#} e. g. {#अग्रनख, अग्रहस्त#} &c.; the {#न्#} is changed to
{#ण#} in the Tatpur. {#अन्तर्वण#} and {#अन्तरयण#} qq. vv.; see also
{#अन्तरीप#}. <sup>b.</sup> in Bahuvr., e. g. {#अन्तःस्वेद, अन्तर्वाष्प;#} some-
times meaning ‘from the interior’, e. g. {#अन्तःसुख, अन्तःप्रज्ञ#}.
<sup>c.</sup> in Avyayībh., e. g. {#अन्तर्वेश्मम्, अन्तर्गिरि#}. <sup>2</sup> After the
noun, in indecl. Tatpur., e. g. {#ते नालिकेरान्तरपः पिबन्तः;#} or
{#जलान्तश्चन्द्रचपलम्;#} or {#उदरान्तः प्रविश्य;#} in such compounds
some comm. consider the former part as representing the
sense of a locative, not of a genitive ({#‘अव्ययत्वात्षष्ठीसमा-#}
{#सप्रतिषेधः’#}), others not; e. g. {#संप्रवेष्टुमिव योषित ईषुः श्लि-#}
{#ष्यतां हृदयमिष्टतमानाम् । आत्मनः सततमेव तदन्तर्वर्तिनो न#}
{#खलु नूनमजानन्#} (comm. {#तेषामिष्टतमानामन्तर्हृदये#}).
<div n="P">VI. See {#अन्तरा#}. E. {#अम्,#} uṇ. aff. {#अरन्#} and āgama {#तुट्;#}
but see the Preface.
