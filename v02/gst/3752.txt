{#अन्तर्#}¦ ind. (see {#निपात, गति, उपसर्ग#}). A particle implying
<div n="lb"><sup>1</sup> within, in the middle, <sup>2</sup> between, <sup>3</sup> under, in its literal and
<div n="lb">metaphorical acceptations. It is used either adverbially or as
<div n="lb">a separable preposition or as a prefix to verbs and occurs
<div n="lb">also, but seldom, in the sense of a noun. With nouns, if
<div n="lb">preceding them it may form Tatpur., Bahuvr. and Avyayībh.,
<div n="lb">if following them indecl. Tatpur. compounds. It means:
<div n="P">I. (if used adverbially) <sup>1</sup> In the middle, within; mostly
<div n="lb">followed or preceded by a noun in the locat.; e. g. {#अप्स्वन्त-#}
<div n="lb">{#रमृतम्#}. <sup>2</sup> In the interior (said of a kingdom &c.); e. g.
<div n="lb">{#लघुवृत्तितया भिदां गतं वहिरन्तश्च#}--comm. {#वहिर्मित्रादि-#}
<div n="lb">{#जनपदेषु । अन्तरमात्यादिषु--नृपस्य मण्डलम् । अभिभूय#}
<div n="lb">{#हरत्यनन्तरः#} &c.). <sup>3</sup> In the mind, in the heart, e. g. {#अभ्यु-#}
<div n="lb">{#दयानन्तरसावधित मुदा नैषधप्रियानन्तरसा#}. <sup>4</sup> By way of
[Page118-b+ 56]
<div n="lb">enclosing or seizing, e. g. {#अन्तर्हत्वा मूषिकां श्येनो गतः#}
<div n="lb">(comm. = {#परिगृह्य#}); comp. III. 1. [This meaning is meant
<div n="lb">by the native Koshas when they render {#अन्तर्#} by {#स्वी-#}
<div n="lb">{#कारे,#} for the Kaśikā interprets Pāṇini I. 4. 65. {#‘परिग्रहः#}
<div n="lb">{#स्वीकरणम्’;#} Mathureśa has probably misunderstood the
<div n="lb">bearing of {#स्वीकरण#} when substituting for the latter word
<div n="lb">the interpretation {#अङ्गीकृतौ#}.]
<div n="P">II. (as a separable preposition) <sup>1</sup> Within, <sup>2</sup> Between;
<div n="lb">followed or preceded <sup>a.</sup> by the genitive, e. g. {#तस्मान्नाक्ष्या-#}
<div n="lb">{#दित्ययोरन्तः परमेश्वरः;#} or {#हिरण्मय्योः कुश्योरन्तरवहित आस;#}
<div n="lb"><sup>b.</sup> (ved.) by the accus., e. g. {#रोदसी अन्तरुर्वी#}.
<div n="P">III. (as a prefix to verbs) <sup>1</sup> In the middle, between, e. g.
<div n="lb">{#अन्तर्हत्य#} ‘having struck in the middle’; compare on the
<div n="lb">contrary {#अन्तर्हत्वा#} 1. 3. <sup>2</sup> Under (implying disappearance);
<div n="lb">e. g. {#धा#} with {#अन्तर्#} to hide, to make invisible, comp. also
<div n="lb">{#अन्तर्धि, अन्तर्धा#} &c. [The intimate relation between {#अन्तर्#}
<div n="lb">and the radical or verb in the latter cases is indicated not
<div n="lb">only by the influence this particle exercises on the meaning
<div n="lb">of the radical and its derivatives, but also by the effect it
<div n="lb">has on the formation of the gerund (comp. {#अन्तर्हत्वा#} I. 4.
<div n="lb">and {#अन्तर्हत्य#} III. 1.) and by the change it may produce in
<div n="lb">the dental {#न्#} of the radical and its derivation affixes, comp.
<div n="lb">e. g. {#अन्तरयण, अन्तर्हणन#} &c.]
<div n="P">IV. (as an indeclinable noun) <sup>1</sup> The interior, any thing
<div n="lb">not belonging to the exterior world, e. g. {#अन्तर्वहिरिति#}
<div n="lb">{#कार्यद्रव्यस्य कारणान्तरवचनादकार्ये तदभावः;#} or {#भुवोऽन्त-#}
<div n="lb">{#रूर्ध्वमधश्च#}. <sup>2</sup> The heart, e. g. {#राक्षम भक्ष मां…अन्तरदाम्#}
<div n="lb">{#(= हृदयं दत्तवती)#}. Compare also {#अन्तर्वत्#} and {#अन्तरा#}.
<div n="P">V. (in composition with nouns) In the middle of, in the in-
<div n="lb">terior of, in the heart of; <sup>1</sup> before the noun: <sup>a.</sup> in Tatpur., e.
<div n="lb">g. {#अन्तर्ज, अन्तर्दुष्ट;#} in several comp. of this class, it may
<div n="lb">seem as if the latter part depended on {#अन्तर्#} in the genitive,
<div n="lb">e. g. {#अन्तःशरीर#} the interior of the body, {#अन्तर्हृदय#} the
<div n="lb">interior of the heart, but it is more correct to consider
<div n="lb">these interpretations as arising from such as ‘the body
<div n="lb">in its interior &c.’; comp. several comp. beginning with
<div n="lb">{#अग्र,#} e. g. {#अग्रनख, अग्रहस्त#} &c.; the {#न्#} is changed to
<div n="lb">{#ण#} in the Tatpur. {#अन्तर्वण#} and {#अन्तरयण#} qq. vv.; see also
<div n="lb">{#अन्तरीप#}. <sup>b.</sup> in Bahuvr., e. g. {#अन्तःस्वेद, अन्तर्वाष्प;#} some-
<div n="lb">times meaning ‘from the interior’, e. g. {#अन्तःसुख, अन्तःप्रज्ञ#}.
<div n="lb"><sup>c.</sup> in Avyayībh., e. g. {#अन्तर्वेश्मम्, अन्तर्गिरि#}. <sup>2</sup> After the
<div n="lb">noun, in indecl. Tatpur., e. g. {#ते नालिकेरान्तरपः पिबन्तः;#} or
<div n="lb">{#जलान्तश्चन्द्रचपलम्;#} or {#उदरान्तः प्रविश्य;#} in such compounds
<div n="lb">some comm. consider the former part as representing the
<div n="lb">sense of a locative, not of a genitive ({#‘अव्ययत्वात्षष्ठीसमा-#}
<div n="lb">{#सप्रतिषेधः’#}), others not; e. g. {#संप्रवेष्टुमिव योषित ईषुः श्लि-#}
<div n="lb">{#ष्यतां हृदयमिष्टतमानाम् । आत्मनः सततमेव तदन्तर्वर्तिनो न#}
<div n="lb">{#खलु नूनमजानन्#} (comm. {#तेषामिष्टतमानामन्तर्हृदये#}).
<div n="P">VI. See {#अन्तरा#}. E. {#अम्,#} uṇ. aff. {#अरन्#} and āgama {#तुट्;#}
<div n="lb">but see the Preface.
