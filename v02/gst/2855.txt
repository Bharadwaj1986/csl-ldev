{#अनुकूलनायक#}¦ Karmadh. m. ({#-कः#}) (In Rhetoric.) One of the
forty-eight characters of a hero or principal personage in
a poetical composition; he is described as a lover attached
only to one mistress and as one of the four varieties (see
also {#दक्षिण, धृष्ट#} and {#शठ#}) of either of the four principal
divisions of the {#नायक#} (q. v.), viz. of the {#धीरोदात्त, धी-#}
{#रोद्धत, धीरललित#} and {#धीरप्रशान्त#} (qq. vv.). The {#अनुकूल-#}
{#नायक#} may again be {#उत्तम, मध्यम#} or {#अधम#} (qq. vv.). E.
{#अनुकूल#} and {#नायक#}.
