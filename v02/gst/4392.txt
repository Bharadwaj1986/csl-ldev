<L>4392<pc>150-b<k1>अन्वक्षरसन्धि<k2>अन्वक्षरसन्धि
{#अन्वक्षरसन्धि#}¦ Tatpur. m. ({#-न्धिः#}) (In vaidik Grammar.) A
technical name for certain Sandhi or meeting of letters at
the end and beginning of words; viz. <sup>a.</sup> when a final vowel
or {#एषः, स्यः#} or {#सः#} are followed by a consonant (the Vi-
sarga of these three words then being dropped); see {#अनु-#}
{#लोम;#} <sup>b.</sup> when a final consonant is followed by an initial
vowel; see {#प्रतिलोम;#} <sup>c.</sup> when a final Visarga is followed
by a consonant called {#ऊष्मन्#} q. v. which itself is followed
by a surd or hard consonant (the preceding Visarga then
being dropped, e. g. {#समुद्रा स्थः,#} instead of {#समुद्राः स्थः#});
see {#वक्त्र;#} <sup>d.</sup> when the particle {#ईम्#} is followed by the words
[Page151-a+ 56]
{#गर्भम्, गावः, वत्सम्, मृजन्ति, पृच्यते, सखायः, विव्याच,#}
{#पुनर्, रिणन्ति#} and {#रथम्#} (the final {#म्#} of {#ईम्#} then being
dropped). E. {#अनु-अक्षर#} and {#सन्धि#}.
