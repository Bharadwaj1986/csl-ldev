{#अप्सुवाह्#}¦ Tatpur. m. ({#-ट्#}) (ved.) Driving in the intermediate
region, in the region between heaven and earth, an epithet
of the horses of Indra; (this would seem the natural inter-
pretation of this word in {%Sāmav.%} 1. 341. = I. 4. 1. 5. 10.; {%Sā-%}
{%yaṇa%} renders it however ‘driving on account of water’,
[Page225-b+ 56]
i. e. ‘precipitating the waters’: {#अप्सु निमित्तभूतासु ये वहन्ति#}
{#तेऽप्सुवाहः । उदकस्य पातयितारः । तान्#} &c.; comp. the
explanation of {#अप्सुजित्#}). E. {#अप्सु#} (loc. plur. of {#अप्#}) and
{#वाह्#} ({#वह्,#} kṛt aff. {#ण्वि#}).
