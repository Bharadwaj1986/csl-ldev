II. {#अप#}¦ m. ({#-पः#}) A sea, an ocean. E. {#अप्#} taddh. aff. {#अच्#}.
[Word and meaning are very doubtful; they are given on
the authority of Rāyamukuṭa, who interprets the word {#अ-#}
{#पगा#} in the Amarakosha: {#आपः सन्त्यत्रेति अर्शआद्यचि अपः#}
{#समुद्रः तं गच्छतीति;#} comp. the E. of {#अपगा#}. Comp. also {#अपवत्#}.
--For beginners it is useful to observe that in compounds like
{#शुष्काप, विमलाप#} &c., the latter part is not {#अप,#} but that such
compounds are to be analyzed into {#शुष्क#} and {#अप्, विमल#}
and {#अप्#} &c., the whole word receiving the samās. aff. {#अ#}.]
