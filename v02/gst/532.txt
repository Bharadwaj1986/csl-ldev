{#अग्रहायण#}¦ Tatpur. m. ({#-णः#}) A name given to the solar month
Mārgaśiras or Mārgaśirṣa (q. v.) when the latter was made
to commence the year; the eighth month of the lunar year
of the Hindus, when the moon is full near the head of
Orion, or about November-December. Also written {#आग्र-#}
{#हायण#}. (The fem. {#अग्रहायणी#} is a false reading for {#आग्र-#}
{#हायणी#} q. v.) E. {#अग्र#} and {#हायण#}.
