{#अभिख्ययादव#}¦ Karmadh. m. ({#-वः#}) The {%celebrated%} Yādava,
i. e. Kṛṣṇa; {%Hemachandra%} (in the definition of {#मत्स्य#}):
{#मत्स्यो मीनान्तरे मीने विराटेऽभिख्ययादवे#} (thus the printed
ed. of {%Hemach. nān.%} and the Mss. of the E. I. H. and of
the R. A. S.; in several Mss. of the {%Viśvaprak.,%} belonging
to the E. I. H. and the R. S., this verse runs: {#°°°विरा-#}
{#टाभिख्ययादवे,#} but as this reading is clearly wrong and as
a correction {#विराटाभिख्ये यादवे#} would be at variance with
the metre, it must be assumed that it ought to be the same
as in the text of {%Hemachandra%}). E. {#अभिख्य#} and {#यादव#}.
