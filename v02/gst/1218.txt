{#अतिनिचृत्#}¦ Tatpur. f. ({#-चृत्#}) The name of a vaidik metre
regulated by the number of syllables; it consists of three
Pādas of seven, six, and seven syllables in each; also
read {#अतिनिवृत्#}. E. {#अति#} (sc. {#क्रान्ता#}) and {#निचृत्#} (or {#निवृत्#}),
in the sense of the accusative.
