<div n="lb">{#-न्वञ्चि; नूचः-नूचीः-न्वञ्चि#} &c.) <sup>1</sup> Following; {#अनूचि#} (loc. sing.),

<div n="lb"><sup>2</sup> (ved.) Going along or lengthwise; as opposed to {#उदच्#} and
<div n="lb">{#तिर्यच्#} qq. vv.--The neuter {#अन्वक्#} is used as adv. and as-

<div n="lb">uses of the form {#अन्वचि#} which has the value there of a
<div n="lb">locat. of {#अन्वच्;#} Patanjali blames him for this liberty: {#अ-#}
<div n="lb">{#युक्तोऽयं निर्देशः । अनूचीति भवितव्यम् । सौत्रोऽयं निर्देशः ।#}.
<div n="lb">But as Pāṇini expresses himself in a similar manner when
<div n="lb">speaking of {#तिर्यच्#} III. 4. 60. {#‘तिर्यच्यपवर्गे’#}--when he in-
<div n="lb">curs a like remark from Patanjali--, it is perhaps more
<div n="lb">just to say that he did not intend to speak in these rules

<div n="lb">{#अनूचि#} and {#तिरश्चि,#} as suggested by Patanjali, would not
