{#अनुष्ठानस्मारक#}¦ Tatpur. m. f. n. ({#-रकः-रिका-रकम्#}) Remind-
ing of religious ceremonies; said of certain vaidik passages
which properly do not answer the general definition given
of a Mantra, but which nevertheless are called so by the
liturgical authors, because they remind of duties taught or
explained in a more explicit manner by other Mantras. E.
{#अनुष्ठान#} and {#स्मारक#}.
