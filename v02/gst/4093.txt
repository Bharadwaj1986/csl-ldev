{#अन्नकूटयात्रा#}¦ Tatpur. f. ({#-त्रा#}) The festival of the mountain
of boiled rice; a festival of the Hindus in the month of
November when they make a pile of boiled rice as a type
of the hill Govardhana in Vraja, near Mathurā, upheld by
Kṛṣṇa for the shelter of the milkmaids. It is called also
Govardhanapūjā and observed especially by cowherds. E.
{#अन्नकूट#} and {#यात्रा#}.
