{#अबन्धक#}¦ I. Tatpur. 1. m. f. n. ({#-न्धकः-न्धिका-न्धकम्#}) Not
binding, not confining.
<div n="P">2. m. ({#-कः#}) A proper name, the same as {#अबद्धक#} (q. v.)
of which it is a various reading; the descendants are called
{#अबन्धकाः#} or {#आबन्धकयः#} ({%Gaṇaratnam.:%} {#बन्धं करोतीति बन्ध-#}
{#को न बन्धकोऽबन्धक;#} but see also the Bahuvr.). E. {#अ#} neg.
and {#बन्धक#}.
<div n="P">II. Bahuvr. 1. m. f. n. ({#-न्धकः-न्धिका-न्धकम्#}) Without a
tie &c., see the meanings of {#बन्ध#}.
<div n="P">2. m. ({#-कः#}) <sup>1</sup> (In Law.) A case (scil. of a loan) when
[Page226-b+ 56]
no pledge has been given; (in such cases the monthly in-
terest is in the direct order of the classes, two, three, four
or five in the hundred; according to Yājnavalkya); e. g.
{%Mitākṣara:%} {#सर्वे ब्राह्मणादयोऽधमर्णा अबन्धके सबन्धके वा#}
{#स्वकृतां साभ्युपगतां वृद्धिं सर्वासु जातिषु दद्युः#}. (<sup>2</sup> The proper
name I. 2. is explained by the {%Gaṇaratnam.%} also as a Ba-
huvr.: {#‘न विद्यते बन्धोऽस्येत्यबन्धकः’#}.) E. {#अ#} priv. and {#बन्ध,#}
in the first meaning with the ellipsis {#प्रयोग#}.
