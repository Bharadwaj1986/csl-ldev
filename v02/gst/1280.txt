{#अतिमुक्त#}¦ Tatpur. I. m. f. n. ({#-क्तः-क्ता-क्तम्#}) <sup>1</sup> Entirely liber-
ated or freed. <sup>2</sup> Free from passion or worldly desire. <sup>3</sup> Seed-
less, barren. E. {#अति#} and {#मुक्त#}.
<div n="P">II. m. ({#-क्तः#}) A large creeper bearing white fragrant flow-
ers (Gærtnera racemosa), ‘used against colds, bilious heat,
feverish delirium, hiccough, sickness &c.’ See also {#पुण्ड्रक,#}
{#माधवीलता#}. E. {#अति#} (sc. {#क्रान्त#}) and {#मुक्ता#} (in the sense of
the accusative) ‘surpassing pearls sc. in whiteness’.
<div n="P">III. m. ({#-क्तः#}) A tree (Dalbergia oujeiniensis). E. {#अति#}
and {#मुक्त#} ‘from its spreading very widely’.
