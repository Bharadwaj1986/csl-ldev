{#अनाथपिण्डद#}¦ Tatpur. m. ({#-दः#}) The name of a celebrated
merchant, the owner of the garden Jetavana near Śrāvasti,
where the Buddha Sākyamuni used to explain his doctrine
to his disciples. He was also called {#अनाथपिण्डिक#}. E.
{#अनाथ#} and {#पिण्ड-द#} ‘giving food to the poor’.
