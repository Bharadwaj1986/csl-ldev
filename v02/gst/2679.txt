{#अनित्यसमास#}¦ Karmadh. m. ({#-सः#}) (In grammar.) A compound
which may be dissolved into its constituent parts without under-
going a material change of sense; e. g. {#राजपुरुषः,#} which may
be dissolved into {#राज्ञः पुरुषः#}. The converse of {#नित्यसमास#} q. v.
