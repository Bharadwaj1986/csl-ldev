{#अनुभूति#}¦ Tatpur. m. ({#-तिः#}) (In the Vaiśeṣika phil.) Appre-
hension, one of the two essential properties of intellect
({#बुद्धि#}); it is four-fold viz. knowledge arising from the senses
(see {#प्रत्यक्ष#}), inference (see {#अनुमिति#}), comparison (see
{#उपमिति#}) and verbal knowledge ({#शब्दज#}). Comp. {#अनुभव#}.
E. {#भू#} with {#अनु,#} kṛt aff. {#क्तिन्#}.
