1. 171. (= I. 2. 2. 3. 7.), 1. 200. (= 1. 3. 1. 1. 7.) and 1. 274. (= I.

{#मुम्;#} (or rather the accus. of {#अभय#} and {#कर,#} with the
udātta on the last syllable).
