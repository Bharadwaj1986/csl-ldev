<div n="P">II. m. ({#-वः#}) A poisonous little animal.
<div n="P">III. n. ({#-वम्#}) A sacrificial vessel consecrated to Mitra
and Varuna. E. doubtful. Perhaps this word is derived
from {#अजका,#} taddh. aff. {#व,#} III. being explained as a vessel
having a piece of wood in the shape of the throat nipples
of a goat and II. as an animal poisonous or deadly as the
disease {#अजका#}.
