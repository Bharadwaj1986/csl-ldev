{#अनुवाद्यत्व#}¦ n. ({#-त्वम्#}) The quality of being the subject or the
basis of an {#अनुवाद#} (q. v.), the being the cause of expla-
natory injunctions, accessory rites &c. (compare {#अनुवाद#}),
one of the three properties of the {#फल#} or ultimate object
of a sacrificial act which is instrumental in fulfilling the
wishes of the agent. (For the two other properties of the
{#फल#} see {#उद्देश्यत्व#} and {#मुख्यत्व#} or {#प्रधानत्व,#} and for those of
the sacrificial act {#उपादेयत्व#} (the correlate of {#अनुवाद्यत्व#}),
{#विधेयत्व#} (the correlate of {#उद्देश्यत्व#}) and {#गुणत्व#} (the correlate
of {#मुख्यत्व#}). E. {#अनुवाद्य,#} taddh. aff. {#त्व#}.
