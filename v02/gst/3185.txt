{#अनुभोग#}¦ Tatpur.(?) n. ({#-गम्#}) (A modern law term.) Enjoy-
ment, usufruct, a grant of hereditary land in Malabar,
either rent-free or at pepper-corn-rent, in reward of service;
also a present of betel or a cocoa nut, from the proprietor
at the time of executing a deed of transfer of a garden or
plantation to the person to whom the transfer is made, as
symbolical of the character of the land made over. E. {#अनु#}
and {#भोग#}.
