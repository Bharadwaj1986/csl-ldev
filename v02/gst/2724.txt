{#अनिरुक्त#}¦ Tatpur. m. f. n. ({#-क्तः-क्ता-क्तम्#}) <sup>1</sup> Not spoken out,
not declared. <sup>2</sup> Not explained, not defined (e. g. the di-
vinity, if spoken of in general terms, or Brahma (n.) that
is without qualities, or an invocation, not containing the
special name of the god addressed, one addressed to the
gods in general &c.). <sup>3</sup> Obscure, incomprehensible, literally
and metaphorically; e. g. Prajāpati, Brahman (n.) or a
hymn uttered inaudibly, so as not being comprehensible to
the by-standers. E. {#अ#} neg. and {#निरुक्त#}.
