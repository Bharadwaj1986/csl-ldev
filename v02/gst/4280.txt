{#अन्यभृत्#}¦ Tatpur. f. ({#-त्#}) A crow (lit. nourishing another, viz.
the Kokila, the crow being supposed to hatch the eggs of
the cuckoo. Comp. {#अन्यपुष्ट, अन्यभृत, अन्यवाप#}. The gender
of this word is not defined in Hemachandra where it occurs;
but it can scarcely be the masc.). E. {#अन्य#} and {#भृत्#}.
