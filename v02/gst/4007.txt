{#अन्त्यानुप्रास#}¦ Tatpur. m. ({#-सः#}) (In Rhetoric.) Alliteration of
the final single or combined consonant together with its
vowel, or as the case be, with its Anusvāra and Visarga;
rhyme; one of the five varieties of {#अनुप्रास#} q. v. It may
occur either at the end of a Pāda, or at the end of words
in the middle of a Pāda. E. {#अन्त्य#} and {#अनुप्रास#}.
