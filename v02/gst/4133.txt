<div n="lb">kind of spirituous liquor, viz. {#सुरा#}. [The latter meaning
<div n="lb">requires an explanation: Manu 11. 93 says: {#सुरा वै मल-#}
<div n="lb">{#मन्नानां#} &c. and forbids the drinking of the spirituous liquor

<div n="lb">quoted by Vijnāneśvara in the Mitākṣara names twelve
<div n="lb">sorts of intoxicating drinks, stating that {#सुरा#} is the very
<div n="lb">worst description amongst them: {#पानसं द्राक्षं माधूकं खार्जूरं#}
<div n="lb">{#तालमैक्षवम् । मधूत्थं सैरमारिष्टमैरेयं नारिकेलजम् । समा-#}
<div n="lb">{#नानि विजानीयान्मद्यान्येकादशैव तु । द्वादशं तु सुरामद्यं सर्वे-#}
<div n="lb">{#षामधमं स्मृतम्;#} and Vijnāneśvara in commenting upon the
