{#अधिक#}¦ I. m. f. n. ({#-कः-का-कम्#}) <sup>1</sup> Exceeding, in addition to
<div n="lb">(the reverse of {#न्यून;#} between both {#साधारण#}). <sup>2</sup> More, more
<div n="lb">than, superior (in place, power, rank, quality or quantity).
<div n="lb"><sup>3</sup> Very much, excessive, excellent. <sup>4</sup> Less, smaller, inferior (cf.
<div n="lb">{#अधि#} 2. 1. c.). {#अधिकम्#} used as adverb. (If {#अधिक#} has the first of
<div n="lb">these meanings, that by which the excess is caused, stands in
<div n="lb">the instrumental or (but seldom) in the locative or forms the
<div n="lb">first part of a Tatpur. compound of which {#अधिक#} is the latter;
<div n="lb">if it has the second meaning, the object compared with, stands
<div n="lb">in the instrumental, the ablative or in the genitive; if it has
<div n="lb">the last meaning, the object compared with stands in the abla-
<div n="lb">tive. Cf. {#अध्यारूढ#}. With numerals {#अधिक#} may form Bahuvr.
<div n="lb">compounds, usually in the plural, if it is the former part of
<div n="lb">the compound; for instance {#अधिकचत्वारिंशाः#} (sc. {#संख्याः#}) i. e.
<div n="lb">more than forty. It is considered as dropped in Dwandwa
<div n="lb">compounds of numerals f. i. in {#पञ्चविंशति,#} equal to {#पञ्चाधि-#}
<div n="lb">{#कविंशति#}.) <sup>5</sup> Embolismic, intercalary (only in composition
<div n="lb">with and prefixed to the name or numeral of a luni-solar
<div n="lb">year, to the name of a month and to the name of a day;
[Page054-a+ 56]
<div n="lb">f. i. {#अधिकाश्विन#}. See also {#अधिकतिथि, अधिकदिन, अधि-#}
<div n="lb">{#कमास, अधिकसंवत्सर,#} and compare {#क्षय#}). <sup>5</sup> Subsequent,
<div n="lb">following (as a day, a chapter or a passage in a book &c.;
<div n="lb">used very much in the same manner as {#ऊर्ध्व#}).
<div n="P">II. n. ({#-कम्#}) <sup>1</sup> The surplus, that which is exceeding.
<div n="lb"><sup>2</sup> (In Nyāya.) Superfluity in reasons or instances, giving
<div n="lb">more reasons or instances than are required or relevant in
<div n="lb">a syllogism. <sup>3</sup> (In rhetoric.) Hyperbole (of which two
<div n="lb">kinds are enumerated: hyperbole concerning the recipient
<div n="lb">(substance, subject &c.) and hyperbole concerning that
<div n="lb">which is to be received (accidents, predicate &c.). E. {#अधि,#}
<div n="lb">taddh. aff. {#कन्#}.
