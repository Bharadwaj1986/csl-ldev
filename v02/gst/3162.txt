{#अनुबन्ध्य#}¦ I. Tatpur. m. f. ({#-न्ध्यः-न्ध्या#}) One of the three prin-
<div n="lb">cipal paśus or sacrificial animals in the Jyotiṣṭoma sacrifice.
<div n="lb">(See also {#अग्नीषोमीयपशु#} and {#सवनीयपशु#}.) It is a cow, and
<div n="lb">the proceedings with it take place after the Avabhṛtha on
<div n="lb">the fifth day of the Agniṣṭoma (q. v.). But as the sacrifice
<div n="lb">of a cow is forbidden in the present or Kali-yuga, a sub-
<div n="lb">stitute for it is an offering of curds of two-milk-whey.--
<div n="lb">Also written {#अनूबन्ध्य#}. E. {#बन्ध्#} with {#अनु,#} kṛtya aff. {#यत्;#}
<div n="lb">with the ellipsis of {#पशु#} for the masc. and of {#गो#} for the
<div n="lb">feminine.
<div n="P">II. m. f. n. ({#-न्ध्यः-न्ध्या-न्ध्यम्#}) Principal, primary, what
<div n="lb">may receive an adjunct, as a root, a disease &c. E. {#अनुबन्ध,#}
<div n="lb">taddh. aff. {#यत्#}.
