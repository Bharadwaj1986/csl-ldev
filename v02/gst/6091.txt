{#अभिनिष्क्रमण#}¦ Tatpur. n. ({#-णम्#}) Going out or forth, leaving,
quitting; e. g. {%Lalitav.:%} {#अभिषेकभूमिधर्मालोकमुखमवक्रमण-#}
{#जन्माभिनिष्क्रमण°°°° संदर्शनतायै संवर्तते#}. In Buddhistic
writings it implies moreover the leaving of the house or the
renouncing of the world for the sake of becoming an an-
chorite. (Comp. {%Burnouf'%}s {%Lotus de la bonne loi%} p. 333. 334.)
E. {#क्रम्#} with {#निर्#} and {#अभि,#} kṛt aff. {#ल्युट्#}.
