<L>2250<pc>067-b<k1>अनन्त<k2>अनन्त
{#अनन्त#}¦ Bahuvr. I. m. f. n. ({#-न्तः-न्ता-न्तम्#}) Endless in time
and space: eternal, unbounded, innumerable &c.
<div n="P">II. m. ({#-न्तः#}) <sup>1</sup> A name of Viṣṇu or Kṛṣṇa. <sup>2</sup> A name
of Baladeva, the elder brother of Kṛṣṇa. <sup>3</sup> A name of
Śiva. <sup>4</sup> A name of Rudra, in an Upanishad of the Athar-
vaveda. <sup>5</sup> A name of Śeṣa, the chief of the Nāgas or ser-
pent race that inhabit the infernal regions: the couch and
constant attendant of Viṣṇu. <sup>6</sup> A name of Vāsuki, another
king of the serpents, the brother of the former. <sup>7</sup> A name
of one of the Viśvadevas. <sup>8</sup> The name of the fourteenth of
the twenty-four Arhats or Jaina deified saints of the present
Avasarpiṇī; see also {#अनन्तजित्#}. <sup>9</sup> The name of a king of
Kashmir; see also {#अनन्तदेव#}. <sup>10</sup> A proper name common to
several authors &c. <sup>11</sup> The name of a plant, Vitex trifolia
(Lin.); see {#सिन्दवार#}. <sup>12</sup> Talc (see {#अभ्रक;#} in this sense the
word is given by some as a neuter). <sup>13</sup> (In arithmetic.)
Infinite quantity: a fraction having a cypher for its deno-
minator; see also {#अनन्तराशि#} and {#खहर#}. <sup>14</sup> The name of
the twenty-third of the lunar asterisms; see {#श्रवण#}. <sup>15</sup> A
silken cord with fourteen knots which the Hindus tie round
the right arm at the festival of Anantachaturdaśī.
<div n="P">III. f. ({#-न्ता#}) <sup>1</sup> The earth. <sup>2</sup> (In arithmetic sometimes
used to denominate) the numeral one. <sup>3</sup> A name of Pār-
vatī, the wife of Śiva. <sup>4</sup> A name of Tārā, a Buddhist
deity. <sup>5</sup> The proper name of the wife of Janamejaya. <sup>6</sup> The
name of the following plants: <sup>a.</sup> Hedysarum alhagi; see
{#यवास#} or {#रोदनी#}. <sup>b.</sup> Echytes frutescens; see {#श्यामा#} or {#गो-#}
{#पी#}. <sup>c.</sup> A sort of potherb; see {#विशल्या#} or {#शक्रपुष्पी#}. <sup>d.</sup> Agrostis
linearis (Koen.) or Panicum dactylon; see {#दूर्वा#} or {#भार्गवी,#}
{#श्वेतदूर्वा#} and {#नीलदूर्वा#}. <sup>e.</sup> Phyllanthus emblica (emblic my-
robolan); see {#आमलकी#}. <sup>f.</sup> Menispermum glabrum or cordi-
folium; see {#गुडूची#}. <sup>g.</sup> Gloriosa superba; see {#लाङ्गली#}. <sup>h.</sup> See
{#हेममानी#}. <sup>i.</sup> Premna spinosa; see {#असिमन्थ#}. <sup>k.</sup> Piper longum
(Long pepper); see {#पिप्पली#} or {#कणा#}. <sup>l.</sup> Terminalia chebula
(yellow myrobolan); see {#हरीतकी#}. <sup>m.</sup> Asclepias pseudosar-
sa; see {#शारिवा#}. <sup>n.</sup> Justicia adhatoda(?). <sup>o.</sup> Bromelia Ana-
nas(?). <sup>p.</sup> Echytes dichotoma(?). <sup>q.</sup> According to some
also the same as {#अनन्तमूल#} q. v.
<div n="P">IV. n. ({#-न्तम्#}) <sup>1</sup> Sky, atmosphere, æther. <sup>2</sup> Talc; (also
given in the latter sense as a masculine). E. {#अ#} priv. and
{#अन्त#}.
