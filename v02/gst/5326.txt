{#अप्कृत्स्नसमाधि#}¦ Karmadh. f. ({#-धिः#}) A fanciful name of a
Buddhistic {%samādhi%} or profound meditation. ({%Burnouf%} ren-
ders it in the {%Lotus de la bonne loi:%} ‘meditation which re-
moves all water’, hut believes that the more correct reading
of this word is {#असकृत्समाधि;#} perhaps it may mean: ‘a
meditation completed by means of water’.) E. {#अप्-कृत्स्न#}
and {#समाधि#}.
