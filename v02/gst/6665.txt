{#अभ्यन्तरकरण#}¦ Karmadh. ({#-णम्#}) The same as {#अन्तःकरणम्;#}
and the reverse of {#वाह्यकरण#}. This form of the word oc-
curs in some Mss., but the more correct form is {#आभ्यन्तर-#}
{#करण#} q. v.; and the compound {#वाह्याभ्यन्तरकरण#} which is
of frequent occurrence in {%Vedānta-, Sāṅkhya-%} and {%Yoga%}
writings, is to be analyzed therefore: {#वाह्य-आभ्य°-क°#}.
