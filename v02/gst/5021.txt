{#अपहसित#}¦ Tatpur. n. ({#-तम्#}) An uneducated mode of laughing,
a manner in which low people would laugh, as ‘with tears
coming into the eyes’ ({#‘नीचानामपहसितम्…अपहसितं#}
{#सास्राक्षम्’#}); mentioned in Rhetoric as one of the {#व्यभिचा-#}
{#रिभावाः#} or accessory conditions of the Comic (or {#हास्यरस#}).
Comp. {#अपहास#}. E. {#अप#} and {#हसित#}.
