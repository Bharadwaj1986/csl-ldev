<L>1901<pc>056-a<k1>अधिप्रष्टियुग<k2>अधिप्रष्टियुग
{#अधिप्रष्टियुग#}¦ Tatpur. m. ({#-गः#}) (ved.) The yoke over the
Praṣṭi horse, to which a fourth horse was put (in a cere-
mony connected with the Vājapeya sacrifice; two horses
being put before a cart and a third in front of the two,
so as to form with them a kind of triangle or tripod
({#प्रष्टि#} q. v.), wherefore the third horse was called meta-
phorically the Praṣṭi). E. {#अधिप्रष्टि#} and {#युग#}.
