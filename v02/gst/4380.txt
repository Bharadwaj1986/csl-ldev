<L>4380<pc>150-a<k1>अन्योन्याभाव<k2>अन्योन्याभाव
{#अन्योन्याभाव#}¦ Tatpur. m. ({#-वः#}) (In the Vaiśeṣika philosophy.)
<div n="lb">Mutual negation, one of the four divisions of {#अभाव#} q. v.;
<div n="lb">viz. that kind of negation or mutual exclusion which is held
<div n="lb">to exist between notions that have no property in common;
<div n="lb">indifference considered as a negation of heterogeneous notions;
<div n="lb">as if one says: ‘shape’ is not ‘jar’ ({#‘अन्योन्याभावत्वं तादात्म्यसं-#}
<div n="lb">{#बन्धावच्छिन्नप्रतियोगिताकाभावत्वम्’#}). This notion is different
<div n="lb">therefore from that of {#पृथक्त्व#} q. v. E. {#अन्योन्य#} and {#अभाव#}.
