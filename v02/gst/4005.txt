{#अन्त्यविपुला#}¦ Karmadh. ({#-ला#}) The name of a metre regulated
<div n="lb">by quantity, a species of the Āryā and a variety of the
<div n="lb">Vipulā; it consists of two lines with 30 mātrās or (71/2) feet
<div n="lb">in the first and 27 mātrās or (71/2) feet in the second line,
<div n="lb">the pause in the first being after the 12th mātrā or 3<sup>d</sup> foot,
<div n="lb">in the second placed otherwise. Compare {#आदिविपुला#} and
<div n="lb">{#उभयविपुला#}. E. {#अन्त्य#} and {#विपुला#}.
