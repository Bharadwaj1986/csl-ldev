{#अनुवेल्लित#}¦ Tatpur. n. ({#-तम्#}) One of the fourteen kinds of
bandages used in Hindoo surgery (cf. {#बन्ध#}), a roller or
bandage to encircle the extremities, used esp. when bones
of the hands or feet are fractured. E. {#वेल्ल्#} with {#अनु,#} kṛt
aff. {#क्त#} and āgama {#इट्#}.
