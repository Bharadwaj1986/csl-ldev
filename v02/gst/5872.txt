{#अभवन्मतयोग#}¦ I. Karmadh. m. ({#-गः#}) (In Rhetoric.) One of
<div n="lb">the twenty one defects to be avoided in the composition of
<div n="lb">a sentence (see {#दोष#} and {#वाक्यदोष#}); viz. not conveying ac-
<div n="lb">curately by a sentence the intended sense, <sup>a.</sup> by connecting
<div n="lb">syntactically words which have no logical relation to each
<div n="lb">other, or <sup>b.</sup> by disconnecting syntactically words which are
<div n="lb">logically connected, or <sup>c.</sup> by erring in the mode of con-
<div n="lb">nexion; e. g. <sup>a.</sup> in the sentence {#येषां तास्त्रिदशेभदानसरितः#}
<div n="lb">{#पीताः प्रतापोष्मभिर्लीलापानभुवश्च चन्दनतरुच्छायासु यैः क-#}
<div n="lb">{#ल्पिताः । येषां हुंकृतयः कृतामरपतिक्षोभाः क्षपाचारिणां किं#}
<div n="lb">{#तैस्त्वत्परितोषकारि विहितं किंचित्प्रवादोचितम्#} there is no
<div n="lb">logical relation between the words in the relative and those
<div n="lb">in the correlative sentence; or in the sentence {#अनेन च्छि-#}
<div n="lb">{#न्दता मातुः कण्ठं परशुना तव । बद्धस्पर्शः कृपाणोऽयं ल-#}
<div n="lb">{#ज्जते मम भार्गव,#} the word {#परशु,#} although intended to ex-
<div n="lb">press the reason, has no logical connexion with the blame
[Page235-a+ 56]
<div n="lb">uttered against Paraśurāma; (thus the old critics: the mo-
<div n="lb">dern critics however find no fault with this sentence); or
<div n="lb"><sup>b.</sup> in the verse {#या जयश्रीर्मनोजस्य यया जगदलंकृतम् । यामे-#}
<div n="lb">{#णाक्षीं विना प्राणा विफला मे कुतोऽद्य सा,#} the word {#एणा-#}
<div n="lb">{#क्षीम्#} ought to stand in the correlative sentence so as to
<div n="lb">become connected with the {%three%} preceding relative sen-
<div n="lb">tences which it is not now; (the {%Sāhityad.%} proposes to mend
<div n="lb">the latter part of this Śloka thus: {#यां विनामी वृथा प्राणा#}
<div n="lb">{#एणाक्षी सा कुतोऽद्य मे#}); or <sup>c.</sup> in the verse {#ज्योत्स्ना च यः#}
<div n="lb">{#पयःपूरस्तारकाः कैरवाणि च । राजति व्योमकासारराजहंसः#}
<div n="lb">{#सुधाकरः,#} the word {#कासार#} standing in the same relation
<div n="lb">to {#व्योम,#} as {#पयःपूर#} to {#ज्योत्स्ना#} &c., it ought not to be part
<div n="lb">of a compound, as this kind of syntax would affect the
<div n="lb">whole simile; or in the sentence {#ईक्षसे यत्कटाक्षेण तदा धन्वी#}
<div n="lb">{#मनोभवः#} the connexion between the two sentences ought
<div n="lb">not to have been effected by {#यद्--तदा,#} but by {#चेत्--#}
<div n="lb">{#तदा#}. See also {#अभवन्मतसंबन्ध#}.
