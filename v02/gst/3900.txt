<div n="lb">terior, e. g. of a hall, a house, {#घटः#} ‘a jar in the interior
<div n="lb">of a hall’; {#अन्तर्वती शाला#} ‘a hall in the interior of another

<div n="lb">vaidik instance {#पुमानन्तर्वान्त्स्थविरः पयस्वान्वसोः कबन्धमृ-#}
<div n="lb">{#षभो बिभर्ति;#} (it has been proposed somewhere to translate
<div n="lb">{#अन्तर्वत्#} in this instance ‘pregnant’).
<div n="P">2. f. ({#-त्नी;#} in the Vedas also {#-वती#}) A pregnant woman.
