<div n="lb">order or direction, regular, successive (liter. with the hair),

<div n="lb"><sup>2</sup> (In vaidik grammar; scil. {#अन्वक्षरसन्धि#}) One of the two

<div n="lb">final vowel or the last syllable of {#एषः, स्यः#} or {#सः#} is fol-

<div n="P">3. m. plur. ({#-माः#}) scil. {#वर्णाः;#} mixed tribes or castes;
<div n="lb">for the special meaning, in which this word is used, see

<div n="P">4. f. ({#-मा#}) A woman of a caste inferior to that of the
<div n="lb">man; e. g. a Kṣatriyā woman when spoken of in reference
