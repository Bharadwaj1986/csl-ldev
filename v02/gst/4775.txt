{#अपरान्तज्ञान#}¦ Tatpur. n. ({#-नम्#}) (In the Yoga philosophy.)
<div n="lb">Foreknowledge of death; one of the perfections of a Yogin:
<div n="lb">{#सोपक्रमं निरुपक्रमं च कर्म तत्संयमादपरान्तज्ञानमरिष्टेभ्यो#}
<div n="lb">{#वा#} i. e. ‘action (the consequence of which is preservation of
<div n="lb">life) is either deliberate or not; from the highest degree of
<div n="lb">meditation on it results the prescience of death, or also
<div n="lb">from ominous symptoms (such as one perceives in himself,
<div n="lb">when one loses the powers of hearing, seeing &c.--{#आध्या-#}
<div n="lb">{#त्मिक#}--, or from apparitions of ghosts, Manes &c.--{#आधि-#}
<div n="lb">{#भौतिक#}--, or from divine visions as that of the paradise,
<div n="lb">of the Siddhas &c.--{#आधिदैविक#} qq. vv.). E. {#अपरान्त#}
<div n="lb">and {#ज्ञान#}.
