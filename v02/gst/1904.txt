{#अधिमन्थ#}¦ Tatpur. m. ({#-न्थः#}) (In medicine.) Severe ophthal-
mia which is described as having the following symptoms:
‘acute pains as if the eyes were torn; throbbing which ex-
tends to half of the head’; it consists of four varieties ac-
cording to its being produced by derangement of the air,
bile, phlegm or blood. Also {#अधीमन्थ#}. E. {#अधि#} and {#मन्थ#}.
