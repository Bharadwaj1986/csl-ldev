{#अन्तरात्मन्#}¦ Tatpur. m. ({#-त्मा#}) <sup>1</sup> The Supreme Soul (comp.
<div n="lb">{#परमात्मन्#} and {#पुरुष#}) as residing in the interior of man, as
<div n="lb">the inward Spirit or individual Soul. In the Upanishads
<div n="lb">the words {#पुरुष, आत्मन्#} and {#अन्तरात्मन्#} are often used
<div n="lb">apparently as synonymes, but the term {#अन्तरात्मन्#} is, more
<div n="lb">especially, appropriated there to the notion of the Supreme
<div n="lb">Soul when it resides, according to their doctrine, in the
<div n="lb">interior of the heart, of a thumb's size ({#‘अङ्गुष्ठमात्रः पु-#}
<div n="lb">{#रुषोऽन्तरात्मा सदा जनानां हृदये संनिविष्टः’#}); [a devia-
<div n="lb">tion, however, in this respect may be noticed in a passage
<div n="lb">of the same Kaṭha-Upan. which contains also the preceding
<div n="lb">quotation (viz. {#अङ्गुष्ठमात्रः पुरुषो मध्य आत्मनि तिष्ठति#})
<div n="lb">where {#पुरुषः#} seems to have been considered as distinct
<div n="lb">from {#आत्मन्#} and not residing in the heart, although Śan-
<div n="lb">kara endeavours to effect a reconciliation with the general
<div n="lb">doctrine by interpreting {#आत्मन्#} in that passage by {#शरीर#}].
<div n="lb">--In the Vedānta the notion of the {#अन्तरात्मन्#} has passed
<div n="lb">into that of the {#जीवात्मन्#} q. v. In the other philosophies
<div n="lb">the terms {#आत्मन्#} and {#पुरुष,#} in the sense they assume in these
<div n="lb">phil., have superseded the term {#अन्तरात्मन्#}.--Manu uses
<div n="lb">the word {#अन्तरात्मन्#} sometimes in the general sense of {#आ-#}
<div n="lb">{#त्मन्,#} sometimes in the special sense of {#जीवात्मन्#} (e. g. {#जी-#}
<div n="lb">{#वसंज्ञोऽन्तरात्मान्यः सहजः सर्वदेहिनाम्#}); Yājnavalkya, how-
<div n="lb">ever, employs the terms {#आत्मन्#} and {#अन्तरात्मन्#} as syno-
<div n="lb">nymes to denote the Universal Soul, while he associates
<div n="lb">with {#पुरुष#} the idea of the manifested or individual Soul ({#अ-#}
<div n="lb">{#नादिरात्मा संभूतिर्विद्यते नान्तरात्मनः । समवायो तु पुरुषो#}
<div n="lb">{#मोहेच्छाद्वेषकर्मजः;#} comp. also {#मोहजालमपास्येह पुरुषो दृश्यते#}
<div n="lb">{#हि यः सहस्रकरपन्नेत्रः सूर्यवर्चाः सहस्रकः । स आत्मा#} &c.).
