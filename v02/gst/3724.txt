{#अन्तःस्थ#}¦ Tatpur. I. m. f. n. ({#-स्थः-स्था-स्थम्#}) <sup>1</sup> Being amidst,
<div n="lb">being between, included, comprehended. <sup>2</sup> Being in the
<div n="lb">interior.
<div n="P">II. m. plur. ({#-स्थाः#}) (ved.) An epithet of the {#अवाञ्चः प्रा-#}
<div n="lb">{#णाः#} or lower vital organs, viz. of the anus and of the
<div n="lb">organs of generation (as being amongst the vital organs or
<div n="lb">{#प्राणाः#} in general).
<div n="P">III. f. ({#-स्था#}) <sup>1</sup> (ved.) An epithet of one of the Sāmi-
<div n="lb">dhenī-Mantras or verses from the Ṛgveda recited at the
<div n="lb">kindling of the sacrificial fire, viz. of the Sāmidhenī which
<div n="lb">begins with the words {#‘अग्निं दूतं वृणीमहे’,#} because this
<div n="lb">Sāmidhenī is amongst the eleven Sāmidhenīs which praise
<div n="lb">the vital functions, in as much as it praises the {#मध्यमप्राण#}
<div n="lb">or the vital function on which rest all the other, i. e. the
<div n="lb">vital energy which resides in the middle of the body. <sup>2</sup> (scil.
<div n="lb">{#देवता;#} ved.) The divinity of the vital organs. [As the
<div n="lb">meanings II. and III. 1. 2. are given on the authority of
<div n="lb">{%Sāyaṇa's%} comm. on the {%Śatapathabr.%} I. 4. 3. 8, it will be ne-
<div n="lb">cessary to quote the latter in full--from two Mss. of the
<div n="lb">E. I. H. and one of the Bodl.--as a so called Extract from
<div n="lb">it, which has appeared in print, is apt to mislead the reader
<div n="lb">completely on the bearing of the word and of the passage
<div n="lb">itself. The comm. runs thus: {#य एवोऽयं मध्यमः प्राण इति ।#}
<div n="lb">{#प्राणापानादिपञ्चवृत्त्याश्रयभूतो यः क्रियाशक्त्यात्मको देहमध्ये#}
<div n="lb">{#ऽवस्थितः प्राणः । तस्य समिन्धनमग्निं दूतमित्यनया दूतो ह्यग्निः#}
<div n="lb">{#सर्वेषां देवानां मुखं च तथा प्राणेऽपीति#} (v. 1. {#प्राणीमीति#}) {#।#}

<div n="lb">{#वृणीमह इति सामिधेनी मध्यमप्राणरूपेण प्राणापानादि संस्तु-#}
