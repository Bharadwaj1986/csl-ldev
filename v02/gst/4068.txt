{#अन्धपूतना#}¦ Karmadh. f. ({#-ना#}) A female imp or devil, one of
the nine evil spirits which are supposed to possess children
and to work in them diseases generally believed to be in-
curable (see {#ग्रह#}); she is imagined ‘of formidable size, tawny,
bald and wearing yellow-red garments’. Compare the fol-
lowing. E. {#अन्ध#} and {#पूतना#}.
