{#अन्यत्रमनस्#}¦ Bahuvr. m. f. n. ({#-नाः-नाः-नः#}) Having the mind
directed on another object, absent (mentally). [The passage
of the Bṛhadāraṇyaka Upan. {#अन्यत्रमना अभूवं नाश्रौषम्#}
&c. is given as a proof by the Sāṅkhya Pravachana Bh. for
the orthodoxy of the Sāṅkhya tenet that {#मनस्#} is capable
of becoming multifarious.] E. {#अन्यत्र#} and {#मनस्#}.
