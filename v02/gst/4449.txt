{#अन्वित#}¦ Tatpur. m. f. n. ({#-तः-ता-तम्#}) <sup>1</sup> Following, pursuing.
<sup>2</sup> Followed by, connected, possessed of, endowed with. <sup>3</sup> Logi-
cally connected, as in construction or sense. <sup>4</sup> Acquired
(mentally), understood, become clear; e. g. {#यद्यत्साधु न#}
{#चित्रे स्यात्क्रियते तत्तदन्यथा । तथापि तस्या लावण्यं रेखया#}
{#किंचिदन्वितम्#} (where--Śākuntala VI. v. 146--{#अन्वित#} does
neither mean ‘imitated, rendered’ nor ‘possessed’). Also
{#अन्वीत;#} comp. {#अनुगत#}. E. {#इ#} with {#अनु,#} kṛt aff. {#क्त#}.
