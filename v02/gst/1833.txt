{#अधिकतिथि#}¦ Karmadh. m. f. ({#-थिः-थिः#}) An intercalary lunar
day. When no tithi (q. v.) begins or ends in a solar day,
the numeral of the preceding is repeated in the calendar,
the first tithi being accounted the intercalated and the second
the proper one. Compare {#क्षयतिथि#}. E. {#अधिक#} and {#तिथि#}.
