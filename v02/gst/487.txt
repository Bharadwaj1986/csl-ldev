{#अग्न्याधान#}¦ Tatpur. n. ({#-नम्#}) The placing of the fire on the
<div n="lb">ground previously prepared for it (see {#अग्निचयन#}); a cere-
<div n="lb">mony which is necessary to make the Āhavanīya and the
<div n="lb">other sacred fires (see {#अग्नि#}) fit for sacrificial use and is fol-
<div n="lb">lowed by the Pavamāneṣtis (q. v.). See {#अग्निसंस्कार#}. The
<div n="lb">act takes place in the spring, if the person belongs to the
<div n="lb">cast of the Brāhmaṇas, in the hot season or grīṣma if he
<div n="lb">is a Kshatriya, in the autumn or śarad if he is a Vaiśya
<div n="lb">and is performed once for all, to serve all future purposes.
<div n="lb">E. {#अग्नि#} and {#आधान#}.
