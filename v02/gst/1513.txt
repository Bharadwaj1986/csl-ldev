{#अत्युक्ता#}¦ Tatpur. f. ({#-क्ता#}) The name of a class of metres, regulated
by number and quantity; it consists of a stanza of four lines
with two syllables in each line and comprises four varieties.
[Page043-b+ 56]
(See <sup>1</sup> {#स्त्री#} or {#काम,#} <sup>2</sup> {#रति#} or {#मही,#} <sup>3</sup> {#सारु,#} <sup>4</sup> {#मधु#}.) Also called
{#अत्युक्था#}. E. {#अति#} (sc. {#क्रान्ता#}) and {#उक्ता,#} another class of
metres (in the sense of the accusative).
