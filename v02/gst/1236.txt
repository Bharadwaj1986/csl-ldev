{#अतिपादनिचृत्#}¦ Tatpur. f. ({#-त्#}) The name of a vaidik metre
regulated by the number of syllables. It is a species of the
Gāyatrī metre and consists of three Pādas of six, eight
and seven syllables in each. Also read {#अतिपादनिवृत्#}. E.
{#अति#} (sc. {#क्रान्ता#}) and {#पादनिचृत्#} (or {#पादनिवृत्#}), in the
sense of the accusative.
