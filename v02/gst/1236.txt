{#अतिपादनिचृत्#}¦ Tatpur. f. ({#-त्#}) The name of a vaidik metre
<div n="lb">regulated by the number of syllables. It is a species of the
<div n="lb">Gāyatrī metre and consists of three Pādas of six, eight
<div n="lb">and seven syllables in each. Also read {#अतिपादनिवृत्#}. E.
<div n="lb">{#अति#} (sc. {#क्रान्ता#}) and {#पादनिचृत्#} (or {#पादनिवृत्#}), in the
<div n="lb">sense of the accusative.
