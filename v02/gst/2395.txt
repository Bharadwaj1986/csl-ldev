{#अनर्वन्#}¦ Bahuvr. m. ({#-र्वा#}) (ved.) <sup>1</sup> Not moving, fixed. <sup>2</sup> Not
passing away, permanent. <sup>3</sup> Without an impediment, not
thwarted, unobstructed. <sup>4</sup> Without an enemy or foe. See
the preceding. (The declension of this word differs from
that of {#अर्वन्#} in so far, as it does not assume the ādeśa {#तृ#}
(i. e. {#त्#}) which is said to be the substitute of the {#न्#} in
{#अर्वन्#} q. v.; e. g. {#अनर्वाणम्, अनर्वाणौ, अनर्वाणः#}.) E. {#अ#}
priv. and {#अर्वन्#}.
