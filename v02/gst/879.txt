{#अजहत्स्वार्था#}¦ Tatpur. f. ({#-र्था#}) A figure of speech, the cha-
<div n="lb">racteristic of the {#उपादानलक्षणा#} q. v. It consists in using
<div n="lb">a word in an elliptic meaning without depriving it of its
<div n="lb">primary sense; in such phrases as {#श्वेतो धावति#} or {#कुन्ताः#}
<div n="lb">{#प्रविशन्ति,#} are to be supplied {#अश्व#} &c. in the former, {#पु-#}
<div n="lb">{#रुष#} &c. in the latter, so as to impart the sense of ‘a white
<div n="lb">horse gallops’, ‘the men with lances enter’, while {#श्वेत#}
<div n="lb">and {#कुन्त#} retain their original sense. See, on the contrary,
<div n="lb">{#जहत्स्वार्था#}.--E. {#अ#} neg. and {#जहत्स्वार्था,#} scil. {#लक्षणा#}.
