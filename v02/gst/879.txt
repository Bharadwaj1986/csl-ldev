{#अजहत्स्वार्था#}¦ Tatpur. f. ({#-र्था#}) A figure of speech, the cha-
racteristic of the {#उपादानलक्षणा#} q. v. It consists in using
a word in an elliptic meaning without depriving it of its
primary sense; in such phrases as {#श्वेतो धावति#} or {#कुन्ताः#}
{#प्रविशन्ति,#} are to be supplied {#अश्व#} &c. in the former, {#पु-#}
{#रुष#} &c. in the latter, so as to impart the sense of ‘a white
horse gallops’, ‘the men with lances enter’, while {#श्वेत#}
and {#कुन्त#} retain their original sense. See, on the contrary,
{#जहत्स्वार्था#}.--E. {#अ#} neg. and {#जहत्स्वार्था,#} scil. {#लक्षणा#}.
