<div n="P">II. f. n. ({#-न्दाः-न्दः#}) The name of two classes of metres
regulated by number and quantity, the first of which com-
prises the classes {#अतिजगती, शक्वरी, अतिशक्वरी, अष्टि,#}
{#अत्यष्टि, धृति, अतिधृति#} (qq. vv.) and the second the classes
{#कृति, प्रकृति, आकृति, विकृति, सङ्कृति, अतिकृति#} (or {#अभि-#}
{#कृति#}), {#उत्कृति#} (qq. vv.).
<div n="P">III. f. ({#-न्दाः#}) The name of one of the consecrated bricks
or iṣṭakās. E. {#अति#} (sc. {#क्रान्त#}) and {#छन्दस्#} I. desire, II.
metre (in the sense of the accusative) ‘going or having
gone beyond desires’ and ‘going beyond the seven Chhan-
das or metres {#गायत्री, उष्णिह्, अनुष्टुभ्, बृहती, पङ्क्ति, त्रि-#}
{#ष्टुभ्#} and {#जगती#}’.
