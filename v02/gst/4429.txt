<L>4429<pc>153-b<k1>अन्वाधेय<k2>अन्वाधेय
{#अन्वाधेय#}¦ Tatpur. n. ({#-यम्#}) (In Law.) An item of a married
woman's property; see {#स्त्रीधन#}. It is defined by {%Kātyāyana%}
as property ‘which is received by a woman, after marriage,
from the family of her husband or from her own relatives’
(i. e. from her maternal uncle &c., according to Viṣṇu,
or from the relatives of her parents, as the author of
the Dāyabhāga interprets the latter word). [{#कात्यायनः ।#}
{#विवाहात्परतो यत्तु लब्धं भर्तृकुलात्स्त्रिया । अन्वाधेयं तदुक्तं#}
{#तु लब्धं बन्धुकुलात्तथा ॥ ‘विष्णुवचने च बन्धुपदं मातुलाद्यभि-#}
{#प्रायम्’#} or {#‘भर्तृकुलात् । श्वशुरकुलादेः । बन्धुकुलात् । पितृमा-#}
{#तृकुलात्#}.--The reading in the printed edd. of the second
portion of the Mitākṣara {#‘लब्धं पितृकुलात्तथा’#} would there-
fore require {#पितृ#} to be taken in the sense of {#‘पितरः#} parents’
not in that of ‘father’; but the given reading seems pre-
ferable as it occurs in several Mss. (E. I. H.) of this work,
in Kullūka on Manu 9. 195 (with the v. 1. {#बन्धुकुले तथा#}), in two
passages of the Dāyabhāga (pp. 116 and 149) with the com-
ment quoted, and in the Dāyakramas. p. 17, where the comm.
is: {#बन्धुकुलादित्यत्र बन्धुपदेन मातापित्रोरुपलक्षणम्#}. In the
Vivādachintāmaṇi p. 139 the latter half of the verse reads
thus: {#अन्वाधेयं तु तत्प्रोक्तं यल्लब्धं स्वकुलात्तथा#}.] {%Bhṛgu%} defines
this property as ‘whatever is received with pleasure by a
woman, after the marriage ceremony, from her husband and
her parents’ [{#ऊर्ध्वं लब्धं तु यत्किंचित्संस्कारात्प्रीतितः स्त्रिया ।#}
{#भर्तुः पित्रोः सकाशाद्वा#} (or {#भर्तुः सकाशात्पित्रोर्वा#}) {#अन्वाधेयं#}
{#तु तद्भृगुः#}]; when the Dāyabhāga and Dāyakramas. in quoting
this passage supply after ‘her husband’: ‘and from those re-
lated to her through her husband, i. e. father-in-law &c.’ and
after ‘her parents’: ‘and from those related to her through
her parents, i. e. grandparents &c.’, so as to effectuate a con-
cordance between Kātyāyana and Bhṛgu. [Dāyabh. {#माता-#}
{#पितृद्वारेण संबन्धिनां पित्रोश्च सकाशाद्यत्तु विवाहात्परतो ल-#}
{#ब्धम् । तथा भर्तुः सकाशाद्भर्तृकुलाच्च श्वशुरादितो यल्लब्धं धनं#}
{#तदन्वाधेयम्;#} Dāyakr. {#भर्तृद्वारेण संबन्धिनां श्वशुरादीनां मा-#}
{#तापितृद्वारेण संबन्धिनां मातामहपितामहादीनां च सकाशात्#}
&c.] E. {#अनु#} (after, scil. marriage) and {#आधेय;#} scil. {#स्त्रीधन#} q. v.
