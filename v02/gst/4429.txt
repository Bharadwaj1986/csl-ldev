{#अन्वाधेय#}¦ Tatpur. n. ({#-यम्#}) (In Law.) An item of a married
<div n="lb">woman's property; see {#स्त्रीधन#}. It is defined by {%Kātyāyana%}
<div n="lb">as property ‘which is received by a woman, after marriage,
<div n="lb">from the family of her husband or from her own relatives’
<div n="lb">(i. e. from her maternal uncle &c., according to Viṣṇu,
<div n="lb">or from the relatives of her parents, as the author of
<div n="lb">the Dāyabhāga interprets the latter word). [{#कात्यायनः ।#}
<div n="lb">{#विवाहात्परतो यत्तु लब्धं भर्तृकुलात्स्त्रिया । अन्वाधेयं तदुक्तं#}
<div n="lb">{#तु लब्धं बन्धुकुलात्तथा ॥ ‘विष्णुवचने च बन्धुपदं मातुलाद्यभि-#}
<div n="lb">{#प्रायम्’#} or {#‘भर्तृकुलात् । श्वशुरकुलादेः । बन्धुकुलात् । पितृमा-#}
<div n="lb">{#तृकुलात्#}.--The reading in the printed edd. of the second
<div n="lb">portion of the Mitākṣara {#‘लब्धं पितृकुलात्तथा’#} would there-
<div n="lb">fore require {#पितृ#} to be taken in the sense of {#‘पितरः#} parents’
<div n="lb">not in that of ‘father’; but the given reading seems pre-
<div n="lb">ferable as it occurs in several Mss. (E. I. H.) of this work,
<div n="lb">in Kullūka on Manu 9. 195 (with the v. 1. {#बन्धुकुले तथा#}), in two
<div n="lb">passages of the Dāyabhāga (pp. 116 and 149) with the com-
<div n="lb">ment quoted, and in the Dāyakramas. p. 17, where the comm.
<div n="lb">is: {#बन्धुकुलादित्यत्र बन्धुपदेन मातापित्रोरुपलक्षणम्#}. In the
<div n="lb">Vivādachintāmaṇi p. 139 the latter half of the verse reads
<div n="lb">thus: {#अन्वाधेयं तु तत्प्रोक्तं यल्लब्धं स्वकुलात्तथा#}.] {%Bhṛgu%} defines
<div n="lb">this property as ‘whatever is received with pleasure by a
<div n="lb">woman, after the marriage ceremony, from her husband and
<div n="lb">her parents’ [{#ऊर्ध्वं लब्धं तु यत्किंचित्संस्कारात्प्रीतितः स्त्रिया ।#}
<div n="lb">{#भर्तुः पित्रोः सकाशाद्वा#} (or {#भर्तुः सकाशात्पित्रोर्वा#}) {#अन्वाधेयं#}
<div n="lb">{#तु तद्भृगुः#}]; when the Dāyabhāga and Dāyakramas. in quoting
<div n="lb">this passage supply after ‘her husband’: ‘and from those re-
<div n="lb">lated to her through her husband, i. e. father-in-law &c.’ and
<div n="lb">after ‘her parents’: ‘and from those related to her through
<div n="lb">her parents, i. e. grandparents &c.’, so as to effectuate a con-
<div n="lb">cordance between Kātyāyana and Bhṛgu. [Dāyabh. {#माता-#}
<div n="lb">{#पितृद्वारेण संबन्धिनां पित्रोश्च सकाशाद्यत्तु विवाहात्परतो ल-#}
<div n="lb">{#ब्धम् । तथा भर्तुः सकाशाद्भर्तृकुलाच्च श्वशुरादितो यल्लब्धं धनं#}
<div n="lb">{#तदन्वाधेयम्;#} Dāyakr. {#भर्तृद्वारेण संबन्धिनां श्वशुरादीनां मा-#}
<div n="lb">{#तापितृद्वारेण संबन्धिनां मातामहपितामहादीनां च सकाशात्#}
<div n="lb">&c.] E. {#अनु#} (after, scil. marriage) and {#आधेय;#} scil. {#स्त्रीधन#} q. v.
