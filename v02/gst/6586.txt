{#अभीमोद#}¦ Tatpur. m. ({#-दः#}) Excessive happiness or joy; e. g.
{%Atharv.:%} {#आनन्दा मोदाः प्रमुदोऽभीमोदमुदश्च ये । उच्छि-#}
{#ष्टाज्जज्ञिरे सर्वे दिवि देवा दिविश्रितः;#} or {#आनन्दा मोदाः#}
{#प्रमुदोऽभीमोदमुदश्च ये । हसो नरिष्टा नृत्तानि शरीरमनु#}
{#प्राविशन्#} (where {#अभीमोदमुदः#} is either Dwandwa ‘ex-
cessive joy and pleasure’ or perhaps an irregular intensitive
formation with the reduplication of the whole radical in-
stead of its first syllable (similar in some degree to the ved.
{#बद्बध्#} the intens. of {#बन्ध्#}); the masc. of the relative, {#ये,#}
being accounted for by the preceding masculines). Comp.
{#अभीलाप#}. E. {#मुद्#} with {#अभि,#} kṛt aff. {#घञ्#}.
