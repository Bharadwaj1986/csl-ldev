<L>3064<pc>093-a<k1>अनुपपादक<k2>अनुपपादक
{#अनुपपादक#}¦ Bahuvr. m. pl. ({#-काः#}) (In the Buddhistic doctrine
of Nepal) a class of Buddhas which is of non-material ori-
gin, also called {#ध्यानिबुद्धाः#} ‘the Buddhas of contemplation’;
in contradistinction from the {#मानुषिबुद्धाः#} or the seven Bud-
dhas of human origin, the last of which is Śākyamuni.
E. {#अ#} priv. and {#उपपादक#} lit. ‘without a parent’.
