{#अग्निविहरण#}¦ Tatpur. n. ({#-णम्#}) Removing the sacrificial fire,
a ceremony in the Jyotiṣṭoma sacrifice which consists in
taking the fire from the Āgnīdhra Manḍapa (q. v.) and
placing it in the Sadas (q. v.), another Manḍapa in the
Saumikī Vedi (q. v.). E. {#अग्नि#} and {#विहरण#}.
