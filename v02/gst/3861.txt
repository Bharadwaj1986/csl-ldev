{#अन्तर्द्वार#}¦ Tatpur. n. ({#-रम्#}) A private door within the house;
<div n="lb">the interpretations of this word vary; according to the
<div n="lb">different comm. it would imply <sup>a.</sup> A private or secret door
<div n="lb">of a house in general. <sup>b.</sup> A secret door of a mansion &c.
<div n="lb">({#सुधादौ गुप्तद्वारम्#}). <sup>c.</sup> A court-door ({#प्रकोष्ठद्वारम्#}). <sup>d.</sup> A
<div n="lb">treasury door ({#कोष्ठकद्वारम्#}). <sup>e.</sup> A side or back-door, a
<div n="lb">sally-port ({#पक्षद्वारम्#}). <sup>f.</sup> A window, a wicket, an eyelet-
<div n="lb">hole ({#जालद्वारम्#}). It corresponds with the Hind. {#खिडकी#}
<div n="lb">or {#खिडकिका#}. E. {#अन्तर्#} and {#द्वार#}.
