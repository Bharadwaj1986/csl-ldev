{#अन्तर्द्वार#}¦ Tatpur. n. ({#-रम्#}) A private door within the house;
the interpretations of this word vary; according to the
different comm. it would imply <sup>a.</sup> A private or secret door
of a house in general. <sup>b.</sup> A secret door of a mansion &c.
({#सुधादौ गुप्तद्वारम्#}). <sup>c.</sup> A court-door ({#प्रकोष्ठद्वारम्#}). <sup>d.</sup> A
treasury door ({#कोष्ठकद्वारम्#}). <sup>e.</sup> A side or back-door, a
sally-port ({#पक्षद्वारम्#}). <sup>f.</sup> A window, a wicket, an eyelet-
hole ({#जालद्वारम्#}). It corresponds with the Hind. {#खिडकी#}
or {#खिडकिका#}. E. {#अन्तर्#} and {#द्वार#}.
