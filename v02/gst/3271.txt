{#अनुरूप#}¦ I. Bahuvr. 1. m. f. n. ({#-पः-पा-पम्#}) <sup>1</sup> Like, resembling.
<sup>2</sup> Fit, suitable. <sup>3</sup> According to.
<div n="P">2. m. ({#-पः#}) The second of three {#तृच#} or stanzas (each
consisting of three verses) recited at a sacrificial act; of the
three {#तृच,#} for instance, in the beginning of the second
portion of the Sāmaveda, {#उपास्मै गायत नराः#} &c., {#दवि-#}
{#द्युतत्या रुचा#} &c., and {#पवमानस्य ते कवे#} &c. which form the
{#बहिष्पवमानस्तोत्र#} and are recited e. g. in the {#द्वादशाह#} sacri-
fice, the Tṛcha which begins with the words {#दविद्युतत्या#}
{#रुचा#} is called the {#अनुरूप#}. (See besides {#स्तोत्रीय#} and {#पर्यास#}.)
<div n="P">3. n. ({#-पम्#}) <sup>1</sup> Conformity, likeness, analogy ({#अनुरूपेण#}
conformably, agreeably to). <sup>2</sup> Fitness, suitableness.
<div n="P">II. Avyayībh. ({#-पम्#}) Conformably, agreeably to. E. {#अनु#}
and {#रूप#}.
