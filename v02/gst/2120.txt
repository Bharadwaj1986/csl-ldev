{#अध्रिगु#}¦ Bahuvr. m. ({#-गुः#}--n. pl. {#-गावः#}) (ved.) <sup>1</sup> Of un-
restrained or irresistible course; esp. as an epithet of Indra,
Agni, Soma, the Rudras and the Aśvins. <sup>2</sup> The name of

<sup>3</sup> The name of a Praishamantra (q. v.) used in the cere-
mony of the killing of the Agnīṣomīyapaśu (q. v.), and
beginning with the words {#दैव्याः शमितार आरभध्वमुत म-#}
{#नुष्या उपनयत मेध्याः#} &c. E. {#अध्रि#} and {#गो#}.
