{#अन्धाहि#}¦ Karmadh. m. f. ({#-हिः-हिः#}) <sup>1</sup> A blind snake, belong-
ing to the not poisonous class. <sup>2</sup> A kind of fish, vulg.
Kūñchiyā; a species of eel; see {#कुचिका#}. [As both meanings
do not occur in the same author, the latter is perhaps only
a further explanation of the former.] E. {#अन्ध#} and {#अहि#}.
