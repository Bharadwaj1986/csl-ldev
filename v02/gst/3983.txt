{#अन्त्य#}¦ 1. m. f. n. ({#-न्त्यः-न्त्या-न्त्यम्#}) <sup>1</sup> Last, ultimate. (In the
Vaiśeṣika philos., {#अन्त्यो विशेषः#} is called ‘the ultimate
degree of particularity or divisibility, which is the condition
of eternal substances only’, viz. the condition of simple,
non-compound substances, of atoms, since there is an end
of discrimination when this last degree of elementary
existence has been arrived at; compare also {#अन्त्यावस्थिति#}.)
<sup>2</sup> Final (as the letter of a word). <sup>3</sup> Nearest, e. g. {#नवान्त्य#}
nearest to nine i. e. ten. Comp. {#अन्तिम#} and the words
named s. v. {#अन्तिक#}. <sup>4</sup> Inferior, lowest, belonging to the
lowest tribe (see the following meanings). [Tatpur. com-
pounds--not Karmadh.--the latter part of which is
{#अन्त्य#} have the udātta accent on the first syllable.]
<div n="P">2. m. ({#-न्त्यः#}) <sup>1</sup> A man of the lowest tribe. See {#अन्त्यज#}.
<sup>2</sup> A Mlechchha or foreigner. See {#अन्त्यज#}. <sup>3</sup> (In Astronomy.)
The twelfth or last lunar month, called Phālguna. <sup>4</sup> A
fragrant grass (Cyperus rotundus). See {#मुस्ता#}. <sup>5</sup> m. pl.
({#-न्त्याः#}) The name of a people or country; read also {#अन्धाः#}
or {#अन्ध्राः#} or {#अध्याः#}.
<div n="P">3. f. ({#-न्त्या#}) <sup>1</sup> A woman of the lowest tribe. See {#अन्त्यज#}.
<sup>2</sup> (In Astronomy) thus defined: {#अहोरात्रवृत्तव्यासार्धं त्रिज्या-#}
{#तुल्यैरङ्कैरङ्क्यते तावत्त्रिज्यातुल्यं भवति तैरङ्कैर्यावत्कुज्या गण्यते#}
{#तावच्चरज्यातुल्या भवति । अथ चरज्यया त्रिज्या युतोना अन्त्या-#}
{#संज्ञा भवति#} (Siddhāntaśir. Gaṇitādhy. ed. Calc. p. 128).
<div n="P">4. n. ({#-न्त्यम्#}) <sup>1</sup> (In Arithm.) A measure of number,
a thousand billions (1000,000,000,000,000). <sup>2</sup> (In Astronomy.)
The last or twelfth sign ({#लग्न#}) of the Zodiac, the Pisces
or {#मीनराशि#}. See {#अन्त्यभ#}. <sup>3</sup> (In Astronomy.) The last or
twenty seventh mansion in the circumference of the moon's
periodical revolution, the Nakshatra Revatī, the Yoga star
of which is <lang n="greek">ζ</lang> Piscium. See {#अन्त्यभ#}. E. {#अन्त,#} taddh. aff. {#यत्#}.
