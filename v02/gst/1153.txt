<div n="P">II. Tatpur. (In the philosophical terminology of the
Upanishads) that which goes beyond the grahas or the
eight instruments of apprehension i. e. the objects or func-
tions of apprehension themselves: thus with the graha {#प्राण#}
(meaning there ‘the nose’) corresponds the atigraha {#अपान#}
(meaning there ‘fragrant substance’), with the graha {#वाच्,#}
the atigraha {#नामन्#} and likewise, with the other six grahas
{#जिह्वा, चक्षुस्, श्रोत्र, मनस्, हस्त, त्वच्,#} the atigrahas {#रस,#}
{#रूप, शब्द, काम, कर्मन्#} and {#स्पर्श#}.--Also called {#अतिग्राह#}.
E. {#अति#} and {#ग्रह#} (in the sense of the accusative).
