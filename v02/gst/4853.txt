{#अपर्ण#}¦ I. Bahuvr. 1. m. f. n. ({#-र्णः-र्णा-र्णम्#}) Leafless.
<div n="P">2. f. ({#-र्णा#}) A name of Umā, as a daughter of Himavat
and Menā, so called because she did not even eat a leaf
during her performance of religious austerities, while her
sisters {#एकपर्णा#} and {#एकपाटला#} partook at least, the one of
one leaf and the other of one Pāṭalā flower. E. {#अ#} priv.
and {#पर्ण#}.
<div n="P">II. Bahuvr. m. f. n. ({#-र्णः-र्णा-र्णम्#}) Free from debt or
obligation; e. g. {#तत्रापर्णाय ततस्वनयाद्भैमी तपस्यपर्णायतत ।#}
{#तुलितसुपर्णाय ततस्तस्यागमनाय सर्तुपर्णाय ततः ।#}. E. {#अप#}
and {#ऋण#}.
