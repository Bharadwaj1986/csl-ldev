{#अनुग्रहसर्ग#}¦ Tatpur. m. ({#-र्गः#}) (In the Sāṅkhya philosophy.) The
creation of mental conditions; in some of the Purāṇas it
is considered as an eighth creation, in others as a fifth, and
subdivided into {#विपर्यय, अशक्ति, सिद्धि#} and {#तुष्टि#} qq. vv.
See also {#प्रत्ययसर्ग#}. E. {#अनुग्रह#} (perhaps in the sense of
‘feeling’ in general) and {#सर्ग#}.
