{#अभिमान#}¦ Tatpur. m. ({#-नः#}) <sup>1</sup> (In Philosophy.) Conceiving
<div n="lb">objects with the notion that they refer to one's own self:
<div n="lb">the mental process which is the essential characteristic of
<div n="lb">{#अहङ्कार#} (of the conception of ‘I’ or of the notion of per-
<div n="lb">sonality) q. v.; e. g. {%Sāṅkhya Sūtra:%} {#अभिमानोऽहंकारः#}
<div n="lb">({%Vijnānāch.:%} {#अहं करोतीत्यहंकारः कुम्भकारवत् । अन्तःकर-#}
<div n="lb">{#णद्रव्यं स च धर्मधर्म्यभेदादभिमान इत्युक्तः#}); or {%Vedānta Sāra:%}
<div n="lb">{#अभिमानात्मिकान्तःकरणवृत्तिरहंकारः;#} and therefore in {%cor-%}
<div n="lb">{%rect writing%} not identical with the philosophical term {#अहं-#}
<div n="lb">{#कार#} itself, although some authors use both words some-
<div n="lb">times apparently as synonymes: {%Vijnānāch.:%} {#अहंकारश्चाभि-#}
<div n="lb">{#मानवृत्तिकमन्तःकरणद्रव्यं न त्वभिमानमात्रं द्रव्यस्यैव लोके#}
<div n="lb">{#द्रव्योपादानत्वदर्शनात्#}. Yet as the notion of personality
<div n="lb">in the orthodox philosophical systems is not our notion
<div n="lb">of self-consciousness, but the notion based on that con-
<div n="lb">dition of the mind which identifies the {%bodily personality%}
<div n="lb">or {%individual existence%} with the Absolute, and as this
<div n="lb">mental condition is held to lead to an erroneous concep-
<div n="lb">tion of the absolute Truth, the terms {#अहंकार#} and {#अ-#}
<div n="lb">{#भिमान#} have generally a {%negative%} bearing, the latter imply-
<div n="lb">ing: ‘conceiving the {%wrong%} or {%erroneous idea%} that the ob-
<div n="lb">jects of the world refer absolutely to one's own {%bodily%} or
<div n="lb">{%individual%} self’; e. g. {%Viśvan.%} (on the {%Nyāya S.%} {#दोषनिमि-#}
<div n="lb">{#त्तानां तत्वज्ञानादहंकारनिवृत्तिः#}): {#अहंकारोऽहमित्यभिमानः#}
<div n="lb">{#स च शरीरादिविषयको मिथ्याज्ञानमुच्यते#} (comp. the {%Sid-%}
<div n="lb">{%dhāntamuktāv.:%} {#अहंकारोऽहमिति प्रत्ययः । तस्याश्रयो विषय#}
<div n="lb">{#आत्मनि शरीरादिरिति#}); or {%Sāṅkhyatattvak.:%} {#यत्खल्वालो-#}
<div n="lb">{#चितं मतं च तत्राहमधिकृतः । शक्तः खल्वहमत्र । मदर्था एवा-#}
<div n="lb">{#मी विषयाः । मत्तो नान्योऽत्राधिकृतः कश्चिदस्त्यतोऽहमस्मी-#}
<div n="lb">{#ति योऽभिमानः सोऽसाधारणव्यापारत्वादहंकारः#}. <sup>2</sup> Con-
<div n="lb">ception in general, but (like the preceding meaning) usually
<div n="lb">with an implied {%negative%} bearing: erroneous conception or
<div n="lb">belief, imagination, fancy; e. g. {%Nyāya Sūtras:%} {#स्फटिकान्य-#}
<div n="lb">{#त्वाभिमानवत्तदन्यत्वाभिमानः#} ‘as crystal is {%fancied%} to be-
<div n="lb">come different (from the proximity with objects it comes
<div n="lb">in contact with) so the mental activity is {%fancied%} to vary’;
<div n="lb">or {#स्वप्नविषयाभिमानवदयं प्रमाणप्रमेयाभिमानः#} ‘like the
<div n="lb">{%conceit%} of things in a dream, is the {%conceit%} of means of
<div n="lb">proof and objects of proof’; or {#मिथ्योपलब्धिविनाशस्तत्त्व-#}
<div n="lb">{#ज्ञानात्स्वप्नविषयाभिमानप्रणाशवत्प्रतिबोधे#} ‘false conception
<div n="lb">ceases through the knowledge of the Truth as the {%imagin-%}
<div n="lb">{%ation%} of objects in a dream vanishes on awaking’; or {%Bho-%}
<div n="lb">{%jadeva%} (on the {%Yoga S.%} {#विवेकख्याति°°#}): {#प्रतिपक्षभावनाब-#}
<div n="lb">{#लादविद्याप्रविलये निवृत्तकर्तृत्वज्ञातृत्वाभिमानाया रजस्तमो-#}
<div n="lb">{#मलानभिभूताया बुद्धेरन्तर्मुखाया या चिच्छायासंक्रान्तिः सा#}
<div n="lb">{#विवेकख्यातिरित्युच्यते#} ‘…of the intellect in which the con-

<div n="lb">ceit of being agent or knower has ceased…’; or {%Sā-%}
<div n="lb">{%hityad.:%} {#उत्साहादिसमुद्बोधः साधारण्याभिमानतः । नृणामपि#}
