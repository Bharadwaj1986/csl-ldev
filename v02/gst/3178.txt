{#अनुभाषण#}¦ Tatpur. n. ({#-णम्#}) (In the Nyāya phil.) Repeating
an argument proposed for discussion, i. e. taking it up;
the contrary (not-repeating it when it has been announced
three times besides the first time) being considered as a
tacit agreement or avowal of defeat. See {#अननुभाषण#}. E.
{#भाष्#} with {#अनु,#} kṛt aff. {#ल्युट्#}.
