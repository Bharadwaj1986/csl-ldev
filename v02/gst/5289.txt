<L>5289<pc>203-a<k1>अपेक्षिन्<k2>अपेक्षिन्
{#अपेक्षिन्#}¦ Tatpur. m. f. n. ({#-क्षी-क्षिणी-क्षि#}) <sup>1</sup> Looking to, ex-
<div n="lb">pecting, hoping. <sup>2</sup> Considering, respecting. <sup>3</sup> Referring to
<div n="lb">(in the latter sense mostly used in scientific writings); e. g.
<div n="lb">in a comm. on the {%Sāṅkhyakār.%} {#अन्योन्याश्रयो न दोषाय त-#}
<div n="lb">{#त्तज्जातीयापेक्षित्वेऽपि तत्तद्व्यक्तीनां परस्परानपेक्षित्वात्;#} or
<div n="lb">in a {%Yoga Sūtra:%} {#तदुपरागापेक्षित्वाच्चित्तस्य वस्तु ज्ञाताज्ञा-#}
<div n="lb">{#तम्;#} or in a comm. on the {%Nyāya S.%} {#स्वस्य स्वापेक्षित्वेऽनि-#}
<div n="lb">{#ष्टप्रसङ्गः#} (see s. v. {#आत्माश्रय#}); also used in contradistinction
<div n="lb">from ‘what is referred to’, e. g. {#तदपेक्षापेक्षित्वनिबन्धनो#}
<div n="lb">{#ऽनिष्टप्रसङ्गः#} ‘an incongruous connexion founded on the
<div n="lb">mutual (imaginary, but not real) relation of the object
<div n="lb">which has the reference, i. e. which is referred to, and the
<div n="lb">object referring to (when {#तदपेक्षापेक्षिन्#} is equal to {#तदपेक्ष-#}
<div n="lb">{#तदपेक्षिन्;#} comp. s. v. {#अन्योन्याश्रय#}); or {#तदपेक्षापेक्ष्यपेक्षित्व-#}
<div n="lb">{#निबन्धनोऽनिष्टप्रसङ्गः#} ‘an incongruous connexion founded
<div n="lb">on the relation between an object referred to, another re-
<div n="lb">ferring to this and another referring to the latter; (comp.
<div n="lb">s. v. {#चक्रक#}). In all these instances the affix {#त्व#} does not be-
<div n="lb">long to {#अपेक्षिन्#} but to the whole compound of which this
<div n="lb">word is the latter part; i. e. {#तत्तज्जातीयापेक्षिन्, परस्परा-#}
<div n="lb">{#नपेक्षिन्, तदुपरागापेक्षिन्, स्वापेक्षिन्, तदपेक्षापेक्षिन्#} &c.,
<div n="lb">taddh. aff. {#त्व#}.--E. {#ईक्ष्#} with {#अप,#} kṛt aff. {#णिनि#}.
