{#अनुकल्प#}¦ Tatpur. m. ({#-ल्पः#}) A secondary or succedaneous in-
junction: an injunction of what is to be done, if the ori-
ginal injunction cannot be acted upon; f. i. to make offer-
ings with nīvāra, if vrīhi--the dravya originally required
--cannot be had; but used also in a more general sense
with regard to other duties. E. {#अनु#} and {#कल्प#}.
