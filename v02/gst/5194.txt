{#अपिशर्वर#}¦ Tatpur. (ved.) 1. m. f. n. ({#-रः-रा-रम्#}) Near the
night, connected with the time near the night.
<div n="P">2. m. ({#-रः#}) <sup>1</sup> The beginning of the night. ({%Sāyaṇa:%}
{#रात्रीमुखम्;#} thus explained by him in the {%Ṛgv.%} verse {#त्वां#}
{#यदग्ने पशवः समासते समिद्धमपिशर्वरे#}.) <sup>2</sup> The end of the
night ({%Sāyaṇa:%} {#गतरात्रकालः;#} thus explained by him in the
{%Ṛgv.%} verse {#मम प्रपित्वे अपिशर्वरे#} &c. where it is opposed
to the time of sunrise, midday and evening.) The word
occurs only in the locative sing. but is probably a masc.
like the Tatpur. {#पूर्वरात्र, मध्यरात्र#} &c. with the ellipsis of
{#काल#}. E. {#अपि#} and {#शर्वरी,#} probably with samās. aff. {#अच्#}.
