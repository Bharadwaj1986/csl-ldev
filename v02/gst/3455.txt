<L>3455<pc>107-b<k1>अनुसन्धान<k2>अनुसन्धान
{#अनुसन्धान#}¦ Tatpur. n. ({#-नम्#}) <sup>1</sup> Congruous or suitable con-
nexion; e. g. {#यथा वाक्यद्वयानुसंधानसंपन्नं प्रकरणं पृथक्प्रमा-#}
{#णम् । तथा प्रकरणद्वयानुसंधानसंपन्नः क्रमः कुतो न मानं स्यात्#}.
<sup>2</sup> (In the Vaiśeṣika phil.) the same as {#उपनय#} of the Nyāya
phil.; viz. in a syllogism (see {#न्याय#}) which according to the
native phil. consists of five parts, the fourth part or the appli-
cation to the terminus major (i. e. the congruous connexion
of the application with the latter); e. g. in a syllogism ‘<sup>a.</sup> a
word is perishable (the {#प्रतिज्ञा#}) <sup>b.</sup> because it is produced
artificially (the {#अपदेश#}) <sup>c.</sup> all that is produced artificially is
perishable (the {#निदर्शन#}) <sup>d.</sup> now, the word has the proper-
ties of what is artificially produced <sup>e.</sup> therefore it is perishable
(the {#प्रत्याम्नाय#} or {#निगमन#})--’ <sup>d.</sup> is the {#अनुसन्धान#} or in the
Nyāya the {#उपनय#}. <sup>3</sup> Inquiry, investigation, searching. <sup>4</sup> Close
or narrow inspection. E. {#धा#} with {#सम्#} and {#अनु,#} kṛt aff. {#ल्युट्#}.
