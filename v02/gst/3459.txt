{#अनुसमय#}¦ Tatpur. m. ({#-यः#}) Necessary association, a connexion
which is the result of reasoning; e. g. {#इत्येवं पदार्थानुसमयो#}
{#ऽभ्युपेतव्यः । न तु काण्डानुसमयः#} ‘thus the connexion (be-
tween the subjects of discussion) results from the bearing
of the words, not from the circumstance that they are named
in the same book’. E. {#अनु#} and {#समय#}.
