<div n="P">2. m. ({#-न्तकः#}) <sup>1</sup> A name of Yama, the god of Death.

<sup>3</sup> The proper name of a king of the Śuṅga dynasty, a son
of Vasumitra. (Some read the latter name {#अन्द्रक#} or {#आर्द्रक#}).

<div n="P">II. m. ({#-कः#}) The same as {#अन्त#}. E. {#अन्त,#} taddh. aff. {#कन्#}.
