{#अपावृत्#}¦ Tatpur. f. ({#-त्#}) (ved.) Returning; {#अनपावृत्#} ‘so as
not to return again’; said of the waters which go to the
ocean. E. {#वृत्#} with {#अप,#} kṛt aff. {#क्विप्,#} the final vowel of

the {%Prātisākhya%} of the {%Ṛgveda.%}
