{#अप्रमोदमान#}¦ Tatpur. n. ({#-नम्#}) (In the Sāṅkhya philosophy.)
<div n="lb">The reverse of {#प्रमोदमान#} q. v., a technical term to denote
<div n="lb">the incapacity of removing the pains that are produced by
<div n="lb">superhuman, i. e. divine or atmospheric influences (cold,
<div n="lb">heat, wind, rain, lightning &c.), by not attending for this
<div n="lb">purpose a holy teacher (the removal of such pains being
<div n="lb">required for the attainment of final liberation); it is amongst
<div n="lb">the seventeen {#बुद्धिबध#} or obstructions of intellect one of
<div n="lb">the eight which are the converse of the {#सिद्धि#} or perfections.
<div n="lb">Comp. {#अप्रमुदित#} and {#अप्रमोद#}. E. {#अ#} neg. and {#अप्रमोदमान#}.
