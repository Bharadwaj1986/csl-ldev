{#अतिकृति#}¦ Tatpur. f. ({#-तिः#}) A class of metres regulated by
number and quantity. See {#अतिच्छन्दस्#}. It comprises two
species (see {#क्रौञ्चपदा#} and {#शम्भु#}) and consists of a stanza
of four lines, with twenty-five syllables in each line.
Also called {#अभिकृति#}. E. {#अति#} (sc. {#क्रान्ता#}) and {#कृति#} (an-
other class of metres; in the sense of the accusative).
