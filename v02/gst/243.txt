<div n="P">II. Bahuvr. Such food as may be eaten during a season
considered impure or improper for the performance of re-
ligious duties, as a season of mourning &c. (See {#अशौच#}.)

barley, sesamum, and sea or river salt. The word occurs
also written {#अक्षारालवण#}. E. {#अ#} priv. and {#क्षारलवण#} (Dvan-
dva), sc. {#अन्न#}.
