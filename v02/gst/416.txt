{#अग्निमारुति#}¦ Karmadh. m. ({#-तिः#}) A name of the saint Agastya.
<div n="lb">E. {#अग्नि#} fire, i. e. the fire of the stomach, the digestive fa-
<div n="lb">culty, and {#मारुति#} a name of Hanumat; because the saint
<div n="lb">could digest as well as the monkey: the epithet alludes to
<div n="lb">a story of his devouring and digesting Vātāpi, a demon,
<div n="lb">who had assumed the form of a sheep.
