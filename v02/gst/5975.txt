{#अभिजन#}¦ Tatpur. m. ({#-नः#}) <sup>1</sup> Native country; e. g. {%Bhaṭṭik.:%}
<div n="lb">{#ततः प्रगदिता वाक्यं मिथिलाभिजना नृपम्#}. <sup>2</sup> The country
<div n="lb">of the ancestors; (this is the sense given to the word by
<div n="lb">{%Patanjali%} to {%Pāṇ.%} IV. 3. 90. in distinction from {#निवास,#} IV.
<div n="lb">3. 89., which would mean according to him the country where
<div n="lb">one lives oneself: {#अथ निवासाभिजनयोः को विशेषः । नि-#}
<div n="lb">{#वासो नाम यत्र संप्रत्युष्यते । अभिजनो नाम यत्र पूर्वैरुषितम्#}).
<div n="lb"><sup>3</sup> The ancestors; (according to {%Kaiyyaṭa%}--hence {%Kāśikā%}
<div n="lb">and {%Vardhamāna%}--on the preceding quotation: {#अभिजन-#}
<div n="lb">{#शब्देन पूर्वे बान्धवा उच्यन्ते#} &c.). <sup>4</sup> Race, family in general;
<div n="lb">e. g. {%Lāṭyāyana:%} {#य एषामध्ययनेऽभिक्रान्तितमः स्यादभिजनेन#}
<div n="lb">{#वा#} ({%Agnisvāmin:%} {#अभिजनः कुलम्#}). <sup>5</sup> The head or ornament
<div n="lb">of a family. <sup>6</sup> Fame, notoriety. E. {#जन्#} with {#अभि,#} kṛt
<div n="lb">aff. {#घञ्#} (according to the commentators of the {%Amarak.;%}
<div n="lb">kṛt aff. {#अच्,#} according to {%Bharatas.%} on the {%Bhaṭṭik.%}).
