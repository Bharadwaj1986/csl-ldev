<L>5975<pc>241-a<k1>अभिजन<k2>अभिजन
{#अभिजन#}¦ Tatpur. m. ({#-नः#}) <sup>1</sup> Native country; e. g. {%Bhaṭṭik.:%}
{#ततः प्रगदिता वाक्यं मिथिलाभिजना नृपम्#}. <sup>2</sup> The country
of the ancestors; (this is the sense given to the word by
{%Patanjali%} to {%Pāṇ.%} IV. 3. 90. in distinction from {#निवास,#} IV.
3. 89., which would mean according to him the country where
one lives oneself: {#अथ निवासाभिजनयोः को विशेषः । नि-#}
{#वासो नाम यत्र संप्रत्युष्यते । अभिजनो नाम यत्र पूर्वैरुषितम्#}).
<sup>3</sup> The ancestors; (according to {%Kaiyyaṭa%}--hence {%Kāśikā%}
and {%Vardhamāna%}--on the preceding quotation: {#अभिजन-#}
{#शब्देन पूर्वे बान्धवा उच्यन्ते#} &c.). <sup>4</sup> Race, family in general;
e. g. {%Lāṭyāyana:%} {#य एषामध्ययनेऽभिक्रान्तितमः स्यादभिजनेन#}
{#वा#} ({%Agnisvāmin:%} {#अभिजनः कुलम्#}). <sup>5</sup> The head or ornament
of a family. <sup>6</sup> Fame, notoriety. E. {#जन्#} with {#अभि,#} kṛt
aff. {#घञ्#} (according to the commentators of the {%Amarak.;%}
kṛt aff. {#अच्,#} according to {%Bharatas.%} on the {%Bhaṭṭik.%}).
