{#अतिसान्तपन#}¦ Tatpur. n. ({#-नम्#}) Severe penance or expiation,
especially for the guilt of eating unclean animals; taking
as food nothing but cow's urine, cowdung, curds, milk,
and ghee, each two days in succession. See {#सान्तपन#} and
{#महासान्तपन#}. E. {#अति#} and {#सान्तपन#}.
