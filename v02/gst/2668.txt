{#अनित्य#}¦ Tatpur. m. f. n. ({#-त्यः-त्या-त्यम्#}) <sup>1</sup> Transient, not ever-
lasting (as the body, or in the Sāṅkhya philosophy the
discrete principles mahat, ahaṅkāra &c. or in the Nyāya
the sound &c. <sup>2</sup> Occasional, not peremptory, not obligatory
as a rule &c. (see also {#काम्य;#} as in the Jyotiṣṭoma the six
samsthās: {#अत्यग्निष्टोम, उक्थ्य, षोडशिन्, अतिरात्र, अप्तोर्याम#}
and {#वाजपेय#} &c.). <sup>3</sup> Not necessary, not immutable. See
{#अनित्यसमास#}. <sup>4</sup> Unstable, inconstant. <sup>5</sup> Unusual, irregular.
({#अनित्यम्#} not always.) E. {#अ#} neg. and {#नित्य#}.
