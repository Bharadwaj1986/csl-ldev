I. {#अपूर्व#}¦ 1. Bahuvr. 1. m. f. n. ({#-र्वः-र्वा-र्वम्#}) <sup>1</sup> Not preceded by (scil.
<div n="lb">any thing), as {#ब्रह्मन्#} n., e. g. {#तदेतद्ब्रह्मापूर्वमनपरमनन्तरमवा-#}
<div n="lb">{#ह्यम्;#} (comp. also {#अपूर्वता#} 4.); or as {#मनस्,#} it being the first of
<div n="lb">the organs ({#इन्द्रिय#}), in the {%Vājasan.%} verse: {#यदपूर्वं यक्षमन्तः#}
<div n="lb">{#प्रजानां तन्मे मनः शिवसंकल्पमस्तु;#} or as a word (not pre-
[Page198-b+ 56]
<div n="lb">ceded by any other word), e. g. in {%Pāṇini'%}s {%Sūtra%} {#जात्व-#}
<div n="lb">{#पूर्वम्#} (comp. also the {%Vārtt.%} 5. to VIII. 1. 72. and the {%Kāśikā%}
<div n="lb">to VIII. 1. 49. (50.)). <sup>2</sup> Not having existed before; e. g. in the
<div n="lb">{%Anuśāsana Parvan:%} {#अपूर्वं च भवेत्पात्रमथवापि चिरोषितः#} &c.
<div n="lb">({%Nīlakanṭha:%} {#अपूर्वः कदाचिन्नागतो न दृष्टश्च#}); new (also
<div n="lb">applied to a religious act which does not follow as a con-
<div n="lb">sequence of other acts, but requires a special injunction);
<div n="lb">e. g. in the {%Vedānta Sūtra:%} {#कार्याख्यानादपूर्वम्#} ({%Śaṅkara:%}
<div n="lb">{#अपूर्वत्वाद्विध्यर्हम्;#} {%Anūpanār.:%} {#अपां प्राणाच्छादकत्वध्यानमे-#}
<div n="lb">{#वापूर्वं विधेयम्#} &c.); compare also the meaning 2. 1. a. b.
<div n="lb"><sup>3</sup> Unprecedented, extraordinary, wonderful; e. g. {#अयं त्व-#}
<div n="lb">{#पूर्वः प्रतिमाविशेषो यः सेव्यमानो रिपुतामुपैति;#} or {#वनमिद-#}
<div n="lb">{#मपूर्वसत्त्वाधिष्ठितम्#} &c.; or {#सर्वशुभकर्महेतोः फलमिदं शृणु-#}
<div n="lb">{#तास्य कर्मस्य । तस्माद्भवत कृतज्ञा अपूर्वशुभसंचयं क्षपित्वेह;#} or
<div n="lb">{#अन्तर्गूढविषो वहिर्मधुमयश्चातीव मायापटुः । को नामायमपूर्व-#}
<div n="lb">{#नाटकविधिर्यः शिक्षितो दुर्जनैः ।#}. <sup>4</sup> Not having had before
<div n="lb">(scil. a husband); used so in {%Pāṇini'%}s {%Sūtra:%} {#कौमारापूर्ववचने#}.
<div n="P">2. n. ({#-र्वम्#}) <sup>1</sup> (In the Mīmānsā philosophy.) <sup>a.</sup> The
<div n="lb">{%unseen or transcendental power%} (<lang n="greek">δυναμις</lang>, = {#अदृष्टपूर्वम्#} or
<div n="lb">{#अत्यन्तमदृष्टम्#} liter. which has not been seen before or
<div n="lb">which is very invisible) of a {%religious or sacrificial%} act which
<div n="lb">produces the desired result at another than the present or
<div n="lb">at a distant time ({%Kumārila:%} {#फलाय विहितं कर्म क्षणिकं चि-#}
<div n="lb">{#रभाविने । तत्सिद्धिर्नान्यथेत्येवमपूर्वमपि गम्यते;#} {%Mādhava:%}
<div n="lb">{#धात्वर्थातिरिक्तं कालान्तरभावि काम्यफलसाधनम्;#} {%the same:%}
<div n="lb">{#यागजन्या काचिच्छक्तिरपूर्वमस्तु#}); if a religious act includes
<div n="lb">one or several other acts, the unseen power which results
<div n="lb">from the performance of the latter and which consists in
<div n="lb">producing their relation with the former, is called {#अवान्त-#}
<div n="lb">{#रापूर्व,#} while the unseen power of the superior act is called
<div n="lb">with regard to the inferior acts {#परमापूर्व#} or {#मुख्यापूर्व#} or
<div n="lb">{#प्रकृतापूर्व#} or {#प्रधानापूर्व#}. That unseen power which is only
<div n="lb">the result of performing the principal sacrifices, such as
<div n="lb">the Jyotiṣṭoma or the Darśapūrṇamāsa, and which con-
<div n="lb">sists in producing {%the%} result, i. e. {%swarga%} or the enjoyment
<div n="lb">of heaven, is called {#फलापूर्व#}. Since such acts include other
<div n="lb">acts which vary in degree or importance, there are various
<div n="lb">degrees of the {#अपूर्व#} with regard to the {#फलापूर्व,#} viz. the
<div n="lb">{#समुदायापूर्व,#} the {#उत्पत्त्यपूर्व#} and the {#अङ्गापूर्व#} (the latter also
<div n="lb">called {#कलापूर्व#}); thus, as the Darśapūrṇamāsa, the perfor-
<div n="lb">mance of which has the power of producing heaven, i. e.
<div n="lb">the {#फलापूर्व,#} consists of the two distinct sacrifices Darśa
<div n="lb">and Pūrṇamāsa which are performed at distinct times, it
<div n="lb">requires an unseen power to effectuate their combining so
<div n="lb">as to constitute the sacrifice Darśapūrnamāsa, (for other-
<div n="lb">wise neither of both would have a relation to the other or
<div n="lb">to that sacrifice itself); this unseen power is the {#समुदाया-#}
<div n="lb">{#पूर्व;#} likewise the {#उत्पत्त्यपूर्व#} causes the six Yāgas ({#आग्नेय-#}
<div n="lb">{#पुरोडाशयागः, ऐन्द्रपयोयागः, ऐन्द्रदधियागः#} which con-
<div n="lb">stitute the Darśa, and the {#आग्नेयपुरोडाशयागः, आज्येनो-#}
<div n="lb">{#पांशुयाजयागः, अग्नीषोमीयपुरोडाशयागः#} which constitute
<div n="lb">the Pūrṇamāsa, and are performed, too, at different
<div n="lb">times,) to combine so as to become severally the Darśa
<div n="lb">and the Pūrṇamāsa; and lastly, as each of these six
<div n="lb">Yāgas comprises again a number of inferior acts, such
<div n="lb">as the beating of the {%vrīhi,%} the grinding of the {%taṇḍula,%}
[Page199-a+ 56]
<div n="lb">the cleansing of the ladles &c., which also are done at
<div n="lb">different times, it requires an {#अङ्गापूर्व#} (or {#कलापूर्व#}) to
<div n="lb">produce the result of their constituting severally each of
<div n="lb">these Yāgas.--Such an unseen power might be predi-
<div n="lb">cated, indeed, of any act, whether sacrificial or not,
<div n="lb">but the term {#अपूर्व#} is expressly reserved by the Mīmān-
<div n="lb">sists to the former ({%Kumārila:%} {#लौकिकं चापि यत्कर्म फले#}
<div n="lb">{#कालान्तरोद्गतौ । तत्रापि शक्तिरेवास्ते न त्वपूर्वगिरोच्यते#}), for
<div n="lb">the corresponding power of a non-sacrificial or profane act is
<div n="lb">called {#संस्कार#}. ({%Kumārila:%} {#यान्यपि च लौकिकानि कृषिघृ-#}
<div n="lb">{#तपानाद्ध्ययनप्रभृतीनि कर्माणि कालान्तरफलत्वेनेष्यन्ते तेषामपि#}
<div n="lb">{#स्वरूपावस्थानासंभवात्संस्कारैरेव तिष्ठद्भिर्व्यवहारसिद्धः । ते#}
<div n="lb">{#त्ववैदिकत्वात्संस्कारा न त्वपूर्वशब्दाभिधेयत्वेन प्रसिद्धाः;#} comp.
<div n="lb">also e. g. {%Mādhava%} in the Pūrvapakṣa of an Adhikaraṇa:
<div n="lb">{#योऽयं स्विष्टकृद्यागः सोऽयमुपयुक्तहविःसंस्कार इत्यविवादम् ।#}
<div n="lb">{#तत्र संस्कारस्य दृष्टप्रयोजनत्वेनावशयंभावे सति तावतैवोपक्षी-#}
<div n="lb">{#णः स्विष्टकृद्यागो नापूर्वस्योपकरोति#} &c.) A doubt may arise,
<div n="lb">whether the beating of rice and such like acts have an
<div n="lb">{#अपूर्व,#} if considered absolutely or by themselves, since their
<div n="lb">result is the unhusking of the rice and since they are per-
<div n="lb">formed for sacrificial purposes, but the Mīmānsists decide
<div n="lb">the question in the negative, as it is the characteristic
<div n="lb">property of the {#अपूर्व#} to be an {%unseen%} power, and the un-
<div n="lb">husking of rice is a {%visible%} result ({%Mādhava:%} {#दृष्टफले संभ-#}
<div n="lb">{#वत्यपूर्वं न कल्पनीयम्#}); again whether the cleaning of the
<div n="lb">sacrificial ladles with kuśa grass and such like acts have an
<div n="lb">{#अपूर्व,#} for their result might be said not to be visible; but the
<div n="lb">solution is the same although from a different reason; viz. be-
<div n="lb">cause they are undertaken for the sake of the {#द्रव्य,#} i. e. these
<div n="lb">inferior acts have an {#अपूर्व#} only in so far as it concerns
<div n="lb">their relation to the superior acts, but not in so far as the
<div n="lb">mere material result (of unhusking, the purity of the ves-
<div n="lb">sels &c.) is concerned; ({%Jaimini:%} {#तानि#} [scil. {#कर्माणि#}] {#द्वैधं#}
<div n="lb">{#गुणप्रधानभूतानि । यैर्द्रव्यं न चिकीर्ष्यते तानि प्रधानभूतानि#}
<div n="lb">{#द्रव्यस्य गुणभूतत्वात्#}--such as the Prayājas, when the con-
<div n="lb">sideration of the {#द्रव्य#} is subordinate to the superior object
<div n="lb">of the sacrifice--{#। यैस्तु द्रव्यं चिकीर्ष्यते गुणस्तत्र प्रतीयेत द्र-#}
<div n="lb">{#व्यप्रधानत्वात्#}--as would be the case with the acts named
<div n="lb">before or with the paring of a sacrificial post, when the
<div n="lb">act is merely done for the sake of preparing a post &c.).
<div n="lb">As the {#अपूर्व#} produces {%the%} result (swarga), it follows more-
<div n="lb">over, that it is not the divinity of the sacrifice who pro-
<div n="lb">duces the result or influences the peculiar nature of the
<div n="lb">sacrifice, the latter being the consequence of the {#अपूर्व#} it-
<div n="lb">self, ({%Mādhava:%} {#किं देवतायाः फलप्रदत्वलक्षणं प्राधान्यं श-#}
<div n="lb">{#ब्दादापाद्यते । वस्तुसामर्थ्याद्वा । नाद्यः#} &c. {#…॥ नापि वस्तु-#}
<div n="lb">{#सामर्थ्याद्देवस्य फलप्रदत्वम् ।…तस्मात्फलप्रदमपूर्वमेव#}
<div n="lb">{#धर्माणां प्रयोजकम् । तथा सत्यग्न्यादिदेवाभावेऽप्यपूर्वप्रयुक्त-#}
<div n="lb">{#धर्माणामतिदेशादस्ति तत्रोहस्यावकाशः#}); and that the sacri-
<div n="lb">fice itself is not the chief object, since it, too, has not the
<div n="lb">(immediate) power of producing the result ({%Mādhava:%} {#अ-#}
<div n="lb">{#पूर्वं मुख्यम् । फलवत्त्वात् । यजिर्न मुख्यः । फलरहितत्वात्#}).--
<div n="lb">A discussion (interesting on account of the questions of
<div n="lb">philosophy of grammar implied by it) is raised by the Mī-
<div n="lb">mānsā writers, viz. whether many words or a single word
<div n="lb">of a sentence and, again, in the latter case which word
[Page199-b+ 56]
<div n="lb">and which part of such a word will convey the notion of
<div n="lb">the {#अपूर्व#}. The question is decided in this way: in vaidik
<div n="lb">passages, enjoining an act which possesses the unseen
<div n="lb">power, such as {#सोमेन यजेत,#} or {#हिरण्यमात्रेयाय ददाति,#}
<div n="lb">or {#तस्मात्सुवर्णं हिरण्यं भार्यम्,#} or {#श्येनेनाभिचरन्यजेत,#} or
<div n="lb">{#चित्रया यजेत पशुकामः,#} not all the words of the sentence
<div n="lb">collectively convey the {#अपूर्व,#} nor a single word expressing
<div n="lb">the substance ({#द्रव्य#}) of the sacrificial act, as {#सोम#} and {#हि-#}
<div n="lb">{#रण्य,#} nor the word expressing a quality ({#गुण#}), as {#सुवर्ण,#}
<div n="lb">nor the word expressing the name ({#कर्मवाचि#}) of the sacri-
<div n="lb">fice, as {#श्येन#} and {#चित्रा,#} but the {%inflected verb%} or the word
<div n="lb">representing it, as {#यजेत, ददाति, भार्यम्#}. Again, in the
<div n="lb">verb the {#अपूर्व#} is not conveyed by the meaning of the radical
<div n="lb">but by that part of it which implies the {%verbal notion%} ({#भा-#}
<div n="lb">{#वना#}), lastly, so far as the {%implicit%} understanding of the
<div n="lb">verbal notion ({#अर्थभावना#} or {#अर्थात्मभावना#}) is concerned,
<div n="lb">any inflection-affix added to the radical might convey it,
<div n="lb">provided the connex of the sentence leaves no doubt, as
<div n="lb">when the word {#-काम#} (e. g. {#स्वर्गकामः#}) is added; but an
<div n="lb">{%explicit%} understanding of it ({#शब्दभावना#} or {#अभिधाभावना#})
<div n="lb">is only imparted by the inflection-affixes of the {%potential,%}
<div n="lb">{%leṭ, the imperative%} and by the affixes of the {%participles of the%}
<div n="lb">{%passive future.%} ({%Mādhava:%} {#लिङ्लेड्लोट्तव्यप्रत्ययमात्रगता श-#}
<div n="lb">{#ब्दभावना । सर्वाख्यातगतार्थभावना । तदुक्तम् । अभिधाभाव-#}
<div n="lb">{#नामाहुरन्यामेव लिङ्ङादयः । अर्थात्मभावना त्वन्या सर्वाख्या-#}
<div n="lb">{#तेषु गम्यत इति ।;#} and: {#सेयं शब्दभावना लिङ्ङादिभिरेव ग-#}
<div n="lb">{#म्यते । अर्थभावना सर्वाख्यातप्रत्ययैर्गम्यत इत्युक्तम् । तस्यां चार्थ-#}
<div n="lb">{#भावनायां स्वर्गस्य भाव्यत्वं कमियोगादवगम्यते;#} in referring to
<div n="lb">{%Kumārila:%} {#…वैदिकलिङ्ङादीनां तदभिधायकत्वात्#} (for this
<div n="lb">reason {#अपूर्व#} is authoritative) {#। ततो धात्वर्थातिरिक्तं कालान्त-#}
<div n="lb">{#रभावि#} &c., see above, p. 198{%b,%} 1. 25).--As the unseen power
<div n="lb">is the consequence of the performance of a sacrificial act which
<div n="lb">‘has not existed before’ i. e. which is absolute ({%Mādhava:%} {#कथं#}
<div n="lb">{#विनश्वरेण फलं कर्तव्यमित्यपेक्षायामपूर्वं कृत्वेत्युच्यते । कथमपूर्वं#}
<div n="lb">{#क्रियत इत्यपेक्षायां यागानुष्ठानप्रकारेणेति#}) the term {#अपूर्व#}
<div n="lb">assumes also the meaning of such a sacrificial act, and as
<div n="lb">the latter requires always an authoritative injunction or
<div n="lb">{#विधि,#} since it would not take place otherwise, {#अपूर्व#} occurs
<div n="lb">also in the sense of such an authoritative injunction, viz.
<div n="lb"><sup>b.</sup> a {%sacrificial act,%} especially a principal act, one not sub-
<div n="lb">ordinate to another act and therefore possessing the un-
<div n="lb">seen power of producing {%the%} result (i. e. heaven); e. g. in
<div n="lb">{%Jaimini'%}s {%Sūtra:%} {#अपूर्वे च विकल्पः स्याद्यदि संख्याभिधानम्,#}
<div n="lb">{%Śabara:%} {#अपूर्वे च दर्शपूर्णमासकर्मणि#} &c.; or in {%Jaimini'%}s
<div n="lb">{%Sūtra:%} {#अपूर्वे वापि भागित्वात्,#} {%Śabara:%} {#अपि वा अपूर्वे एते#}
<div n="lb">{#उभे#} (viz. {#अग्निहोत्र#} and {#मासाग्निहोत्र#}) {#अपि कर्मणी स्याताम्;#}
<div n="lb">or in {%Jaimini'%}s {%Sūtra:%} {#अपूर्वे त्वविकारोपदेशात्प्रतीयेत,#} {%Śa-%}
<div n="lb">{%bara:%} {#अपूर्वे त्वप्रकृतिपूर्वके कर्मणि#} (i. e. in an act not sub-
<div n="lb">ordinate to a superior act); or in {%Jaimini'%}s {%Sūtra:%} {#प्रधानं#}
<div n="lb">{#त्वङ्गसंयुक्तं तथाभूतमपूर्वं स्यात्तस्य विध्युपलक्षणात्;#} or {%Mādhava%}
<div n="lb">in an Adhikaraṇa: {#वैरूप्याच्च निरर्थत्वादपूर्वं कर्म युज्यते#}.
<div n="lb"><sup>c.</sup> the authoritative or binding {%injunction%} (comp. {#विधि#})
<div n="lb">which produces the unseen power &c.; e. g. {%Kumārila%} (on
<div n="lb">the Sūtra {#चोदना पुनरारम्भः#}): {#आरम्भचोदनैवापूर्वस्यापि चो-#}
<div n="lb">{#दना,#} thus commented upon by {%Mādhava:%} {#गुरुणा यन्नियो-#}
<div n="lb">{#गाख्यमपूर्वमभिप्रेयते#} &c.; or {%Mādhava:%} {#चोदितं यदपूर्वं तस्यैव#}
[Page200-a+ 56]
<div n="lb">{#फलवत्त्वेनानुष्ठेयतयेतिकर्तव्यताकाङ्क्षा युक्ता;#} the relation be-
<div n="lb">tween {#विधि#} and {#अपूर्व#} may be seen e. g. from the follow-
<div n="lb">ing Adhikaraṇa of {%Mādhava:%} {#उपव्यानेऽनुवादो वा विधिर्वा-#}
<div n="lb">{#द्यो यतः स्मृतौ । प्राप्तं मैवमपूर्वत्वात्क्रतौ लेटा विधीयते;#} or
<div n="lb">from {%Jaimini'%}s {%Sūtra:%} {#विधिर्वा स्यादपूर्वत्वाद्वादमात्रं ह्यनर्थ-#}
<div n="lb">{#कम्;#} (where the {#अपूर्वत्व#} expresses the quality of what may
