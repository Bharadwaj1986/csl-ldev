{#अन्यतरतोदन्त#}¦ Bahuvr. m. f. n. ({#-न्तः-न्ता-न्तम्#}) Having teeth
on one side only, having but one row of teeth (as {#प्रजाः,#}
living beings); opposed to {#उभयतोदन्त#} i. e. men, horses
and other animals which have two rows of teeth; both
[Page140-b+ 56]
categories being intended in a vaidik passage to comprise
all living beings in general. E. {#अन्यतरतस्#} and {#दन्त#}.
