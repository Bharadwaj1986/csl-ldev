<L>4406<pc>152-a<k1>अन्वर्थग्रहण<k2>अन्वर्थग्रहण
{#अन्वर्थग्रहण#}¦ Tatpur. n. ({#-णम्#}) Taking or understanding a
word in its literal or etymological (not in its conventional
or technical) sense; e. g. the word {#बहुवचने#} in Pāṇini VIII.
2. 81, where it is not be taken in the technical sense ‘plural’,
but in the sense {#बहूनामर्थानां वचने,#} as otherwise the form
{#अमी#} would not result from the rule. E. {#अन्वर्थ#} (Avyayībh.)
and {#ग्रहण#}.
