{#अपवृति#}¦ Tatpur. f. ({#-तिः#}) Opening. [In the present edition
of the {%Medinī%} it occurs as a meaning of {#आच्छादन,#} viz.
{#‘आच्छादनं संपिधाने वस्त्रेऽपवृतिमात्रके’;#} but a better read-
ing instead of the latter word is clearly {#प्रावृतिमात्रके,#} as
{#आच्छादन#} does not mean ‘covering’ and ‘opening’ at the
same time; the {%Śabdamuktāmahārṇava,%} which quotes this read-
ing, explains therefore {#आच्छादनम् न#} (i. e. {#नपुंसकं#}) {#तिरोधाने#}
{#अंशुके प्रावरणसामान्ये च#}.] E. {#वृ#} with {#अप,#} kṛt aff. {#क्तिन्#}.
