{#अन्तर्गतोपमा#}¦ Karmadh. f. ({#-मा#}) A hidden comparison, one
<div n="lb">conveyed by the words used, but not expressed as such
<div n="lb">(e. g. by omitting the particle of comparison); as in the
<div n="lb">instance {#जलनिधिमीयुरतः समेत्य मायाम्#} ‘combined they
<div n="lb">went over the sea which was therefore (like a) delusion’.
<div n="lb">Compare: {#अपह्नुतिरभीष्टा च किंचिदन्तर्गतोपमा । भूतार्था-#}
<div n="lb">{#पह्नवाज्ज्ञेया क्रियतेऽस्या भिदा यथा ॥#}. (See s. v. {#अपह्नुति#}.)
<div n="lb">E. {#अन्तर्गत#} and {#उपमा#}.
