{#अनागामिन्#}¦ Tatpur. I. m. f. n. ({#-मी-मिनी-मि#}) <sup>1</sup> Not arriv-
ing, not coming. <sup>2</sup> Not future.
<div n="P">II. m. ({#-मी#}) The third of the four orders of the Bud-
dhists, generally termed Āryās; (see besides {#श्रोतआपन्न,#}
{#सकृदागामिन्#} and {#अर्हत्#}): literally, one who does not return,
but it means one who has only to pass through forty
thousand kalpas and then is freed from transmigration. E.
{#अ#} neg. and {#आगामिन्#}.
