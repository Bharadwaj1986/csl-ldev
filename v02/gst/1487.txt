{#अत्यम्लपर्णी#}¦ Bahuvr. f. ({#-र्णी#}) A creeper and medicinal plant,
‘of pungent and astringent properties, employed as a sto-

and {#पर्ण,#} with the fem. aff. {#ङीष्#}.
