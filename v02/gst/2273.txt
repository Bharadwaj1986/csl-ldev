{#अनन्तवत्#}¦ I. m. f. n. ({#-वान्-वती-वत्#}) Endowed with infinity,
eternal.
<div n="P">II. m. ({#-वान्#}) A name or epithet of one of the four pā-
das or feet of Brahman (in the symbolical language of the
Upanishads), consisting of four Kalās or parts, viz. earth,
intermediate space, heaven and ocean. The other three
feet are called {#प्रकाशवत्, ज्योतिष्मत्#} and {#आयतनवत्#}
(qq. vv.).
