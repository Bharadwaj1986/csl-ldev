{#अनेकाश्रित#}¦ Tatpur. m. f. n. ({#-तः-ता-तम्#}) Depending upon
more than one, abiding in more than one; according to the
Vaiśeṣika phil. such dependance is the characteristic of
the notion of conjunction ({#संयोग#}), of disjunction ({#विभाग#}),
of separatedness prevailing between two or more things
({#द्विपृथक्त्वादि#}) and of the numerals from two upwards. See
the preceding. E. {#अनेक#} and {#आश्रित#}.
