{#अग्नीध्र#}¦ Tatpur. I. m. ({#-ध्रः#}) <sup>1</sup> A proper name: the son of
Priyavrata and Kāmyā, a king of Jambudvīpa; one of the
ten sons of the first Manu, Svāyambhuva. <sup>2</sup> The same as
{#अग्नीध्#} q. v. In this meaning the word seems to be a
misspelling of {#आग्नीध्र#}.
<div n="P">II. f. ({#-ध्री#}) Kindling or exciting the sacrificial fire by
[Page014-a+ 56]
throwing clarified butter &c. into it. See {#आग्नीध्री#} which
seems to be the more correct form of this word. E. {#अग्नि#}
and {#इध्र#} (from {#इन्ध्,#} uṇ. aff. {#क्रन्#}).
