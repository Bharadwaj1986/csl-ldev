{#अप्रसवधर्मिन्#}¦ Tatpur. m. f. n. ({#-र्मी-र्मिणी-र्मि#}) Not having
the property of producing, not being prolific; in the {%Sāṅkhya%}
philosophy one of the characteristics of Purusha or Spirit,
in contradistinction from {#प्रसवधर्मिन्,#} the property of Matter
[Page218-a+ 56]
and its evolutions, (‘producing’ or ‘prolific’ meaning there
‘changing, becoming transformed’, as {#अहङ्कार#} is a pro-
duct, i. e. a transformation of {#बुद्धि#} &c.): {#‘प्रसवधर्मि व्यक्तं#}
{#प्रधानं च । अप्रसवधर्मी पुरुषो न हि किंचित्पुरुषात्प्रसूयते’#}.
E. {#अ#} neg. and {#प्रसवधर्मिन्#}.
