{#अपसव्य#}¦ I. Bahuvr. 1. m. f. n. ({#-व्यः-व्या-व्यम्#}) <sup>1</sup> Not left,
right; the same as {#दक्षिण।--अपसव्यं कृ,#} to keep the right
side towards one, (comp. {#प्रदक्षिणं कृ#}), or to put on the right
shoulder (scil. the sacrificial thread). <sup>2</sup> Contrary, adverse.
<div n="P">2. n. ({#-व्यम्#}) <sup>1</sup> The sacrificial thread worn over the right
shoulder and passed under the left, at Śrāddhas; comp.
{#प्राचीनावीत#}. <sup>2</sup> (In Astronomy) defined thus: {#ताराग्रहाणां#}
{#चतुर्धासंगरान्तर्गतापरं युद्धम्;#} e. g. in the {%Vasiṣṭha-Sid-%}
{%dhānta:%} {#अंशादूनेऽपसवाख्यं युद्धमेकोऽत्र चेदणु#}.
<div n="P">II. Avyayībh. ({#-व्यम्#}) The same as {#अपसलवि#} in both
meanings; comp. also the quotation under this word. E.
{#अप#} and {#सव्य#}.
