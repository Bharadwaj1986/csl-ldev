{#अपध्वंसज#}¦ Tatpur. m. ({#-जः#}) The offspring of a father inferior
<div n="lb">in caste to that of the mother, as a Sūta, the son of a
<div n="lb">Kshatriya man and a Brāhmaṇī woman &c.; the same as
<div n="lb">{#प्रतिलोमज#} q. v. Such men have not the rights of the twice-
[Page163-b+ 56]
<div n="lb">born, but the duties of a Śūdra; they do not receive the
<div n="lb">investiture with the sacred string and must follow occupa-
<div n="lb">tions which are held in disrespect by the three first castes.
