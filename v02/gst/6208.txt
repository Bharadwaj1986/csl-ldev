{#अभिमन्युपुर#}¦ Tatpur. n. ({#-रम्#}) The name of two towns in
Kashmir: the first founded by Abhimanyu, the successor
of Nāgārjuna, the second by the wife of Kshemagupta, in
commemoration of the reign of his successor Abhimanyu.
E. {#अभिमन्यु#} and {#पुर#}.
