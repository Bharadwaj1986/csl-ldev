{#अप्रायुस्#}¦ Tatpur. m. ({#-युः#}) (ved.) With ungone life, with
brilliant vigour; an epithet of Agni whose life or brilliancy
does not vanish before daylight, although all other beings
appear, as it were, lifeless during night (according to
{%Sāyaṇa'%}s interpretation of {%Ṛgv.%} I. 127. 5.). E. {#अ#} neg. and
{#प्रायुस्#}.
