{#अभिभूति#}¦ Tatpur. 1. f. ({#-तिः#}) <sup>1</sup> The being overpowered, de-
feat, discomfiture; e. g. {%Kiratārj.:%} {#अभिभूतिभयादसूनतः सु-#}
{#खमुज्झन्ति न धाम मानिनः#}. <sup>2</sup> Overpowering, defeating,
victory; in the Veda also used as epithet of a masc., e. g.
{%Ṛgv.:%} {#युवं श्वेतं पेदवे…अदत्तमश्वम् । जोहुत्रमर्यो अभि-#}
{#भूतिम्#} ‘the horse which is victory (i. e. victorious) over
the enemy’; or {%Ṛgv.%} or {%Atharv.:%} {#अतः संगृभ्याभिभूत आ भर#}
‘(Indra) who is victory i. e. victorious (over the enemies)’.
Comp. the use of {#अभिक्षदा;#} but it is possible, too, to refer
the latter instances to {#अभिभूति#} 2. <sup>3</sup> Disrespect, humiliation.
