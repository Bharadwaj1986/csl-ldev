<L>4532<pc>159-a<k1>अपघन<k2>अपघन
{#अपघन#}¦ Tatpur. m. ({#-नः#}) Any limb or member of the body,
<div n="lb">as hands, feet &c. (but not the whole body, as the {%Kāśikā%}
<div n="lb">explicitly observes on Pāṇ. III. 3. 81.: {#अपघनोऽङ्गावयवै-#}
<div n="lb">{#कदेशो न सर्वः । किं तर्हि पाणिः पादश्चाभिधीयते;#} hence also
<div n="lb">the commentators on the {%Amarak.%} explain the word either
<div n="lb">{#हस्ताद्यवयव#} or {#हस्तपादाद्यवयव#} or {#हस्तपादाद्यङ्ग#} &c. Yet
<div n="lb">{%Bharatasena%} on the Bhaṭṭik. 7. 62. not merely renders {#ललि-#}
<div n="lb">{#तापघनां = मनोहरशरीरां (स्त्रियम्),#} (equally so the {%Vaija-%}
<div n="lb">{%yantī%} and {%Kalāpapradīpa;%} the {%Bhaṭṭichandrikā%} = {#सुन्दरक-#}
<div n="lb">{#लेवरीम्#}), but quotes the opinion of another: {#अपहन्यते#}
<div n="lb">{#ऽनेनेत्यपघनो देहः,#} according to which {#अपघन#} would there-
<div n="lb">fore mean ‘body’ in general; this interpretation, however,
<div n="lb">seems to be incorrect on account of the negative bearing of
<div n="lb">the preposition {#अप;#} comp. {#संहनन#} and the words of the
<div n="lb">{%Bhāg. Pur.%} {#देहस्तु सर्वसंघातः#}). E. {#हन्#} with {#अप,#} kṛt aff. {#अप्#}.
