of a cloud, {#स्तम्भितान्तर्जलौघः#}. <sup>2</sup> The interior of the water;
comp. {#मध्येजल;#} but {#अन्तर्जले#} may be taken also as two

(where the neglect of Sandhi {#°ले उ°#} is either a misprint
in the Mitākṣara or a liberty of Parāsara who in a pre-
vious verse writes {#मयूरमेषौ हत्वा च एकभक्तेन शुद्ध्यति#} (for
it seems impossible to interpret {#अन्तर्जले#} in that passage
otherwise than as a locat. of a {%Tatpur.%} or as {#अन्तर्#} and {#जले#}).
