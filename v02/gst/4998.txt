{#अपस्पृच्#}¦ (?) Tatpur. m. f. n. ({#-क्-क्-क्#}) (ved.) (Perhaps.) Yield-
ing, giving; in the {%Ātharvav.%} 13. 1. 27: {#वि मिमीष्वव पयस्व-#}
{#तीं घृताचीं देवानां धेनुरनपस्पृगेषा#} ‘make her to have milk
and to become possessed of butter for the gods: this cow
does not yield any’. E. {#स्पृच् (= पृच्)#} with {#अप,#} or {#पृच्#}
with {#अप,#} āgāma {#सुट्,#} kṛt aff. {#क्विप्#}. [This interpretation
is hypothetical, in want of a comm. to the Atharv. The
same meaning might result in deriving {#अपस्पृक्#} from {#अपस्पृश्#}
‘sprinkling, shedding’, the radicals {#पृच्#} and {#स्पृश्#} being
connected in origin; but the former seems more natural.]
