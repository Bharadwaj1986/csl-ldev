{#अन्तरप्रभव#}¦ Tatpur. m. f. n. ({#-वः-वा-वम्#}) Born between (in
Manu 1. 2. applied to the offspring of parents of different
castes, whether the caste of the father be superior--see
{#अनुलोम#}--or inferior--see {#प्रतिलोम#}--to that of the
mother; as the word {#वर्ण#} precedes in the passage in question,
the use of the word in this sense is restricted, probably to
the verse in question). E. {#अन्तर#} and {#प्रभव#}.
