{#अनकदुन्दुभि#}¦ Bahuvr. m. ({#-भिः#}) A name of Vasudeva, the
father of Kṛṣṇa. More usually written {#आनकदुन्दुभि,#}
but given also with the short initial by some commentators
of the Amarakosha. E. {#अनक#} (instead of {#आनक#}) and {#दुन्दु-#}
{#भि#} ‘because the gods beat the Anaka-drums in his house
when Kṛṣṇa was born’.
