{#अनल#}¦ m. ({#-लः#}) <sup>1</sup> Fire. <sup>2</sup> A name of Agni or the god of fire.
<sup>3</sup> The name of one of the eight Vasus. <sup>4</sup> A name of Vasu-

digestive faculty. <sup>8</sup> Bile. <sup>9</sup> The name of several plants,
viz. <sup>a.</sup> Plumbago zeylanica (see {#चित्रक#}), <sup>b.</sup> Plumbago rosea
(see {#रक्तचित्रक#}), <sup>c.</sup> Semicarpus anacardium (see {#भल्लातक#}).
<sup>10</sup> Wind. <sup>11</sup> (In astronomy.) The name of the fiftieth year
[Page072-b+ 56]
of Bṛhaspati's cycle of sixty years. <sup>12</sup> (In astronomy.)
The name of the third lunar mansion or Kṛttikā(?). <sup>13</sup> (In
arithmetic sometimes used to denote) the numeral three; see
{#अग्नि#}(9.). E. {#अन्,#} in the causative, uṇ. aff. {#कलच्;#} ({#अनल#}
