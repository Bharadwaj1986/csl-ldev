{#अभिह्वार#}¦ Tatpur. m. ({#-रः#}) (ved. Probably.) Evil, misfor-
tune, sin; e. g. {%Atharv.:%} {#यो अस्य समिधं वेद क्षत्रियेण समा-#}
{#हिताम् । नाभिह्वारे पदं नि दधाति स मृत्यवे#} ‘he does
not put his foot into misfortune (or into sin which leads)
to death’. ({%Roth%} and {%Whitney%} in their edition of the
{%Atharv.%} write {#अभिह्वार;#} but the former gives in a quotation
of this passage in his Dictionary the form {#अभिह्वर#} which
seems less correct.) E. {#ह्वृ#} with {#अभि,#} kṛt aff. {#घञ्#}.
