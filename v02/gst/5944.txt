{#अभिघात#}¦ Tatpur. 1. m. ({#-तः#}) <sup>1</sup> Striking, beating, wounding; e. g.
<div n="lb">{%Kaṇāda S.:%} {#अभिघातान्मुसलसंयोगाद्धस्ते कर्म;#} or {%Suśruta:%} {#वि-#}
<div n="lb">{#घातादभिघाताद्वा हीनसत्त्वस्य#} &c.; or {%Bhaṭṭik.:%} {#द्विष्कुर्वतां च-#}
<div n="lb">{#तुष्कुर्वन्नभिघातं नगैर्द्विषाम् । वहिष्करिष्यन्संग्रामाद्रिपूञ्ज्व-#}
<div n="lb">{#लनपिङ्गलः#}. <sup>2</sup> Pain, infliction; e. g. in a comm. of the
<div n="lb">{%Yoga S.%} (on the word {#दौर्मनस्य#}): {#दौर्मनस्यमिच्छाभिघाता-#}
<div n="lb">{#च्चेतसः क्षोभः;#} or {%Lalitav.:%} {#मनःसंवरो धर्मलोकमुखमभिघा-#}
<div n="lb">{#तव्यापादमिथ्यादृष्टिप्रहाणाय संवर्तते#}. <sup>3</sup> Warding off, removal;
<div n="lb">e. g. in {%Gauḍapāda'%}s comm. on the {%Sāṅkhyakār.:%} {#आधिभौ-#}
<div n="lb">{#तिकस्य#} (scil. {#दुःखस्य#}) {#रक्षादिनाभिघातो दृष्टः#}. <sup>4</sup> (In the
<div n="lb">{%Vājas. Prātisākhya.%}) The vehemence or physical excite-
<div n="lb">ment which accompanies the recital of mantras at the
<div n="lb">evening oblation ({#तृतीयसवन#}), when the voice is loudest,
<div n="lb">it being low at the morning oblation ({#प्रातःसवन#}) and louder
<div n="lb">in the noon-oblation ({#माध्यन्दिनसवन#}). Compare besides
<div n="lb">the terms {#आयाम, मार्दव, विश्रम्भ, आक्षेप#}.
<div n="P">2. n. ({#-तम्#}) A harsh pronunciation (e. g. when putting
<div n="lb">a question), produced by the neglect of Sandhi rules, more
<div n="lb">esp. by combining a preceding soft {%aspirate%} sound ({#घ, झ,#}
<div n="lb">{#ढ, ध, भ#}) with a following unaspirate sound (either {#क, च,#}
<div n="lb">{#ट, त, प#} or {#ग, ज, ड, द, भ#}), or a hard aspirate sound
<div n="lb">({#ख, छ, ठ, थ, फ#}) with a following hard unaspirate sound
<div n="lb">({#क, च, ट, त, प#}), or a soft unaspirate sound ({#ग, ज, ड,#}
<div n="lb">{#द, ब#}) with a following hard aspirate sound ({#ख, छ, ठ,#}
<div n="lb">{#थ, फ#}). ({%Keralagrantha,%} as quoted by {%Rādhāk.%}) Also {#अभी-#}
<div n="lb">{#घात#}. E. {#हन्#} with {#अभि,#} kṛt aff. {#घञ्#} and {#न्#} changed to {#त्#}.
