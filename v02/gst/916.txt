{#अजिन#}¦ I. n. ({#-नम्#}) The hide of a tiger, lion &c., but more
especially of the antilope or the black species of it (see
{#अजिनयोनि#} and {#कृष्णाजिन#}) and particularly appropriated
to the religious student as a garment, seat, bed &c.
<div n="P">II. m. ({#-नः#}) The name of a descendant of Pṛthu, the
son of Havirdhāna by Dhiṣaṇā. E. {#अज्,#} uṇ. aff. {#इनच्;#}
but it would seem that I. is derived from {#अज,#} with taddh.
aff. {#इनच्#}.
