<L>4010<pc>130-a<k1>अन्त्यावसायिन्<k2>अन्त्यावसायिन्
{#अन्त्यावसायिन्#}¦ Tatpur. m. ({#-यी#}) A man of a degraded tribe,
<div n="lb">the offspring of a Niṣādī woman by a Chāṇḍāla male. Ac-
<div n="lb">cording to Angiras this term comprehends seven classes, viz.
<div n="lb">{#चाण्डाल, श्वपच, क्षत्तृ, सूत, वैदेहक, मागध#} and {#आयोगव#}
<div n="lb">qq. vv.; Vijnāneśvara applies it to Chāṇḍālas and men who
<div n="lb">have become outcastes in general: {#‘चाण्डालपतितादीनाम्’#}.
<div n="lb">Comp. {#अन्तावसायिन्#} and {#अन्त्यज#}. E. {#अन्त्य#} and {#अवसायिन्#}
<div n="lb">lit. ‘engaged in the lowest occupations’.
