{#अपपात्र#}¦ Bahuvr. m. f. n. ({#-त्रः-त्रा-त्रम्#}) Deprived of vessels.
<div n="lb">According to {%Manu,%} Chāṇḍālas and Śvapachas--the lowest
<div n="lb">tribes who are not permitted to reside within the precincts
<div n="lb">of a Brāhmanic community--must be deprived also of
<div n="lb">vessels, since no vessel used by them, as Kullūka observes,
<div n="lb">is capable of purification. E. {#अप#} and {#पात्र#}.
