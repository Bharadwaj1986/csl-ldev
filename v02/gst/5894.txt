{#अभि#}¦ ind. (see {#निपात, उपसर्ग, गति, कर्मप्रवचनीय#}) A particle
<div n="lb">implying the notion of proximity, hence of conjunction,
<div n="lb">relation, superiority, excess, in their literal and metapho-
<div n="lb">rical acceptations (and thus becoming in many instances
<div n="lb">synonymous as well with {#आ, अनु#} as with {#अधि, अपि,#}
<div n="lb">{#अति#}); it is used either as a prefix to verbs, and as such,
<div n="lb">in the Vedas and archaic passages of the classical literature,
<div n="lb">also detached from the verb (very much in the same man-
<div n="lb">ner as German prepositions when connected with verbs):
<div n="lb">or as a separable preposition: or, in the Vedas and archaic
<div n="lb">passages of the classical literature, as an adverb. With
<div n="lb">nouns it may form Tatpur., Bahuvr. and Avyayībh. com-
<div n="lb">pounds. It appears, too, in the protracted form {#अभी;#} e. g.
<div n="lb">(ved.) {#अभी षु णः,#} and comp. {#अभीक, अभीघात, अभीषङ्ग#} &c.
<div n="P">1. ({%as a prefix to verbs%} it means) <sup>1</sup> Towards, to (im-
<div n="lb">plying proximity); e. g. {#गम्#} or {#इ#} or {#या,#} with {#अभि,#} ‘to
<div n="lb">go towards, to approach’; {#क्रम्#} or {#धाव्,#} with {#अभि,#} ‘to
<div n="lb">attack’; {#वह्#} with {#अभि,#} ‘to carry towards’; {#वद्#} with {#अभि,#}
<div n="lb">‘to salute’; in many instances where the radical itself im-
<div n="lb">plies this notion, it increases merely the power of the verbal
<div n="lb">meaning and becomes often untranslateable in English; e. g.
<div n="lb">{#पीड्#} ‘to squeeze, to vex’ has the same meaning, but in an
<div n="lb">increased degree, with {#अभि, ग्रह्#} with {#अभि#} means, like
<div n="lb">{#ग्रह्,#} ‘to receive’; {#भर्त्स्#} ‘to reproach, to threaten’ has the
<div n="lb">same sense with {#अभि; हन्#} with {#अभि#} means the same as
<div n="lb">{#हन्#} &c. <sup>2</sup> Towards, for, against (implying relation, when
<div n="lb">the same remark applies as before); e. g. {#लष्#} with {#अभि,#}
<div n="lb">‘to desire, to wish for’, {#राध्#} with {#अभि,#} ‘to be favourable
<div n="lb">towards’, {#द्रुह्#} with {#अभि,#} ‘to have animosity against’;
<div n="lb">{#युज्#} with {#अभि#} (esp. pass.), ‘to bring an action against,
<div n="lb">to accuse’. <sup>3</sup> On, upon (implying contiguity and answer-
<div n="lb">ing frequently the cognate English be-); e. g. {#सु#} with {#अभि,#}
<div n="lb">‘to pour the juice of the Soma upon’, {#सिच्#} with {#अभि,#}
<div n="lb">‘to sprinkle upon, to be-sprinkle, {#जन्#} with {#अभि,#} ‘to be
<div n="lb">be-got’. <sup>4</sup> On, above, over (implying superiority or master-
<div n="lb">ship); e. g. {#भू#} or {#अस्#} (cl. 2.) with {#अभि,#} ‘to overpower’;
<div n="lb">{#अस्#} (cl. 1. or cl. 4.) with {#अभि,#} ‘to study’ (comp. {#गम्, इ#}
<div n="lb">&c. with {#अधि#}).
<div n="P">2. ({%as a separable preposition%}) with a noun following
<div n="lb">or preceding in the accusative; <sup>1</sup> Towards, to, in the di-
<div n="lb">rection of; e. g. {%Vājas.:%} {#सुप्रजाः प्रजाः प्रजनयन्परीह्यभि रा-#}
<div n="lb">{#यस्पोषेण यजमानम्;#} or {%Manu:%} {#तं चेदभ्युदियात्सूर्यः#} &c.; (comp.
<div n="lb">s. v. {#अभिनिर्मुक्त#}); or {%comm. on Pāṇ.:%} {#वृक्षमभि विद्योतते विद्युत्#}.
<div n="lb"><sup>2</sup> Towards, with regard to (implying relation); e. g. {%Ṛgv.%} or {%Vā-%}
<div n="lb">{%jas.:%} {#त्रिपादूर्ध्व उदैत्पुरुषः पादोऽस्येहाभवत्पुनः । ततो वि-#}
<div n="lb">{#ष्वङ्व्यक्रामत्साशनानशने अभि;#} or {%Siddh. K.:%} {#भक्तो हरिमभि#}.
<div n="lb"><sup>3</sup> For the sake of, on behalf of, on account of; e. g. {%Sāmav.:%}
<div n="lb">{#यत्र न पूर्वे पितरः पदज्ञा स्वर्विदो अभि गा अद्रिमिष्णन्;#} or
<div n="lb">{%Bhaṭṭik.:%} {#अभि द्योतिष्यते रामो भवन्तमचिरादिह#}. <sup>4</sup> In, on;
<div n="lb">e. g. {%Ṛgv.:%} {#सध्रीचीनेन मनसा तमिन्द्र ओजिष्ठेन हन्मनाहन्नभि#}
<div n="lb">{#द्यून्#} ‘…on certain days’; or {#यत्ते गात्रादग्निना पच्यमाना-#}
<div n="lb">{#दभि शूलं#} (on the spit) {#निहतस्यावधावति#} &c. <sup>5</sup> Over (im-
<div n="lb">plying dominion, mastership); e. g. {%Ṛgv.:%} {#स तु श्रुधि श्रुत्या#}
<div n="lb">{#यो दुवोयुर्द्यौर्न भूमाभि रायो अर्यः#}. <sup>6</sup> By (implying distri-
<div n="lb">bution), severally; e. g. {%comm. on Pāṇ.:%} {#वृक्षं वृक्षमभि सिञ्चति#}
<div n="lb">or {#देवं देवमभि सिञ्चति#}.

<div n="P">3. ({%as adverb%}) <sup>1</sup> Before, in front of, towards; e. g.
<div n="lb">{%Ṛgv.:%} or {%Atharv.:%} {#गा न व्राणा अवनीरमुचदभि श्रवो दावने#}
<div n="lb">{#सचेताः#} (scil. {#इन्द्रः#}), where {%Sāyaṇa%} explains {#अभि#} with the
<div n="lb">ellipsis {#ददाति (अभ्याभिमुख्येन ददातीति शेषः)#}. <sup>2</sup> All round,
<div n="lb">everywhere, entirely (comp. {#अभितस्#}); e. g. {%Ṛgv:%} {#अभि#}
<div n="lb">{#त्वा देव सवितरीशानं वार्याणाम् । सदावन्भागमीमहे#} (where
<div n="lb">{#त्वा#} is not to be connected with {#अभि,#} but with {#ईमहे;#} {%Sā-%}
<div n="lb">{%yaṇa:%} {#त्वां प्रति भागं भजनीयं धनमभि सर्वत ईमहे#}); or {%Sā-%}
<div n="lb">{%mav.%} or {%Vājas.:%} {#अभि त्यं देवं सवितारमोण्योः कविक्रतुमर्चामि#}
<div n="lb">{#सत्यसवं रत्नधामभि प्रियं मतिम्;#} or {%Śāntip.%} of the {%Mahābh.:%}
<div n="lb">{#धनाद्धर्मः प्रभवति शैलादभि नदी यथा#}.
<div n="P">4. ({%in composition with nouns%}) <sup>a.</sup> Tatpur. <sup>1</sup> if it is {#उपपद,#}
<div n="lb">it retains the meaning which it has as prefix to the radical;
<div n="lb">e. g. in {#अभिक्रम, अभिघात, अभिषेक#} &c.; <sup>2</sup> if it is not {#उ-#}
<div n="lb">{#पपद,#} it implies superiority or excess: excellently, very,
<div n="lb">very much; e. g. {#अभिधर्म, अभिनीत, अभ्यधिक#}.
<div n="P"><sup>b.</sup> Bahuvr. <sup>1</sup> Towards, in the direction of; e. g. {#अभि-#}
<div n="lb">{#मनस्, अभिमुख, अभिद्यु,#} <sup>2</sup> Excellently, very, very much;
<div n="lb">e. g. {#अभिरूप, अभ्यङ्क, अभिक्रतु#}.
<div n="P"><sup>c.</sup> Avyayībh. Towards, in the direction of; e. g. {#अभ्य-#}
<div n="lb">{#ग्नि, अभिदक्षिणम्, अभिवातम्#}. E. doubtful; the native E.
<div n="lb">{#अ#} neg. and {#भि#} ({#भा,#} uṇ. aff. {#कि#}) is not very plausible.
