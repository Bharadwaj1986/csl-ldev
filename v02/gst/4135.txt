{#अन्नरस#}¦ Tatpur. m. ({#-सः#}) <sup>1</sup> The essential properties of food,
esp. the good or salutary properties; e. g. {#अपथ्यैः सह संभुक्ते#}
{#व्याधिरन्नरसे यथा#} ‘just as disease may lurk in the good
essence of food when it is eaten with unwholesome sub-
stances’; or {#यमस्त्वन्नरसं प्रादात्#} ‘Yama gave (Nala) the
distinguished properties of food’ (comm. {#‘अन्नरसं यादृशे#}
{#तादृशेऽप्यन्ने विशिष्टरसवत्ताम्’#}). <sup>2</sup> (In the Taittirīya Upan.)
<sup>a.</sup> Semen virile, as the essence of {#अन्न#} 2. 2.; <sup>b.</sup> the same as
{#अन्न#} 2. 2. q. v. See the following. E. {#अन्न#} and {#रस#}.
