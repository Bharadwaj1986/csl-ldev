<div n="P">2. f. ({#-धा#}) The seventeenth Nakshatra (q. v.) or lunar
mansion, described as a row of oblations; it consists of
four, or according to a different reading, of three stars; its
chief and middlemost star is placed by the authorities in
3° or 2° or 1° 45'S. and in 224° or 224° 5'E., and must in-
tend the star near the head of the Scorpion (<lang n="greek">δ</lang> Scorpionis)
and the asterism probably comprises <lang n="greek">βδπ</lang> and <lang n="greek">ρ</lang> Scorpionis.
Also written {#अनूराधा#}. See {#राधानुराधा#} and {#विशाखानुराधा#}.
E. {#अनु#} and {#राधा,#} the name of the sixteenth Nakshatra.
<div n="P">II. 1. m. f. n. ({#-धः-धा-धम्#}) Born under the constellation
Anurādhā.
<div n="P">2. m. ({#-धः#}) A proper name, the son of Pāṇḍu-Śākya
and founder of the village or town Anurādha (see {#अनुराध-#}
{#ग्राम#} and {#अनुराधपुर#}), the ancient capital of Ceylon. E.
{#अनुराधा#} with {#लुक्#} of the taddh. aff. {#अण्#}.
