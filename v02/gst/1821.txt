{#अधस्तात्#}¦ ind. The same as {#अधस्#} in the three first meanings.
It is used, like this word, in its two first meanings in the
sense of a nominative, ablative and locative and may in its
first meaning govern a noun in the genitive, more seldom in
the ablative. See {#अधस्#}. E. {#अध्#} (considered as a substitute
of {#अधर,#} but more probably the thematic form common to
{#अधम, अधर, अधस्#} and {#अधस्तात्#}), taddh. aff. {#अस्ताति#}.
