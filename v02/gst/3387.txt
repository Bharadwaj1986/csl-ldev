{#अनुशस्त्र#}¦ Tatpur. n. ({#-स्त्रम्#}) (In Medicine.) A subsidiary in-
strument or surgical means in general, used either in want
of the proper instrument or when the patient is afraid of
the latter; as such are named: bambu ({#त्वक्सार#}), crystal,
glass ({#काच#}), ruby ({#कुरुविन्द#}), leeches, fire, alkali ({#क्षार#}),
the finger nail, and several plants ({#गोजी, शेफालिका, शा-#}
{#कपत्र, करीर, बालाङ्गुलि#}). E. {#अनु#} and {#शस्त्र#}.
