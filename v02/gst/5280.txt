{#अपृथग्ग्रहण#}¦ Tatpur. n. ({#-णम्#}) Not taking or perceiving se-
parately; e. g. in the {%Nyāya Sūtra:%} {#तदाश्रयत्वादपृथग्ग्रहणम्#}

threads)’, i. e. a web cannot be perceived separately from
its threads, since it consists thereof. E. {#अ#} neg. and {#पृथ-#}
{#ग्ग्रहण#}.
