{#अपरान्त#}¦ Karmadh. m. ({#-न्तः#}) The western extremity, the
<div n="lb">western border, the western sea-shore; e. g. {#सोऽपरान्तेषु#}
<div n="lb">{#तीर्थानि पुण्यान्यायतनानि च । तानि सर्वानि गत्वा स प्रभास-#}
<div n="lb">{#मुपजग्मिवान्#} ({%Arjunam.%} {#अपरान्तेषु = समुद्रसमीपेषु पश्चिमेषु;#}
<div n="lb">{%Nīlak.%} = {#पश्चिमसमुद्रतीरेषु#}); or {#अपरान्तादहं कृष्ण संप्रती-#}
<div n="lb">{#हागतः#} ({%Rāmānanda:%} {#अपरान्तात् = पश्चिमसमुद्रतीरात्#}).--
<div n="lb">The plur. {#अपरान्ताः#} is the name of the country or the in-
<div n="lb">habitants of the western borders.--{%Wilson%} ({%Viṣṇup.%} p.
<div n="lb">189, n. 60) renders {#अपरान्ताः#} ‘the people not beyond the
<div n="lb">borders’, as opposed to {#परान्ताः#} ‘the people beyond the
<div n="lb">borders’ (i. e. of {#परा#} and {#अन्त#}); but as {#पर#} when opposed to
<div n="lb">{#अपर#} assumes meanings corresponding with those of {#पूर्व#} (see
<div n="lb">s. v. {#अपर#} II.), {#परान्ताः,#} as contradistinguished from {#अप-#}
<div n="lb">{#रान्ताः,#} may mean perhaps ‘the people of the eastern borders’
<div n="lb">when {#अपरान्ताः#} would retain the sense given before. See
<div n="lb">the following.--<sup>2</sup> Last term, completion, e. g. in the {%Yoga-%}
<div n="lb">{%Sūtra:%} {#क्षणप्रतियोगी परिणामोऽपरान्तनिर्ग्राह्यः क्रमः#} ‘ma-
<div n="lb">turity (or change) is the reverse of what is but a moment;
<div n="lb">by the completion (of maturity) the order (of the moments)
<div n="lb">becomes comprehensible’. <sup>3</sup> Death. See the Yoga term {#अ-#}
<div n="lb">{#परान्तज्ञान#} and comp. {#पूर्वान्त#} and {#परान्त#}. E. {#अपर#} and {#अन्त#}.
