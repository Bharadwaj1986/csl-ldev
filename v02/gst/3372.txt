{#अनुव्याख्यान#}¦ Tatpur. n. ({#-नम्#}) According to the Bṛhadāraṇ-
<div n="lb">yaka-Upan., one of the eight categories or characteristics of
<div n="lb">the text of a Brāhmaṇa; it is defined either as a synonyme
<div n="lb">of {#अर्थवाद#} q. v. or as a passage of a Brāhmaṇa in which
<div n="lb">short and obscure dicta (or {#सूत्र#}) which have occurred in
<div n="lb">another portion of the Brāhmaṇa, are discussed and ex-
<div n="lb">plained. For the other categories comp. {#इतिहास, पुराण,#}
<div n="lb">{#विद्या, उपनिषद्, श्लोक, सूत्र#} and {#व्याख्यान#}. E. {#अनु#} and
<div n="lb">{#व्याख्यान#}.
