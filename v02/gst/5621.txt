{#अप्राप्तव्यवहार#}¦ Tatpur. m. ({#-रः#}) (In law.) A minor, one
<div n="lb">incapable from nonage of conducting his own affairs; (ac-
<div n="lb">cording to {%Kṛṣṇa Tarkal.,%} a boy not more than fifteen
<div n="lb">years old: {#अप्राप्तव्यवहारा वालकाः पञ्चदशवर्षादनधिकव-#}
<div n="lb">{#यस्काः;#} according to the {%Mitākṣara,%} a boy under sixteen
<div n="lb">years, the same as {#पौगण्ड#}); his inherited property must
<div n="lb">be confided to the care of relatives or friends; ({%Kātyāyana:%}
<div n="lb">{#अप्राप्तव्यवहाराणां धनं व्ययवर्जितम् । न्यसेयुर्बन्धुमित्रेषु#}); he
<div n="lb">is not amenable for the debts of his deceased father, ({%Nā-%}
<div n="lb">{%rada:%} {#अप्राप्तव्यवहारश्चेत्स्वतन्त्रोऽपि हि नर्णभाक्;#}) nor liable
[Page220-b+ 56]
<div n="lb">to arrest or summons, ({#अप्राप्तव्यवहारश्च दूतो दानोन्मुखो#}
<div n="lb">{#व्रती । विषमस्थश्च नासेध्यो न चैतानाह्वयेन्नृपः#}). E. {#अ#} neg.
<div n="lb">and {#प्राप्तव्यवहार#}.
