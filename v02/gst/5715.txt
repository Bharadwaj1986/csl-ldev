{#अबल#}¦ I. Tatpur. n. ({#-लम्#}) Want of strength, weakness;
e. g. in the {%Daśakumārach.:%} {#इतः किल जनाद्भगवतस्त्रिवर्ग-#}
{#बलाबलज्ञानम्;#} also figur., comp. {#अर्घबलाबल#}. E. {#अ#} neg.
and {#बल#}.
<div n="P">II. Bahuvr. 1. m. f. n. ({#-लः-ला-लम्#}) <sup>1</sup> Weak, feeble,
infirm; e. g. in the {%Bhāgavata Pur.:%} {#अहो वयं धन्यतमा य-#}
{#दत्र त्यक्ताः पितृभ्यां न विचिन्तयामः । अभक्ष्यमाणा अबला#}
{#वृकादिभिः स रक्षिता रक्षति यो हि गर्भे;#} or in the {%Hitop.:%}
{#विपन्नायां नीतौ सकलमबलं सीदति जगत्#}. <sup>2</sup> Without a
protector, unprotected; see the instance 3. 1.
<div n="P">2. m. ({#-लः#}) <sup>1</sup> The name of a plant (Tapia cratœva).
<sup>2</sup> Time (?). <sup>3</sup> A hole (?). [The two last meanings which
are very suspicious, occur in the fourth chapter of an E.
I. H. Ms. (2544) of the {%Anekārthamañjarī;%} this chapter it-
self, however, is omitted in three other E. I. H. Mss. of
the same work and, to judge from its character, seems
added by a latter writer; these two meanings would suggest
the assumption of an error in the copyist, for they remind
us of {#वेला#} and {#विल,#} but the word occurs in the beginning
of the Śloka and is written with the initial {#अ#}.]
<div n="P">3. f. ({#-ला#}) <sup>1</sup> A woman; e. g. in the {%Bhaṭṭik.:%} {#नश्यन्ति#}
{#ददर्श वृन्दानि कपीन्द्रः । हारीण्यबलानां हारीण्यबलानाम्,#}
where the first {#अबलानाम्#} means ‘of the women’, and the
second ‘unprotected’ or ‘weak’ (comm. {#अबलानां स्त्रीणां ।#}
{#अबलानामविद्यमानरक्षकाणाम्#} or {#अतिकृशानाम्#}). <sup>2</sup> (In
Astronomy.) The sign of the zodiac, {%Virgo.%} <sup>3</sup> One of the
ten Buddhist earths (?); comp. {#अचला#}. [An E. I. H. Ms.
(1512) of {%Ajayapāla'%}s {%Nānārthasaṅgraha%} has the following
[Page227-a+ 56]
extraordinary explanation of {#अबला, ‘अबलाख्या श्याला-#}
{#कस्त्री पूगरक्तमुखाम्भसोः’#}.] E. {#अ#} priv. and {#बल#}.
