{#अप्रमाणशुभ#}¦ Bahuvr. m. pl. ({#-भाः#}) (In Buddhistic doctrine.)
The name of a class of divinities which belong to the
second degree of the third dhyāna or contemplation (literally,
according to the Buddh. interpretation, ‘of immeasurable
virtue’). E. {#अप्रमाण#} and {#शुभ#}.
