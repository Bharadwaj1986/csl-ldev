{#अभिषव#}¦ Tatpur. 1. m. ({#-वः#}) <sup>1</sup> The religious act of pressing
out the juice of the Soma plant or Asclepias acida (see
also {#सोमाभिषव#}), or in default of this plant, of its substi-
tute, the Pūtīkā or Cæsalpinia bonducella (see also {#पूती-#}
{#काभिषव#}); it constitutes an essential ceremony of the
Soma sacrifices (see {#ज्योतिष्टोम#}), and is performed the day
after the sacrificial acts called {#उपसद्#} (q. v.), by means
of stones ({#ग्रावन्#}) which press on two boards (called
{#अधिषवण#}); the act takes place in the southern part of a
building erected in the western part of the Uttaravedi near
a cart which is placed there and bears, like the building,
the name {#हविर्धान#}. (Comp. e. g. {%Mādhava vv. ll.:%} {#यदि#}
{#सोमं न विन्देत पूतीकानभिषुणुयादिति श्रूयते;--दीक्षादि-#}
{#वसादूर्ध्वं सोमाभिषवदिवसात्पूर्वं कर्तव्या होमा उपसदः;#}--

{#वमापादिताः;#} {%Mādhava:%} {#सोमाभिषवाधारयोरधिषवणफल-#}

{#षवः;--हविर्धाने ग्रावभिरभिषुत्याहवनीये हुत्वा सदसि#}
{#भक्षयन्ति;--ज्योतिष्टोमे श्रूयते । उत यत्सुन्वन्ति सामिधेनी-#}
