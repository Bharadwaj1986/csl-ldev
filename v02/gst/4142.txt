{#अन्नविकारत्व#}¦ n. ({#-त्वम्#}) The being a transformation of food;
of the metaphysical or mystical food (see {#अन्न#} 2. 2.), e. g. {#अन्नं#}
{#वै विराडिति श्रुतेरन्नविकारत्वम्#} ‘from the vaidik passage
“Virāj is food” follows his being a transformation of food’;
comp. {#अन्नब्रह्मन्#} and the following. E. {#अन्नविकार,#} taddh.
aff. {#त्व#}. This word ought perhaps to be read {#अन्नविकारित्व#}.
