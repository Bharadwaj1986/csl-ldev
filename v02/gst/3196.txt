{#अनुमरण#}¦ Tatpur. n. ({#-णम्#}) Dying with or following in death;
<div n="lb">the term usually denotes the cremation of a Hindu widow,
<div n="lb">whose husband's corpse is not on the spot, and with whose
<div n="lb">shoes she, therefore, ascends the pile: this is prohibited to
<div n="lb">Brāhmaṇa women; or the cremation of a widow with the
<div n="lb">body of the husband (see {#सहमरण#}). E. {#अनु#} and {#मरण#}.
