{#अन्वाचय#}¦ Tatpur. m. ({#-यः#}) Adding an object of secondary
import, or such an object itself; used only in reference to
{#प्रधान, मुख्य#} or {#प्राधान्य,#} the subject of primary import:
{#‘यत्रैकस्य प्राधान्येनापरस्य गौण्येनाख्यानं सोऽन्वाचयः’;#} thus
a comm. of the Amarak. in explaining the various appli-
cations of the particle {#च,#} one of which is {#‘अन्वाचये’#} in-
stances (comp. the Siddhāntakaum. on Pāṇini II. 2. 29.):
{#भो भिक्षो भिक्षामट गां चानय,#} when the going out for alms
is enjoined to the beggar as his principal object and his
bringing a cow, when he can see one, as the matter of
secondary import; or of the three Sūtras of Pāṇini VII. 3.
117. 118. 119., the two former contain the {#प्रधान#} and the latter
the {#अन्वाचय#}. See the following. E. {#अनु#} and {#आचय#}.
