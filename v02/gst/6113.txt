{#अभिन्नपदश्लिष्ट#}¦ Karmadh. n. ({#-ष्टम्#}) (In Rhetoric.) A species
<div n="lb">of paronomasia, considered as an {#अलङ्कार#} (q. v.) or elegant
<div n="lb">mode of expression, viz. a connexion of words which ad-
<div n="lb">mit of a double interpretation, the sentence consisting chiefly
<div n="lb">of {%such%} words (and not of words which would admit but
<div n="lb">of one sense); an instance is the following verse of the
<div n="lb">{%Bhaṭṭik.:%} {#भुवनभरसहानलङ्घ्यधाम्नः पुरुरुचिरत्नभृतो गुरूरुदे-#}
<div n="lb">{#हान् । श्रमविधुरविलीनकूर्मनक्रान्दधतमुदूढभुवो गिरीनहींश्च#}
<div n="lb">(scil. {#जलनिधिमगमन्#}), where all the compounds are at-
<div n="lb">tributes as well of {#गिरीन्#} as of {#अहीन्,#} according to the
<div n="lb">double sense in which they may be taken. See {#श्लिष्ट#} or
<div n="lb">{#श्लेष#}. E. {#अभिन्न-पद#} and {#श्लिष्ट#}.
