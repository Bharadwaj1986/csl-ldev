{#अभिन्नपदश्लिष्ट#}¦ Karmadh. n. ({#-ष्टम्#}) (In Rhetoric.) A species
of paronomasia, considered as an {#अलङ्कार#} (q. v.) or elegant
mode of expression, viz. a connexion of words which ad-
mit of a double interpretation, the sentence consisting chiefly
of {%such%} words (and not of words which would admit but
of one sense); an instance is the following verse of the
{%Bhaṭṭik.:%} {#भुवनभरसहानलङ्घ्यधाम्नः पुरुरुचिरत्नभृतो गुरूरुदे-#}
{#हान् । श्रमविधुरविलीनकूर्मनक्रान्दधतमुदूढभुवो गिरीनहींश्च#}
(scil. {#जलनिधिमगमन्#}), where all the compounds are at-
tributes as well of {#गिरीन्#} as of {#अहीन्,#} according to the
double sense in which they may be taken. See {#श्लिष्ट#} or
{#श्लेष#}. E. {#अभिन्न-पद#} and {#श्लिष्ट#}.
