{#अपमृत्यु#}¦ Tatpur. m. ({#-त्युः#}) <sup>1</sup> Sudden death, death caused by
some casualty, as by animals, lightning &c., not by sick-
ness or decay. <sup>2</sup> A minor death: a term applied to a des-
perate sickness, an alarming danger, to any awful accident
or perilous circumstance, from which, contrary to expectation,
the subject recovers or escapes (Molesw.). E. {#अप#} and {#मृत्यु#}.
