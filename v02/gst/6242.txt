<div n="lb">see {%N. B.;%} {#-खम्#}) <sup>1</sup> With the face directed towards, facing,

<div n="lb">{%Rāmāy.:%} {#न नास्तिकेनाभिमुखो बुधः स्यात्;#} or {%Lalitav.:%} {#ये#}
