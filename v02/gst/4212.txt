{#अन्यतस्#}¦ ind. Used in the same manner as 1. the ablative and
<div n="lb">2. the locative of {#अन्य,#} absolutely or with the ellipsis of a
<div n="lb">word implying ‘place’, ‘manner’ &c., viz.
<div n="P">1. = the ablative of {#अन्य,#} ‘from another’; e. g. {#भावितं#}
<div n="lb">{#पक्षतोऽभीष्टौ त्यक्त्वा वर्णौ सरूपकौ । अन्यतो भाविताङ्केन#}
<div n="lb">{#ततः पक्षौ विभज्य च#} &c., i. e. ‘removing the factum from one
<div n="lb">side and the simple colours and absolute numbers from the
<div n="lb">other, as optionally selected, and dividing both sides of the
<div n="lb">equation by the coefficient of the factum &c.’; also with a
<div n="lb">noun depending on it in the ablative, e. g. in the comm. of
<div n="lb">the Kāsikā on Pāṇ. IV. 1. 40. ‘{#तोपधादन्यतः प्रातिपदिका-#}
<div n="lb">{#द्वर्णवाचिनोऽनुदात्तात्स्त्रियां ङीष्प्रत्ययो भवति#}.
<div n="P">2. = the locative of {#अन्य#} and in the same sense as {#अन्यत्र,#}
<div n="lb">viz. <sup>1</sup> In another, on another; e. g. {#अन्यतो वृत्तौ#} (comm.
<div n="lb">= {#अन्यत्र जातौ#}) ‘in another way’; or in Hemachandra's
<div n="lb">explanation of {#विज्ञान, ‘मोक्षे ज्ञानं विज्ञानमन्यतः,#} i. e.
<div n="lb">{#ज्ञान#} means (knowledge of what concerns) final emancipa-
<div n="lb">tion, {#विज्ञान#} other (knowledge), where {#अन्यतः#} represents
<div n="lb">the locative {#अन्यस्मिन्,#} the explaining words being put in
<div n="lb">the locative, and does not mean ‘in another case, else-
<div n="lb">where’. <sup>2</sup> (Scil. {#देशे#}) Elsewhere; e. g. {#प्रतिपत्तिशून्यमुत्था-#}
<div n="lb">{#प्यान्यतः प्रवृत्तः;#} or {#यान्तोऽन्यतः…आत्मवर्ग्याः;#} also re-
<div n="lb">peated: ‘in one place--in another place’, e. g. {#अशृण्वन्न-#}
<div n="lb">{#न्यतः शब्दं प्रपलायन्त चान्यतः । आक्रन्दमन्यतोऽकुर्वेस्तेनाहन्यन्त#}
<div n="lb">{#चान्यतः ॥#}. Comp. {#अन्यतोवात#}. <sup>3</sup> (Scil. {#देशे#}) In one place,
<div n="lb">in one direction (ved.); comp. {#अन्यतोघातिन्, अन्यतोमुख,#}
<div n="lb">{#अन्यतोऽरण्य#}. <sup>4</sup> (Scil. {#देशे#}) On the other side, on the con-
<div n="lb">trary. <sup>5</sup> (Scil. {#प्रकारे#}) In another way or manner, otherwise;
<div n="lb">e. g. {#वाष्पभरस्तया चलितया निःश्वस्य मुक्तोऽन्यतः#}. E. {#अन्य,#}
<div n="lb">vibhakti-taddh. aff. {#तसिल्#}.
