{#अभिशोचन#}¦ Tatpur. n. ({#-नम्#}) Pain, grief or excessive pain,
excessive grief; e. g. {%Atharv.:%} {#नैनं प्राप्नोति शपथो न कृत्या#}
{#नाभिशोचनम् । नैनं विष्कन्धमश्नुते यस्त्वा बिभर्त्याञ्जन#}. E.
{#शुच्#} with {#अभि,#} kṛt aff. {#ल्युट्#} or {#अभि#} and {#शोचन#}. (The
word is udātta on the third syllable; a derivation with kṛt
aff. {#युच्#} according to {%Pāṇ.%} III. 2. 150. and a meaning ‘one
who causes pain’ is therefore not admissible.)
