<div n="lb">of an arithmetical progression; comp. {#मध्यधन#} and {#सर्वधन;#}
<div n="lb">e. g. {#व्येकपदघ्नचयो मुखयुक्स्यादन्त्यधनं मुखयुग्दलितं तत् ।#}

<div n="lb">first quantity is the amount of the last; that added to the

<div n="lb">nominated the computed sum’. E. {#अन्त्य#} and {#धन#}.
