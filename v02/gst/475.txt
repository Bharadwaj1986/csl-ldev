{#अग्नीषोमप्रणयन#}¦ Tatpur. n. ({#-नम्#}) Carrying forth the fire and
<div n="lb">the Soma; a ceremony in the Jyotiṣṭoma sacrifice which
<div n="lb">takes place after the Vaisarjana homa (q. v.) and consists
<div n="lb">in taking the fire from the Prāchīnavaṇśa (q. v.) Manḍapa
<div n="lb">and carrying it to the Āgnīdhra (q. v.) Manḍapa, and in
<div n="lb">the same time taking the Soma from the Prāchīnavaṇśa
<div n="lb">and carrying it to the Havirdhāna Manḍapa, a building
<div n="lb">erected in the Saumikī Vedi (q. v.). See also {#अग्निप्रणयन#}.
<div n="lb">E. {#अग्नीषोम#} and {#प्रणयन#}.
