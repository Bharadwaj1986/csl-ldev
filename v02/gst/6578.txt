{#अभीपत्#}¦ Tatpur. m. ({#-न्#}) (ved.) <sup>1</sup> A pond or any other place
where water accumulates. <sup>2</sup> Favour, kindness. An option
between both meanings is proposed by {%Sāyaṇa%} on {%Ṛgv.:%} {#अभी-#}
{#पतो वृष्टिभिस्तर्पयन्तम्#} ({%Atharv.:%} {#अभीपतो वृष्ट्या तर्पयन्तम्#}),
viz. {#अभीपत आनुकूल्येन#} i. e. abl. sing.; or {#अभीपतोऽभिग-#}
{#मनवतः सलिलाधारान् । तटाकादीन्#} i. e. acc. plur.; the se-
cond meaning ({%favour%}) reminds of {#अन्वीप,#} but the first
seems more congenial with the context. E. probably {#अप्#}
(an obsolete rad.; comp. {#आप्#}) with {#अभि,#} kṛt aff. {#शतृ#}.
