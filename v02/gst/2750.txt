{#अनिलपर्याय#}¦ Tatpur. m. ({#-यः#}) (In Medicine.) An inflammation
<div n="lb">affecting the whole eye, described as produced from the
<div n="lb">vitiated Rasa wind (see {#अनिल#} 1. f.) ‘which pervades the eye-
<div n="lb">lids, eye and eyebrows and makes them diseased one after the
<div n="lb">other’. The less correct reading of this word is {#अनिलपयय#}.
<div n="lb">See also {#मारुतपर्याय#} and {#वातपर्याय#}. E. {#अनिल#} and {#पर्याय#}.
