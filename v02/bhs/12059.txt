<L>12059<pc>432,1<k1>माहिष्मती<k2>माहिष्मती
{@Māhiṣmatī@}¦ (= Pali Māhissatī), n. of a city, capital
of the Avantis, as in DN ii.235.20; originally so in Mv
iii.208.18, where mss. are corrupt; Senart correctly em.
to Māhiṣmatī, but leaves a blank for the people-name;
mss. ca vartināṃ; read c' Avantīnāṃ with DN. In Sn
1011 Māhissatī is mentioned along with Ujjenī, which
confirms its association with Avanti.
