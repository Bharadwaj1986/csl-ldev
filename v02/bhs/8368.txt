{@Nirgrantha Jñātiputra@}¦ (cf. prec.), also written
{@Nigrantha@}, and in Av Jñataputra (= Pali Nigaṇṭha Nāta-
putta or Nātha°), one of the six famous heretical teachers
of Buddha's day (see s.v. {@Pūraṇa Kāśyapa@}): named with
the others, Mvy 3550; Mv i.253.17 (v.l. Nigr°); 257.1;
iii.383.17 (v.l. Nigr°); Divy 143.12 (mss. Nigr°); Av i.231.5
(Jñātap°; best ms. Nigr°). His followers are supposed to
be the Jains.
