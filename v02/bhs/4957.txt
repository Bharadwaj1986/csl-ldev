{@? Kutarārkaka@}¦, n. of a yakṣa in Kurukṣetra: Māy
57; dual dvandva, Tarārkakutarārkakau; division be-
tween the two names uncertain, but Tarārkaku-tarārkakau
seems implausible. Lévi, p. 97, refers to Tarantukārantu-

name of two {%localities%} (acc. to BR, tīrthas) in Kurukṣetra.
