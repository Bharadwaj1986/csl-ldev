One might be tempted to assume a misreading for Skt.
nāpitī. But note Pali nahāminī, Pv iii.1.13, repeated in
comm. 176.1 with gloss kappaka-jātikā. Our word looks
like confirmation of Kern's conjecture, cited PTSD s.v.
nahāmin, that the Pali form stands for °pin(ī).
