<L>12220<pc>438,1<k1>मृगार<k2>मृगार
{@Mṛgāra@}¦ (= Pali Migāra), n. of a rich man, or acc.
to MSV the chief minister, of Śrāvastī: Divy 44.13; MSV
ii.52.12. Acc. to MSV, as in Pali sources, father-in-law of
Visākhā (our {@Viśākhā@} 2); she was instrumental in instruct-
ing him, and he called her his mother, whence she is called
{@Mṛgāra-mātar@} (Migāra°), cf. MSV ii.69.10 ff.; see next
(but acc. to some Pali sources she actually had a son,
also named Migāra: DPPN).
