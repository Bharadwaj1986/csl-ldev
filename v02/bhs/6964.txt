ākāśagamanaṃ cāpi tiryaṃ cāpi nabhastale Mmk 148.20

demand tiriya, contrary to text, in tiryebhyo dadau

m.c., Samādh 19.17; Suv 48.1; RP 27.10 (text here tīr°);
32.8. The stem tirya has been recorded only in verses.
