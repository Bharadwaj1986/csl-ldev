in Mv (i.79.11; ii.93.5, 16; 220.16), and occurs also LV
35.4; Av ii.107.3 (mss., Speyer em. to s); RP 18.7; 34.8;
Śikṣ 52.6; so also most mss. Sukh 74.3 (ed. s); otherwise
with s, SP 27.7; 203.11 hīnādhimuktāś ca kusīdarūpā;
Av i.15.15 ff.; 216.1; Śikṣ 50.17; 275.4; Mmk 73.12;
({@2@}) noun, m. or nt., {%sloth%} (not so recorded in Pali; =
{@kausīdya@}), clearly in RP 35.13 (vs) lābhābhibhūtasya
kusīda (n. sg.) vardhate, kusīdabhūtasya praṇaṣṭa śraddhā;
perhaps also in Gv 220.7, above.
