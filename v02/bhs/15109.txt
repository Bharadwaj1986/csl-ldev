<L>15109<pc>530,2<k1>शुद्धान्तिक<k2>शुद्धान्तिक
{@śuddhāntika@}¦, adj. with {@parivāsa@} (= Pali suddhanta-
parivāsa), a kind of probation imposed for concealed
saṃghāvaśeṣa offenses when the period of concealment
was unknown: MSV iii.72.11; 73.7. Acc. to the Pali Vin.
comm., it lasted for a time equal to that elapsed since
the offender's ordination (see SBE 17.417, note 1).
