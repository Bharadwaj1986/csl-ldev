Mironov °pauruṣam, and so Prāt 537.18, implying the

na sādh° (Mvy prints nasādh° as one word) Mironov

nāsādh°, erroneously; cf. Pali MN i.74.12 sādhika-porisā,
{%of more than a man's height%}; this is confirmed by Tib.
mi gaṅ tsam las mthor, {%to the height of an average man%}.
For na sādhika Prāt (l.c.) has a lacuna, which Finot
fills by the erroneous nāsādhikaṃ from Minayeff's Mvy.
