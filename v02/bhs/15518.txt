{%caste%}: (vayaṃ tvāṃ) jñātimadhyād utkṣipāmaḥ, saṃkāraṃ
pātayāmo Divy 273.2, 11; but mss. are not unanimous,
and in same phrase 272.24 it is alleged that they read
salokānāṃ (! kept in text) for saṃkāraṃ; quite uncertain.
