<L>11878<pc>426,2<k1>महासंविधान<k2>महासंविधान
{@mahāsaṃvidhāna@}¦, nt., {%the Great Arrangement%} or
{%Preparation%}, made by a Bodhisattva in the Tuṣita heaven
when he is about to descend to earth for his last existence:
Mv i.198.12; ii.2.7. It consists in the departure of many
devas from heaven to be reborn on earth so as to support
the Bodhisattva in his mission.
