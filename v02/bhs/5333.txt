{@kleśa@}¦ (also semi-MIndic {@kileśa@}), m. (= Pali kilesa),
{%impurity, depravity%}; on relation to {@anuśaya@}, q.v., see esp.

kleśa: rāga, pratigha, māna, avidyā, kudṛṣṭi, vicikitsā;

em. Senart), and others, § 3.109. A discussion in Burnouf,
