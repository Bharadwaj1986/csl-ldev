l'eau’; Pali Vin. iii.213.8 udakena vā vuḷhaṃ. This seems
to prove that the Prāt intended ūḍhacīvaro, altho the
repetition in 492.6 has vā rūḍhacīvareṇa. Neither rūḍha-

either emend to vā ūḍha°, or understand vā-r-ūḍha° with
hiatus-bridging r.]
