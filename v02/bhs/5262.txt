{@Koṣṭhila@}¦, also {@Mahā-k°@} and {@(Mahā-)Kauṣṭhila@},
qq.v. (= Pali Koṭṭhita or °ika or Mahā-k°, who was
declared ‘first of the paṭisaṃbhidappatta’ among Buddha's
disciples, cf. below), a disciple of Buddha: in Av ii.195.4
declared first of the pratisaṃvitprāpta; here and in 195.7,
196.9 (also called Mahā-k° 195.5) he is evidently identified
with {@Dīrghanakha@}, q.v.; this identification not recorded
in Pali but confirmed MSV iv.15.14 ff.; it should be noted
that in Pali (Mahā-)Koṭṭhita is intimately associated
with Sāriputta, who in Av, MSV is a nephew of Dīr-
[Page195-b+ 71]
ghanakha, and who was present on the occasion of the
Dīghanakha-sutta even in Pali (MN i.497 ff.).
